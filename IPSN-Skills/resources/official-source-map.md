# Official Source Map - IPSN (and its SenSys successor)

Access date: 2026-07-09

This map records the official and cross-check sources used for IPSN-specific facts in this pack.
IPSN is the ACM/IEEE International Conference on Information Processing in Sensor Networks,
co-sponsored by **ACM SIGBED and IEEE** and run inside **CPS-IoT Week**. Its calendar, page budget,
and track structure were decided per edition, and — the governing fact — **IPSN merged into the
reconstituted SenSys (Embedded AI and Sensing Systems) from 2025-2026**. Reopen the current
successor (SenSys) call and the CPS-IoT Week page before giving submission-ready advice.

## Verified IPSN-lineage facts used in skills

- **IPSN 2024 was the 23rd ACM/IEEE International Conference on Information Processing in Sensor
  Networks**, held in **Hong Kong** within **CPS-IoT Week 2024** (May 13-16, 2024). It used
  **double-blind** review, the **ACM Primary Article Template** (two-column, 9 pt), a **≤12-page**
  budget inclusive of figures, tables, and references, and **HotCRP** (`ipsn2024.hotcrp.com`).
  This was the **last standalone IPSN edition** (sources 1-3, 5).
- **IPSN's signature two-track structure:** the **Information Processing (IP) track** (algorithms,
  estimation, signal processing, inference, learning, localization) and the **Sensor Platforms,
  Tools and Design Methods (SPOTS) track** (hardware, embedded software, tools, measurement,
  deployments). Analysis/processing of collected data → IP; hardware/software platforms and tools →
  SPOTS (source 6, consistent across IPSN 2006-2015 and later calls).
- **Awards:** IPSN gave a **Best Paper Award** and a **Best Research Artifact Award** (source 4),
  reflecting a real artifact and deployment culture.
- **Sponsorship and publication:** co-sponsored by **ACM SIGBED and IEEE**; proceedings appeared in
  **both the ACM Digital Library and IEEE Xplore** (sources 5, 10).
- **Co-location:** IPSN ran inside **CPS-IoT Week**, alongside **RTAS, HSCC, ICCPS** (and
  historically IoTDI) (sources 2, 9).

## The merger (the governing fact)

- **SenSys, IPSN, and IoTDI merged.** **SenSys 2025** (CPS-IoT Week 2025, Irvine, May 6-9, 2025)
  was the first combined event; IPSN and IoTDI stopped running as standalone conferences from 2025
  (source 7).
- From **2026**, the merged venue is **SenSys — the ACM/IEEE International Conference on Embedded
  Artificial Intelligence and Sensing Systems**. **SenSys 2026** ran within **CPS-IoT Week 2026**
  (Saint Malo, France, May 11-14, 2026); its paper submission deadline was **13 November 2025 AoE**
  (registration **6 November 2025**), with **double-blind** review and desk-rejection for anonymity
  violations; full papers **12 pages** / short papers **6 pages** (Experiences category); regular
  technical papers publish in the **ACM** proceedings and demos/posters in the **IEEE** proceedings
  (sources 8, 9).
- **As of the 2026-07-09 access date**, SenSys 2026 has already occurred, so the live next target
  for IPSN-lineage work is the **following SenSys edition** — its exact dates and deadline are
  **待核实**.

## No editor-in-chief; conference cost model

Like every conference in this collection, IPSN had **no standing editor-in-chief**; the rotating
analogue was the per-edition General Chair(s) and per-track (IP / SPOTS) Program Chairs, appointed
under CPS-IoT Week, ACM SIGBED, and IEEE. The cost model is conference registration; at least one
author presents an accepted paper.
