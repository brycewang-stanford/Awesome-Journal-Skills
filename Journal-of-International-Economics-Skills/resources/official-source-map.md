# JIE Official Source Map

Primary and authoritative sources behind the Journal of International Economics (JIE)
specific facts injected into this skill pack.
Access date for all entries: **2026-06-01**. Re-verify volatile items (editors, fees,
policy wording, portal URLs) before relying on them — journal pages change, and several
Elsevier/ScienceDirect pages returned HTTP 403 to automated fetches.

## Official journal pages (Elsevier / ScienceDirect)

- **https://www.sciencedirect.com/journal/journal-of-international-economics/publish/guide-for-authors** — Guide for Authors. Verifies: non-refundable submission fee **USD 190 / EUR 169.20 / JPY 20,660**; **reduced fee USD 95 when all authors are PhD students**; VAT added for authors in relevant European countries; abstract **concise and factual, not exceeding 150 words**, references avoided in the abstract (cite author(s)/year(s) only if essential); **1-7 keywords** required; Elsevier applies the journal's reference style at the proof stage (authors may submit in any consistent style as long as required bibliographic elements — author names, journal/book title, article/chapter title, year, volume, article number or pagination — are present; DOIs encouraged); scope spans trade patterns and commercial policy, exchange rates, open-economy macro, international finance, sovereign debt, factor mobility, spatial economics, and international monetary and fiscal theory and policy; replication materials (computer programs and data sets) required at the JIE secure repository. (Page returned 403 on direct automated fetch; facts cross-confirmed via the sources below.)
- **https://www.sciencedirect.com/journal/journal-of-international-economics/about/editorial-board** — Editorial Board. Intended to verify the current (Co-)Editor roster. (Returned 403 on direct fetch — see 待核实 note on editor titling below.)
- **https://www.journals.elsevier.com/journal-of-international-economics/announcements/expedite-editorial-decisions** — Prior Review Process (PRP) announcement. Verifies: authors may attach the editorial decision letter and referee reports from a rejection at **AER, Econometrica, JPE, QJE, or Review of Economic Studies** to expedite editorial decisions; select **'PRP' from the Article Type menu** at submission; **same submission fee as a regular submission (no extra fee)**.
- **https://www.elsevier.com/researcher/author/policies-and-guidelines/submission-fees** — Elsevier submission-fees policy page. Verifies: submission-fee revenue is used to fund **JIE-sponsored summer schools** and the biennial **Jagdish Bhagwati Award** for the best paper published in the journal.
- **https://data.mendeley.com/journal/00221996** — JIE on Mendeley Data. Verifies: replication files for published JIE papers are hosted/available via **Mendeley Data** for the journal.

## Editors, scope, and submission process — secondary, cross-checked

- **https://ideas.repec.org/s/eee/inecon.html** — RePEc/IDEAS series page for the journal. Verifies: **publisher is Elsevier**; Co-Editors-in-Chief listed as **Martin Uribe (Columbia University)** and **Costas Arkolakis (Yale University)**; additional editors include **Jonathan Eaton, Charles Engel, Andres Rodriguez-Clare, and Robert W. Staiger**.
- **https://arkolakis.com/activities/journal-of-international-economics/** — Co-Editor Costas Arkolakis's own activities page. Verifies: submission types are **regular, short, or Prior Review Process (PRP)**; historical acceptance rate of regular submissions **~10-15%**, **desk rejects ~25%**; authors are advised to **suggest a (Co-)Editor whose profile fits the paper** at submission.
