# Exemplar Library — *American Educational Research Journal* (AERJ)

> **Verified via web search, access date 2026-06-08.** Confirmed entries below were checked on SAGE
> Journals under *American Educational Research Journal* (AERA's flagship; DOI stem
> `10.3102/00028312`). Following this repo's discipline, **only web-verified papers carry a specific
> attribution**; additional topic×method cells are kept as future candidates rather than filled from
> memory. Expand them by confirming each candidate's `0002831X` DOI before adding. Positioning aid —
> read the originals; do not reproduce numbers.
>
> **Sibling-confusion guard.** AERJ is NOT: *Educational Researcher* (ER), *Review of Educational
> Research* (RER, stem `00346543`), *Educational Evaluation and Policy Analysis* (EEPA), *Journal of
> Educational Psychology* (JEP, APA), *Sociology of Education* (ASA). AERJ publishes original empirical
> and theoretical studies (quantitative *and* qualitative); RER publishes reviews, ER publishes short
> commentary/agenda pieces — a frequent misattribution.

its substantive core (equity; teaching quality and achievement; school choice, accountability, and

## How to use

- **State the educational problem and the "so what" for the field early**, then the design
  (`aerj-writing-style` / this pack's writing skill).
- **Match method to claim** — AERJ accepts both rich qualitative theory-building (Ladson-Billings) and
  quantitative multilevel inference (Hill-Rowan-Ball); the vendored `code/` supports the quantitative
  lane (multilevel/panel/quasi-experimental designs).
- **Position against the AERA family** — be explicit why the paper is AERJ rather than RER (a review),
  ER (a commentary), or EEPA (a policy analysis).
