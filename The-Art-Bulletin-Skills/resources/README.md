# The Art Bulletin Resources

Action-oriented resources for *The Art Bulletin* skill pack. The `skills/` give the advice; this
directory lets an agent **model and benchmark** an Art Bulletin article — see a house-style opening and
study verified exemplars. Pair these with the relevant `skills/artbull-*/SKILL.md`.

*The Art Bulletin* is the College Art Association's flagship **art-history (humanities)** journal. Its
method is **close visual analysis of specific works, tied to numbered figures, grounded in
historical/archival evidence** — not data, statistics, or code. Accordingly **this pack ships no code
kit**; the QJE-style `code/` directory is intentionally absent because it would be discipline-
inappropriate here.

## Suggested workflow

1. **Scope and frame** with [`../skills/artbull-topic-selection`](../skills/artbull-topic-selection/SKILL.md)
   and [`../skills/artbull-scholarly-positioning`](../skills/artbull-scholarly-positioning/SKILL.md);
   model the opening on [`worked-examples/01-introduction.md`](worked-examples/01-introduction.md).
2. **Build the argument and the looking** with
   [`../skills/artbull-argument-development`](../skills/artbull-argument-development/SKILL.md) and
   [`../skills/artbull-visual-analysis`](../skills/artbull-visual-analysis/SKILL.md), using
   [`external_tools.md`](external_tools.md) for high-resolution images and archives.
3. **Benchmark** your method × field against verified articles in
   [`exemplars/library.md`](exemplars/library.md).
4. **Confirm submission, length, figure, and citation policy** in
   [`official-source-map.md`](official-source-map.md) before preparing the manuscript.

## A note on verification

The exemplars library is **web-verified** (access date 2026-06-08) against Taylor & Francis (DOI prefix
`10.1080/00043079…`) and JSTOR; unverifiable candidates are omitted and documented rather than guessed.
Volatile journal facts in [`official-source-map.md`](official-source-map.md) carry **待核实 / TO VERIFY**
flags — re-check them on the live CAA page before relying on them.
