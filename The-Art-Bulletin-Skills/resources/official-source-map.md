## Official journal, submission & preparation pages (College Art Association)

- **https://www.collegeart.org/publications/art-bulletin**
  — The Art Bulletin home (CAA). Verifies: the journal "publishes leading scholarship in the English
  language in all aspects of art history as practiced in the academy, museums, and other
  institutions"; **founded 1913**; published **four times a year (March, June, September,
  December)**; **rigorous peer review**; owned by the **College Art Association (CAA)** and
  distributed through **Taylor & Francis / Routledge**; available as a benefit of CAA membership.
- **https://www.collegeart.org/publications/art-bulletin/submissions**
  — The Art Bulletin Submission Guidelines. Verifies: submissions are **electronic only** (e-mail or
  large-file transfer); files must be **Microsoft Word**, **not PDF** (PDFs cannot be altered to
  preserve anonymity); **double-blind peer review** — remove all author-identifying information from
  text and notes; submit separate Word files for the **manuscript**, **abstract** (**no more than
  100 words**), a **cover sheet** with a **biographical statement of no more than 50 words** plus
  contact info and word count, an **illustrations file** (single Word file **no larger than 10 MB**,
  **maximum of 20 images** with captions), and a separate **list of captions**; **articles up to
  16,000 words including endnotes**; submit to **the.art.bulletin@collegeart.org**; if accepted, the
  author must **supply high-resolution images promptly**; it is the **author's responsibility** to
  determine fair use and to obtain/pay for permissions.
- **https://www.collegeart.org/publications/art-bulletin/preparation**
  — Manuscript preparation. Verifies: **endnotes, not footnotes**, numbered consecutively;
  figures numbered consecutively (no period); detailed **caption / credit-line** format (artist,
  italicized title, date, medium, dimensions in inches then centimeters, collection and location,
  copyright/credit in parentheses); picture list marked **"S," "M," or "L"** for relative size; it is
  the **author's responsibility to pay any costs incurred for the article, including photography and
  permissions expenses**; permission required to quote published material unless governed by fair use.

## Style guide

- **https://www.collegeart.org/publications/preparation** and the downloadable
  **https://www.collegeart.org/pdf/publications/CAA-Publications-Style-Guide.pdf**
  — CAA Publications Style Guide. Verifies: CAA follows **The Chicago Manual of Style** for The Art
  Bulletin and Art Journal; **note (endnote) citation** style per **CMOS chapter 14**; permissions
  and rights guidance aligns with **CMOS chapter 13**.

## Bounded live-check rules

- Treat the CAA *Publications Style Guide* as the current Chicago-style authority; if CAA's live
  preparation page differs on edition number, follow the newer page and update this map.
- Current Co-Editors-in-Chief verified 2026-06-22 (Maya Stanfield-Mazzi, Stephen Whiteman); re-confirm

  acceptance and should be checked only if chosen.
- Check the live CAA page before routing shorter formats, interventions, or book-review work.
