## Author-side checks

- HotCRP profile, conflicts, and topic areas entered before deadline week, on the
  correct per-edition instance.
- Current acmart/acmsmall template version; 25-pages-of-text count measured on the
  built PDF, bibliography excluded.
- Full-double-blind sweep: PDF metadata, acknowledgements, self-citation voice, and
  the anonymity of every proof archive and repository link.
- Proof-artifact toolchain pins (opam switch / `lean-toolchain` / Agda and stdlib
  versions) recorded where the artifact README can quote them.
- Conditional-acceptance readiness: know before submitting who will own the fall
  revision, the artifact evaluation, and the January travel if the paper crosses.

## Do not infer

- Do not project POPL 2027's July 9 deadline onto POPL 2028, and do not guess the
  author-response window or revision deadline — both were unrendered at check time.
- Do not assume badge wording, page caps, or double-blind mechanics carry over
  between editions; the 2023 shift from lightweight to full double-blind is the
  standing proof that POPL changes structural rules.
- Where sources conflict, the newest official call, the chairs' direct
  communication, or the live HotCRP instructions control; log the conflict in the
  source map rather than silently choosing.
