# RED Official Source Map

Primary and authoritative sources behind the Review of Economic Dynamics (RED)-specific facts
injected into this skill pack. Access date for all entries below: **2026-06-01**.
Re-verify volatile items (editors, fees, policy wording, abstract limit) before relying on them — journal pages change.

Facts that could not be independently confirmed from a directly readable official page are marked **待核实**.

## Official journal / society pages

- **https://economicdynamics.org/info-for-authors/** — SED "Information for Authors" page. Verifies: submission fee is **USD 175**; the review process does not begin until the fee is received; reduced **USD 100** fee if all coauthors are full-time students at submission; **no fee for papers resubmitted a second or later time** (post-first-round revisions are exempt); submission is directed via the journal's ScienceDirect page; pointer to the data/code availability policy.
- **https://economicdynamics.org/our-editors/** — SED editors page. Verifies: **Loukas Karabarbounis** (University of Minnesota / Minneapolis Fed) is the **Coordinating Editor** (RED's editor-in-chief equivalent); lists Editors, Editorial Advisory Board, and Associate Editors; **Christian Zimmermann** appears among the editors as the data/code editor.
- **https://economicdynamics.org/availability-of-data-and-computer-code-for-published-papers/** — "Availability of Data and Computer Code for Published Papers" policy. Verifies: authors of accepted papers must supply data and code **before final acceptance**; empirical papers provide final datasets plus the code that manipulates them (and a description of how the final dataset was derived from original sources); computational/theoretical papers provide the final programs that generate the results; a **readme.txt** is required (software/OS used, program execution order, expected computation time, random seeds where applicable); materials submitted as open-standard archives (**.zip, .gz, .gzip**) to **Christian Zimmermann**; files posted on the RED website and indexed on RePEc; exemptions (e.g., proprietary/confidential data) approved by the Coordinating Editor at the time of submission.
- **https://economicdynamics.org/review-of-economic-dynamics/** — SED page describing RED. Verifies: RED is the official journal of the **Society for Economic Dynamics**; publishes meritorious original contributions to dynamic economics; SED targets fast reviews (**~two-month average post-desk turnaround**).
- **https://www.sciencedirect.com/journal/review-of-economic-dynamics** — ScienceDirect journal homepage / "Submit your article" entry point. Verifies: submission runs through Elsevier off the ScienceDirect page; manuscripts handled in Elsevier Editorial Manager.
- **https://www.sciencedirect.com/journal/review-of-economic-dynamics/about/editorial-board** — Elsevier editorial board page. Verifies: **Loukas Karabarbounis** listed as Coordinating Editor (current as of 2025–2026).
- **https://ideas.repec.org/s/red/ccodes.html** — IDEAS/RePEc "Review of Economic Dynamics — Computer Codes" series. Verifies: RED maintains a dedicated, citable replication-archive series indexed on RePEc.

## Guide for Authors (extracted, not directly readable in full)

- **https://www.sciencedirect.com/journal/review-of-economic-dynamics/publish/guide-for-authors** — Elsevier Guide for Authors. Source of: abstract **not exceeding 250 words** and able to stand alone (references in the abstract discouraged); **single-anonymized** review with a **minimum of two reviewers** after an editor suitability (desk) screen; **author-year (Harvard-style)** reference/citation system; **1 to 6 keywords**; generative-AI use must be declared at submission; free **SSRN preprint** posting offered during submission (manuscript made public once it passes initial desk review); LaTeX accepted via Elsevier's `elsarticle` class with "your paper your way" initial formatting.
  - **待核实**: this page returned **HTTP 403** to direct automated fetch; the items above were captured via search-engine extraction rather than a direct page read, so the exact current wording is not independently double-verified.

## History / publisher / scope

- **https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Review_of_Economic_Dynamics** — Verifies: publisher **Elsevier on behalf of the Society for Economic Dynamics**; quarterly; published since **1998**; **ISSN 1094-2025**.
  - **待核实** (editor-in-chief discrepancy): Wikipedia still lists **Matthias Doepke** as editor-in-chief, but the SED's own current editors page and the Elsevier editorial board list **Loukas Karabarbounis** as Coordinating Editor. This pack treats **Karabarbounis** as current, since the SED page is the authoritative/up-to-date source.
