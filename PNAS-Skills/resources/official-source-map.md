## Verified facts
- **Editor-in-Chief (verified 2026-06-23):** May R. Berenbaum, entomologist, University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign; listed as Editor-in-Chief on the official PNAS masthead (NAS President: Marcia K. McNutt). (#1, #4)
- **Publisher / owner:** National Academy of Sciences (NAS); PNAS is the official journal of the NAS. Multidisciplinary. (#1)
- **APC / fees:** Open-access publication charge approximately **$4,975** (immediate OA with site license) / **$5,475** (without site license); **delayed (after embargo) OA approximately $2,575.** Discounts and waivers apply under transformative/read-and-publish agreements (e.g. UC, Jisc, Max Planck) and for lower-income-country corresponding authors. **Confirm the live figure on the publication-charges page — fees change yearly.** (#2)
- **ISSN:** 0027-8424 (print) / 1091-6490 (online). (#1)
- **Submission portal / review model:** Submit via the PNAS online system (pnascentral). Two tracks — **Direct Submission** (editor-assigned, standard peer review) and **Contributed Submission** (an NAS member communicates their own paper and arranges review). See `pnas-track`. (#3)

## Live-check rules
- Treat the Editor-in-Chief and APC figures as volatile; re-confirm on the masthead and the publication-charges page before submission.
- Submission tracks and member-contribution rules evolve — verify the current track options on the PNAS author center each cycle.
