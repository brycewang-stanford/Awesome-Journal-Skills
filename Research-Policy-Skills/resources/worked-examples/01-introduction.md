# Worked Example: Reframing an Introduction for Research Policy

This is a fictional example. It does not describe a real paper.

## Before

> This paper studies an important topic using a large dataset and several robustness checks. We contribute to the literature by adding new evidence and showing heterogeneous effects.

## Diagnosis

- The first sentence does not name the Research Policy audience or the journal's innovation, science policy, technology management, entrepreneurship, R&D, and knowledge production scope.
- The contribution could fit several siblings: Strategic Management Journal, Management Science, Industrial and Corporate Change, and Journal of Business Venturing.
- The mechanism words that matter here are absent: innovation system, science policy, patent evidence, R&D organization.

## After

> This paper asks how **innovation system** changes the decision margin that Research Policy readers care about. The design ties the estimate to **science policy**, separates the main mechanism from **patent evidence**, and reports uncertainty in a way that makes the policy or theory implication portable beyond the study setting.
