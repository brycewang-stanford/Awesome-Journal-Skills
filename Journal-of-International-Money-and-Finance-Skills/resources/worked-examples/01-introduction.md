# Worked Example: Reframing an Introduction for Journal of International Money and Finance

This is a fictional example. It does not describe a real paper.

## Before

> This paper studies an important topic using a large dataset and several robustness checks. We contribute to the literature by adding new evidence and showing heterogeneous effects.

## Diagnosis

- The first sentence does not name the JIMF audience or the journal's international finance, exchange rates, global banking, capital flows, and open-economy macro-finance scope.
- The contribution could fit several siblings: Journal of International Economics, Journal of Monetary Economics, JMCB, and Journal of Financial Economics.
- The mechanism words that matter here are absent: exchange-rate pass-through, capital-flow shocks, global financial cycle, sovereign risk.

## After

> This paper asks how **exchange-rate pass-through** changes the decision margin that JIMF readers care about. The design ties the estimate to **capital-flow shocks**, separates the main mechanism from **global financial cycle**, and reports uncertainty in a way that makes the policy or theory implication portable beyond the study setting.
