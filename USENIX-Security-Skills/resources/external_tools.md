## Author-side verification passes

Run each pass before the relevant deadline; do not infer any of it from a previous cycle.

- **Cycle and portal**: confirm which cycle you are targeting and register on that cycle's
  HotCRP site — both cycles' sites can be live in overlapping windows.
- **Registration record**: title, abstract (the real one), full author list, topics, and
  conflicts entered before the registration deadline; these freeze a week before the paper
  deadline.
- **Format**: unaltered USENIX Security LaTeX template, ≤ 13-page body, the "Ethical
  Considerations" and "Open Science" appendices present and in order before the references, no
  whitespace tampering.
- **Anonymity**: author identity, self-references, funding acknowledgements, GitHub/repo links,
  and PDF metadata all stripped; artifact pointers go to an anonymous mirror.
- **Ethics + Open Science content**: disclosure timeline and IRB/equivalent documented where
  live systems or human subjects are involved; artifacts enumerated with resolvable locations
  or justified omission.
- **Artifact**: anonymous mirror retrievable from a logged-out session; claims-to-commands map
  drafted for later Phase-1/Phase-2 evaluation.
