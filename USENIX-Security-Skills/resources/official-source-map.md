reopen the current Call for Papers, the per-cycle HotCRP deadline pages, the Call for Artifacts,

## Verified 2026/2027 facts used in skills

- **USENIX Security '26** is the 35th USENIX Security Symposium, August 12-14, 2026, at the
  Baltimore Marriott Waterfront, Baltimore, MD (source 1). Program co-chairs: Elissa Redmiles
  (Georgetown) and Ben Stock (CISPA).
- **Two cycles per year.** Sec '26 Cycle 1: registration Aug 19, 2025; submission Aug 26, 2025;
  early reject Oct 7, 2025; notification Dec 4, 2025; finals Jan 15, 2026. Cycle 2: registration
  Jan 29, 2026; submission Feb 5, 2026; early reject Mar 17, 2026; notification May 14, 2026;
  finals Jun 11, 2026. All submission deadlines 11:59 pm AoE (sources 2, 8).
- **Format.** Main body ≤ 13 pages in the unaltered USENIX Security LaTeX template, immediately
  followed by mandatory "Ethical Considerations" and "Open Science" appendices (up to one page
  each) before the references; whitespace tampering forbidden (source 2).
- **No more Major Revisions.** Beginning '26, the venue retired Invited-Major-Revision; the
  intermediate outcome is "Accepted on Shepherd Approval" with at most a two-week shepherding
  pass (source 2).
- **Open Science is mandatory.** Every submission includes an Open Science appendix stating where
  its artifacts live; paper acceptance is conditional on Phase-1 artifact-availability
  verification before finals are due (sources 2, 3).
- **Two-phase artifact evaluation.** Phase-1 (availability) is mandatory and acceptance-conditional;
  Phase-2 (Functional, Results Reproduced) is optional and runs after finals. Three badges issued
  independently (sources 3, 4).
- **Ethics.** Vulnerability disclosure expected as soon as discovered absent strong justification;
  IRB consultation documented where research can cause harm; a subsection/appendix required for
  experiments against live systems (source 5).
- **USENIX Security '27** is the 36th Symposium, August 11-13, 2027, Denver, CO. Program co-chairs
  Adam Doupe (Arizona State) and Andrei Sabelfeld (Chalmers). Cycle 1: registration Aug 18, 2026;
  submission Aug 25, 2026. Cycle 2: registration Jan 19, 2027; submission Jan 26, 2027 (source 7).
- **USENIX ATC ended after 2025**, making USENIX Security USENIX's flagship summer event; the ATC
  name continued under ACM SIGOPS sponsorship (source 9).
- **Open access.** USENIX publishes Security proceedings (and talk recordings) open access with no
  author fee (sources 1, 10 and the USENIX open-access policy).
