# CPS Submission Preflight Checklist

Copy-paste and tick before submitting to **SAGE Track (ScholarOne)**. Verify every volatile item on the
live SAGE author-instructions page for *Comparative Political Studies* (检索于 2026-06；以官网为准).

## Fit & contribution
- [ ] The paper makes a **comparative** claim (cross-national, subnational, or over-time) with leverage
- [ ] The contribution is stated on page one and **travels** to other regions/regime types
- [ ] Venue is correct: CPS (comparative-politics specialist), not APSR/AJPS/World Politics/IO/BJPS
- [ ] The single strongest rival explanation is adjudicated by the design/evidence

## Manuscript & formatting
- [ ] **Anonymized** main file (no names, affiliations, acknowledgments, grant numbers, or identifying metadata)
- [ ] Separate **title page** with all author/contact/acknowledgment info
- [ ] Word count **≤ 11,000** (references, tables, figures **excluded**); count reported as required
- [ ] **Unstructured abstract, ~150 words**, between title and body
- [ ] References in **APA-style author-date** (SAGE house); every in-text cite has a list entry and vice versa
- [ ] Double-spaced, 1" margins, 12-pt (Times New Roman preferred)
- [ ] Self-references neutralized ("our prior work" → "[Author, year]")

## Exhibits
- [ ] Main figure conveys the finding on its own; captions/notes give estimator, FE, clustering, N, CI level
- [ ] Cross-case exhibits ordered/grouped to reveal the comparative pattern
- [ ] Figures grayscale-safe and vector/high-res; exhibit numbers match the replication output

## Identifiers & transparency
- [ ] **ORCID** linked for the submitting author in SAGE Track (co-authors encouraged to link before acceptance)
- [ ] **Data availability statement** included (available where / why not shared)
- [ ] Quantitative papers: replication materials (data, code, log files, README, seeds, pinned versions)
      **staged for the CPS Dataverse** — final acceptance is gated on the deposit
- [ ] Restricted data: explanation + access instructions; editorial office consulted if sharing is required
- [ ] Optional **anonymized pre-analysis plan** attached as supplementary material; registered vs. exploratory marked

## Final pass
- [ ] Supplementary / online appendix complete and referenced from the main text
- [ ] Every printed result reproduces from the deposited script
- [ ] All claims re-verified against the live SAGE author-instructions page

→ After a decision, use `cps-rebuttal` to structure the response to reviewers and the editor.
