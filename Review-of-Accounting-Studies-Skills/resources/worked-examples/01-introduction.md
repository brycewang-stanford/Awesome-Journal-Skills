# Worked Example: Reframing an Introduction for Review of Accounting Studies

This is a fictional example. It does not describe a real paper.

## Before

> This paper studies an important topic using a large dataset and several robustness checks. We contribute to the literature by adding new evidence and showing heterogeneous effects.

## Diagnosis

- The first sentence does not name the RAST audience or the journal's analytical, empirical, and experimental accounting research with strong economics foundations scope.
- The contribution could fit several siblings: The Accounting Review, Journal of Accounting Research, Journal of Accounting and Economics, and Contemporary Accounting Research.
- The mechanism words that matter here are absent: accounting disclosure, audit quality, capital-market accounting, tax and reporting.

## After

> This paper asks how **accounting disclosure** changes the decision margin that RAST readers care about. The design ties the estimate to **audit quality**, separates the main mechanism from **capital-market accounting**, and reports uncertainty in a way that makes the policy or theory implication portable beyond the study setting.
