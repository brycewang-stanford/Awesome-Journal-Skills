## Verified on official pages (2026-06)

- **Submission fee: US$500**, payable within 7 days of submission (the manuscript is removed otherwise); on a desk rejection a refund is issued minus a US$75 administrative fee. Published quarterly by **Springer**. (Springer RAST submission guidelines)

## Verified editor (2026-06-22)

- **Lead editor:** RAST's lead editorial role is titled **Managing Editor**, currently **Lakshmanan (Shiva) Shivakumar** (London Business School) — corroborated by the official Springer editorial-board page (via search snippets; the page redirects to an auth gate on direct fetch), the LBS RAST page, and the LBS appointment announcement. (Paul E. Fischer, Wharton, served as an earlier editor c. 2015–2019 and may still appear as a co-/associate editor; the historical roster is not load-bearing here.) Re-verify the masthead before relying on it.
