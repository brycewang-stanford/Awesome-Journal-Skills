# Review of Accounting Studies Manuscript Skeleton

## Title

A concise title naming the mechanism or contribution.

## Abstract

State the question, design or review frame, key finding, and implication. Re-check any journal-specific word limit.

## 1. Introduction

- Problem for the RAST audience.
- Main result or synthesis.
- Contribution relative to The Accounting Review, Journal of Accounting Research, Journal of Accounting and Economics, and Contemporary Accounting Research.

## 2. Background and Framework

Use the terms that matter: accounting disclosure, audit quality, capital-market accounting, tax and reporting, earnings information.

## 3. Evidence / Model / Review Design

Document data, assumptions, review protocol, or qualitative coding.

## 4. Results or Synthesis

Report estimates, mechanisms, conceptual framework, or evidence map.

## 5. Robustness / Alternative Explanations

Organize by threat, not by appendix order.

## 6. Conclusion

Name what changes for the field and what remains unresolved.
