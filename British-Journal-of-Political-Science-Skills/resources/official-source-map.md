## Official journal, submission & author pages

- **https://www.cambridge.org/core/journals/british-journal-of-political-science** — BJPS on Cambridge
  Core (publisher home, current issues, aims and scope). Verifies: publisher = **Cambridge University
  Press**; a **broadly based** journal aiming to cover "developments across a wide range of countries
  and specialisms," drawing contributions from **all fields of political science** (political theory,
  political behaviour, public policy, international relations) and from **related disciplines**
  (sociology, social psychology, economics, philosophy); 50+ years of publication; widely recognised as
  a premier journal in the field.
- **https://www.cambridge.org/core/journals/british-journal-of-political-science/information/author-instructions**
  — BJPS Author Instructions. Verifies: article types — **Research Articles**, **Letters**,
  **Comments**; submission via the journal's online system (Editorial Manager, `editorialmanager.com/bjpols`;
  see 待核实 below on ScholarOne vs Editorial Manager); **Comments carry no article-processing charge**;
  Research Articles and Letters may be eligible for Gold Open Access via Cambridge agreements.
- **https://www.cambridge.org/core/journals/british-journal-of-political-science/information/instructions-contributors/style-guide**
  — BJPS style guide. Verifies: **Harvard author-date** referencing (in-text `(Elster 1983, 174)`;
  reference list `Elster J (1983) Explaining Technical Change. Cambridge: Cambridge University Press.`);
  tables fit within **190 × 120 mm**, figures within **200 × 133 mm at minimum 300 dpi**.
- **https://assets.cambridge.org/JPS/JPS_ifc.pdf** — BJPS Information for Contributors (IFC). Verifies:
  **abstract not exceeding 150 words** for all article types; manuscripts in **double spacing**, left
  margin ≥ 25 mm/1 inch, right margin ≥ 40 mm/1.5 inches, typesize ≥ **12 point**; accepted manuscripts
  in **MS Word or LaTeX**.

## Peer review & editorial policy

- **https://www.cambridge.org/core/journals/british-journal-of-political-science/information/author-instructions/preparing-your-materials**
  — Preparing your materials (peer-review preparation). Verifies: BJPS applies a **double-blind peer
  review process usually involving at least two referees**; for the anonymous version authors must remove
  **author names and affiliations, acknowledgements of funding or gratitude to specific readers and
  conference audiences, and links to personal or university websites**.

## Research transparency & replication-data policy

- **https://dataverse.harvard.edu/dataverse/BJPolS** — **British Journal of Political Science**
  collection on **Harvard Dataverse** (the BJPolS Dataverse). Verifies: replication materials are hosted
  on Harvard Dataverse; on upload the author receives **citation information including a DOI** for the
  data.
- BJPS replication policy (on the Cambridge author pages): Verifies: BJPolS is a **signatory of the Data
  Access and Research Transparency (DA-RT)** statement; **authors are not allowed to withhold information
  used to perform an analysis** featured in a BJPolS piece; **exemptions can be granted for
  restricted-access source data**; replication-policy questions go to the BJPolS editorial staff (待核实
  current contact email on the live page).

## Owner, publisher, editors, history

- **https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/British_Journal_of_Political_Science** — Verifies: BJPS founded
  **1971**; published quarterly by **Cambridge University Press**; covers "all aspects of political
  science"; associated with the **British Academy**; awards the **Brian Barry Prize in Political
  Science** (with CUP). Lists (at access time) eight editors: Shaun Bowler, René Lindstädt, Petra
  Schleiter, Lucas Leemann, Cristina Bodea, Paul Bou-Habib, Tobias Böhmelt, Ruth Dassonneville (待核实 —
  the masthead changes; confirm the current team on the journal's editorial-board page).
