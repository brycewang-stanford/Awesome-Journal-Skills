# JEP Pre-Submission Self-Check (8 sections)

A last-pass checklist for a *Journal of Economic Perspectives* (JEP) proposal or
manuscript before emailing the editorial team. Verify volatile specifics (editor
roster, submission address, exact front-matter and disclosure rules) on the
official AEA/JEP pages (检索于 2026-06；以官网为准).

## 1. Venue fit (is it JEP, not a sibling?)
- [ ] An accessible **synthesis for a broad audience**, not a new research result (else: AER/AEJ/field journal)
- [ ] Not the long, technical **survey of record** (else: Journal of Economic Literature, JEL)
- [ ] Rigorous **synthesis**, not advocacy or an op-ed
- [ ] Broadly interesting + timely ("why now") + synthesizable for non-specialists

## 2. Proposal vs. manuscript
- [ ] Approaching cold → a **2–5 page proposal** (preferred over a finished manuscript)
- [ ] Invited → draft matches the **agreed scope / angle / length**
- [ ] Symposium piece → stays within the assigned angle; does not re-cover companion pieces

## 3. Accessibility (the JEP bar)
- [ ] Readable by **~90% of AEA members**; useful to economists outside the subfield
- [ ] **No equations or proofs in the body**; ideas explained via economic intuition
- [ ] Subfield jargon defined on first use or removed; no method-flexing

## 4. Narrative & argument
- [ ] Opens with a hook (puzzle/fact/example/debate), not "the literature on…"
- [ ] One stateable through-line; body organized by ideas, not by paper
- [ ] 3–5 takeaways land explicitly; "what we don't know" included

## 5. Evidence & exhibits
- [ ] Effect sizes in plain units; a few load-bearing numbers, well-sourced
- [ ] Precision conveyed as ranges / degree of agreement — **no significance asterisks**
- [ ] Exhibits self-explanatory (finding-title + caption); no coefficient tables; grayscale-safe

## 6. Balance & objectivity
- [ ] Competing views steelmanned; verdict driven by weight of evidence
- [ ] Positive findings separated from normative/policy judgments
- [ ] Author's stake disclosed where relevant; uncertainty stated, not rounded to consensus

## 7. Front matter & house style
- [ ] Plain-language, hook-forward **abstract** (no machinery)
- [ ] **JEL codes** + keywords per current AEA guidance
- [ ] Written for clarity (AEA house style applied by the copyeditor at acceptance)

## 8. Declarations & sending
- [ ] **AEA disclosure** statement (interested-party ≥ US$10,000 over 3 yrs; funding sources or "none")
- [ ] **AI not listed as an author**; data & code aware (required at conditional acceptance if empirical)
- [ ] Sent as **Word or PDF** by email to **jep@aeapubs.org** (re-confirmed on the official page)
