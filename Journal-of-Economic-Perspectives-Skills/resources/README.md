# Journal of Economic Perspectives — Resources

Capability layer for the **Journal of Economic Perspectives (JEP)** skill pack.
JEP is the AEA's **open-access, non-technical synthesis** journal: a piece distills
a body of economics into an accessible argument for a broad audience, **not** a new
identified result. Accordingly this pack **ships no `code/` pipeline** — there is
no dataset to clean and no regression to run. The actionable craft here is
**topic fit, narrative, plain-language translation, evidence-without-equations,
exhibits for general readers, balance, and the editor-driven process.**

> **Synthesis venue — no code kit.** Unlike the empirical-economics packs in this
> repository, JEP work is communication, not estimation. The shared
> empirical-methods hub is linked below only as **background for judging the
> evidence a synthesis cites** — never as a pipeline to run.

## How to use

1. **Screen fit** with [`../skills/jep-topic-selection`](../skills/jep-topic-selection/SKILL.md):
   broadly interesting, timely, synthesizable, and survives translation?
2. **Pitch first** with [`../skills/jep-proposal-and-symposium`](../skills/jep-proposal-and-symposium/SKILL.md) —
   a 2–5 page proposal, not a manuscript.
3. **Build the essay** — model the opening on
   [`worked-examples/01-introduction.md`](worked-examples/01-introduction.md);
   structure with [`../skills/jep-narrative-arc`](../skills/jep-narrative-arc/SKILL.md);
   translate with [`../skills/jep-accessibility-and-translation`](../skills/jep-accessibility-and-translation/SKILL.md);
   present evidence via [`../skills/jep-evidence-without-equations`](../skills/jep-evidence-without-equations/SKILL.md).
4. **Judge the evidence you cite** — when a study anchors a claim, sanity-check its
   design against the shared **reviewer-objection-checklist** and
   **reporting-standards** (background hub above) before featuring it.
5. **Benchmark** against verified JEP pieces in
   [`exemplars/library.md`](exemplars/library.md) — and confirm you are not
   confusing JEP with **JEL** or the AER.
6. **Before sending** — walk this pack's
   [`../skills/jep-submission`](../skills/jep-submission/SKILL.md) preflight and the
   [`official-source-map.md`](official-source-map.md) for venue facts.

> The shared-hub material is venue-neutral and used here only as **background** for
> assessing cited evidence; always defer to this pack's skills and
> `official-source-map.md` for what JEP specifically requires.
