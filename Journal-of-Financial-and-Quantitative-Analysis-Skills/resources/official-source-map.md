# JFQA Official Source Map

Primary and authoritative sources behind the JFQA-specific facts injected into this skill pack.
Access date for all entries: **2026-06-01**. Re-verify volatile items (editors, fees, policy wording, formatting rules) on the live pages before relying on them — journal pages change. Items not confirmable from an official page are flagged **需复核 (to be verified)**.

## Official journal & submission pages

- **https://jfqa.org/submissions/** — JFQA submissions page. Verifies:
  - Published through **Cambridge University Press** by the **University of Washington Foster School of Business**.
  - Submission via **Editorial Manager** (`editorialmanager.com/jfqa/`); an account separate from a Cambridge Core account is required.
  - **$350 submission fee** (**re-verified 2026-06-22, unchanged**), credit card only; a **$275 refund** is issued if the Managing Editor does not send the paper to a reviewer (a desk reject therefore retains a **$75** handling charge).
  - **Abstract: one paragraph, no more than 100 words.**
  - Formatting: **8.5 × 11 inch paper, 1-inch margins, 12-pt Times New Roman, double-spaced** main body and appendices; uploaded as a single **text-searchable PDF** sent blind to the reviewer(s).
  - **One-year submission ban**: resubmitting a substantially-modified previously-rejected paper without disclosing the prior rejection in the cover letter leads to desk rejection and a one-year bar on further submissions from the corresponding author.
  - No fixed word/page limit, but excessively long submissions are likely to be desk rejected.
  - Final formatting governed by the **"Style Guide for Accepted and Conditionally Accepted Papers"** and the **"Style Sheet for Copyeditors and Proofreaders."**

- **https://jfqa.org/jfqa-code-sharing-policy/** — JFQA Code Sharing Policy. Verifies:
  - Mandatory for submissions made **on/after January 1, 2024** if accepted; voluntary for earlier submissions.
  - At acceptance and before online publication authors must post: (1) source code reproducing all reported findings from the raw data; (2) the raw datasets, or a **pseudo dataset** with enough observations that all programs execute if data are restricted by copyright/confidentiality; (3) a **"read me"** file documenting software, languages, and data formats plus a roadmap of program execution order.
  - Materials archived in the **JFQA Dataverse** hosted at the **Harvard University Dataverse** as supplemental materials.
  - **Exceptions** (e.g., delayed code sharing) must be requested **on the initial submission** and are decided by the handling editor; approved exceptions are noted in the published paper.
  - JFQA may use **external verification services** to validate code for randomly selected publications.
  - Code is licensed for **academic research only** and users must acknowledge the code's origin.

- **https://www.cambridge.org/core/journals/journal-of-financial-and-quantitative-analysis/information/author-instructions** — Cambridge Core author instructions. Verifies:
  - Published by Cambridge University Press; submission via Editorial Manager (`editorialmanager.com/jfqa/default.aspx`).
  - "This journal uses a **double-anonymous** model of peer review."
  - The journal prints **less than 9%** of the more than **1,000** manuscripts submitted annually.
  - A submission account separate from a Cambridge Core account is required.

- **https://jfqa.org/jfqa-home/staff/** — JFQA staff page (**re-verified 2026-06-22**). Verifies:
  - Seven **Managing Editors** (current as of 2026-06-22): **Hendrik Bessembinder** (Arizona State University), **Ran Duchin** (Boston College), **Thierry Foucault** (HEC Paris), **Jarrad Harford** (University of Washington), **Kai Li** (University of British Columbia), **George Pennacchi** (University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign), **Stephan Siegel** (University of Washington). JFQA has **no single Editor-in-Chief** — papers are assigned to one of the Managing Editors.
  - **UPCOMING CHANGE (announced 4 June 2026):** George Pennacchi retires and **steps down as Managing Editor effective 1 September 2026** (he keeps handling in-process/R&R papers, no new submissions); **Mariassunta Giannetti** (Stockholm School of Economics) replaces him. Re-verify the live slate at/after that date.
  - Advisory editors and 50+ associate editors; sponsoring institution = Foster School of Business, University of Washington.

## Scope (from publisher description)

- JFQA publishes **theoretical and empirical** research in financial economics: **corporate finance, investments, capital and security markets, financial institutions**, and **quantitative methods** relevant to finance. Source: jfqa.org / Cambridge Core journal homepage.
