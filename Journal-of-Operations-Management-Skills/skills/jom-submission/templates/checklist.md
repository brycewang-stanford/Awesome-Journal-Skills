# JOM Pre-Submission Checklist

> A completed checklist must accompany every JOM manuscript — "Submissions will not be considered in the absence of this checklist." Confirm current requirements on the Wiley JOM Author Guidelines and the JOM author-resource pages; items marked 待核实 in `resources/official-source-map.md` need verification.

## 1. Fit & scope
- [ ] Operations/supply-chain phenomenon is at the **heart** of the research question, not just the context
- [ ] Study is **empirical** (survey / archival-secondary / behavioral experiment / field-case / intervention-based / meta-analysis) — not a purely analytical or optimization model
- [ ] Engages and advances OM-relevant theory
- [ ] Has clear practical relevance for operations managers (for-profit or nonprofit)

## 2. Department routing
- [ ] Preferred Department chosen from the 12
- [ ] Alternate Department chosen and marked as alternate
- [ ] Framing engages the preferred Department's mission/literature

## 3. Theory & hypotheses
- [ ] Operations mechanism stated (operational / behavioral / organizational)
- [ ] Hypotheses derived a priori (no HARKing)
- [ ] Mediators/moderators specified and measured

## 4. Design & analysis
- [ ] Design matches the claim and the unit/level of analysis
- [ ] Measurement validity (survey): reliability, CFA, AVE/discriminant validity
- [ ] Common-method remedies (survey) reported beyond a single-factor test
- [ ] Endogeneity/identification strategy stated and tested (archival)
- [ ] Manipulation/attention checks reported (experiment)
- [ ] Estimator matches design; SEs clustered appropriately; effect sizes reported
- [ ] Ready for the Empirical Research Methods Department method check

## 5. Exhibits
- [ ] Descriptives + correlation table (reliabilities on diagonal for survey)
- [ ] Result tables with SEs, clustering noted, N, effect sizes
- [ ] Interaction/contingency plots for moderation
- [ ] Mechanism/process or data-structure figure
- [ ] APA-consistent, self-explanatory exhibits

## 6. Formatting
- [ ] ~40 pages or fewer (over-long manuscripts are returned for streamlining)
- [ ] Double-spaced, single-column, 12-point, one-inch margins
- [ ] Pages numbered; no running headers/footers
- [ ] APA citation/reference style (consistent format)
- [ ] Abstract within limit (待核实 — confirm current word limit)

## 7. Integrity & similarity
- [ ] iThenticate/CrossCheck run; no single source >~1% (outside quotations)
- [ ] Overall similarity <15%, or justified in the cover letter
- [ ] Not under review elsewhere; preprints handled per policy

## 8. Anonymization (double-anonymous, asymmetric)
- [ ] Author names, affiliations, acknowledgements removed from manuscript
- [ ] Self-identifying citations masked; file metadata stripped
- [ ] Full author identities entered in the Wiley ReX portal fields only

## 9. Disclosures & data
- [ ] AI use disclosed
- [ ] Prior dissertation/conference version disclosed
- [ ] Prior use of the data disclosed and cited
- [ ] Previous reject-and-resubmit (if any) disclosed
- [ ] Wiley Data Availability Statement included
- [ ] ORCID linked

## 10. Cover letter
- [ ] 2 Departments (one preferred) named
- [ ] ≥3 Associate Editors and ≥3 ERB reviewers recommended, conflict-free
- [ ] All disclosures restated; similarity justified if needed
