## VERIFIED facts injected into the skills (with source #)

- **Publisher / owner:** Published by **Wiley** on behalf of the **Association for Supply Chain Management (ASCM, formerly APICS)**; ASCM owns the journal. (#1, #7)
- **Mission / scope:** Original, *empirical* operations management research with both academic and practical relevance, where **operations are at the heart of the research question, not just in the context** (manufacturing, service, and supply-chain operations; for-profit and nonprofit). (#1)
- **Empirical-only mandate:** JOM explicitly does **not** publish purely analytical models or optimization techniques — "these belong in operations research, industrial engineering, or analytical OM journals." "It is the observation that renders the research empirical." (#1, #2)
- **Dominant methodology:** Empirical OM — behavioral/empirical OM, survey, archival/secondary-data, field, case, experimental, and intervention-based research, paired with meaningful theoretical argumentation. (#1, #2)
- **12 Departments (routing):** Healthcare Operations; Innovation and Project Management; Inter-organizational Operations; Intervention Based Research; Operational Systems; Operations Interfaces; Public Policy and Industry Studies; Social Impact; Strategy and Organization; Sustainable Operations; Technology Management; Empirical Research Methods. (#3)
- **Empirical Research Methods Department:** Publishes methods papers AND performs method checks on incoming empirical submissions. (#3)
- **Review model:** Double-anonymous (double-blind), with an **asymmetric identity rule** — author identities are shared with the Editors-in-Chief and Department Editors but **not** with reviewers or Associate Editors (and vice versa). (#2)
- **Cover-letter protocol:** Must specify **2 Departments (one marked preferred)** and recommend **at least 3 Associate Editors and at least 3 Editorial Review Board (ERB) reviewers** free of conflicts. (#2)
- **Disclosures:** AI use, prior dissertation/conference versions, prior data use, and any previous "reject and resubmit" decisions must be disclosed. (#2)
- **Submission system:** Wiley Research Exchange (ReX) at wiley.atyponrex.com/journal/JOOM. (#2, #6)
- **Submission checklist:** A completed checklist must accompany the manuscript — "Submissions will not be considered in the absence of this checklist." (#5)
- **Length:** No strict page limit, but most manuscripts should not exceed **~40 pages**; very long manuscripts are returned for streamlining before review. (#5)
- **Formatting:** Double-spaced, single-column, 12-point font, one-inch margins, numbered pages, no running headers/footers. (#5)
- **Citation style:** **APA** for citations/references; any consistent format accepted at first submission (journal style preferred); Wiley applies final reference styling at proof stage. (#5)
- **Similarity / text overlap:** iThenticate/CrossCheck screening; no single source should account for more than **~1%** of wording (outside quotations); overall similarity **>15%** must be justified in the cover letter. (#5)
- **Editors:** Co-Editors-in-Chief **Elliot Bendoly** (The Ohio State University) and **Rogelio Oliva** (Texas A&M University); Managing Editor **Jamie Sanchagrin**. (#4)
- **Fees / open access:** **No submission fee**; hybrid open access — subscription publication is free; an Article Processing Charge applies only if the author chooses CC-BY open access. (#1, #7)
- **Data policy:** As a Wiley journal, JOM requires a **Data Availability Statement** for research- and synthesis-based articles, encourages public archiving of data/code/scripts/materials with a persistent identifier (DOI/accession number), and asks that shared data be cited. (#8)
- **History / index:** Established **1980**; ISSN 0272-6963 (print) / 1873-1317 (web); **8 issues/year**; hybrid open access; on the **Financial Times FT50** list. (#7)
