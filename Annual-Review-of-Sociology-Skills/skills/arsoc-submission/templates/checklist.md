# ARSoc Delivery Self-Check

A pre-delivery checklist for a **commissioned** *Annual Review of Sociology* invited review.
Re-confirm every volatile item (检索于 2026-06；以官网为准) on the Annual Reviews author pages
before relying on it. ARSoc does **not** accept unsolicited finished manuscripts.

## Article type & intake
- [ ] This is an **invited/commissioned** article delivered in the invited-article workflow
- [ ] No attempt to submit cold (ARSoc commissions topics; you do not self-submit a finished review)
- [ ] Scope agreed with the commissioning editor in writing (traditions + methods in/out)

## Manuscript
- [ ] Title page: title, complete author affiliations, keywords
- [ ] Review-style abstract (the subfield, why-now, organizing idea, headline takeaway, forward agenda) — not a primary-paper abstract
- [ ] An explicit **organizing framework / spine**, not an annotated bibliography
- [ ] The close names a concrete **forward research agenda**, not "more research is needed"
- [ ] References complete and accurate (Annual Reviews house style applied at production)
- [ ] Length within the volume envelope (exact word/abstract limits 待核实 — confirm)

## Exhibits
- [ ] Who-found-what table(s) ordered by framework cell; comparable objects only; mode + credibility noted
- [ ] Conceptual/framework figure renders the spine and is self-contained
- [ ] Figure specs met; reproduced exhibits have permission + attribution

## Transparency
- [ ] Coverage account documented (databases, dates, terms/traditions, inclusion/exclusion, saturation, counts)
- [ ] Qualitative / computational / demographic / theoretical modes all covered in the search
- [ ] Any meta-analysis/bibliometrics: data + code deposited with a citable DOI + stated protocol

## Declarations
- [ ] Conflict-of-interest / potential-bias disclosure prepared per Annual Reviews policy
- [ ] Funding sources stated
- [ ] AI not listed as an author

- [ ] Exact length and abstract limits
- [ ] Reference and format style
