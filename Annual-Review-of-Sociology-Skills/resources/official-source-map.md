## Sibling boundary

- **ASR (American Sociological Review) / AJS (American Journal of Sociology)** — sociology's primary-research flagships; they publish original empirical findings, ARSoc does not.
- **Sociological Theory** — theory *development*, not a survey of evidence.
- **Annual Review of Psychology / Annual Review of Economics** — sister Annual Reviews series in adjacent disciplines; same intake model, different field.
- Handbook chapters — deeper/longer/exhaustive; ARSoc reviews are bounded and accessible.
