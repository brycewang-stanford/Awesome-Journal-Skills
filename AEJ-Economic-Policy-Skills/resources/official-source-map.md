# AEJ: Economic Policy — Official Source Map

Primary and authoritative sources behind every AEJ: Policy-specific fact injected into this skill pack.
**Access date for all entries: 2026-06.** AEJ: Economic Policy is published by the **American Economic
Association (AEA)**; the AEA owns and operates the editorial and publication process. Re-verify volatile
items (editors, submission fee, exact policy effective dates) before relying on them — AEA pages change.
Items that could not be confirmed from a primary fetch are marked **待核实 (to be verified)**.
Volatile facts are also tagged **(检索于 2026-06；以官网为准)** in the skills themselves.

## Official AEA / AEJ: Policy pages (publisher / editorial authority)

- **https://www.aeaweb.org/journals/pol** — AEJ: Economic Policy journal home (AEA). Verifies:
  the journal "focuses on the role of economic policy in economic outcomes," covering **public
  economics; urban and regional economics; public-policy aspects of health, education, welfare and
  political institutions; law and economics; economic regulation; and environmental economics**; it
  **publishes four issues per year (quarterly)**; **ISSN 1945-7731 (print) / 1945-774X (online)**; links
  to submission guidelines, the style guide, and reviewer guidelines. Accessed 2026-06.
- **https://www.aeaweb.org/journals/pol/submissions** — AEJ: Policy submission guidelines (AEA submission
  system). Verifies: manuscripts are submitted and **double-blind peer reviewed** through the AEA system;
  JEL codes and keywords requested; a submission fee applies (amount and reduced/waived categories
  **VOLATILE — 待核实**, see Unverified). Accessed 2026-06. **待核实 partial:** the live fee figure and the
  precise format limits are AEA-portal-rendered and should be confirmed on the page at submission time.
- **https://www.aeaweb.org/journals/policies/disclosure-policy** — AEA Disclosure Policy (applies to all
  AEA journals incl. AEJ: Policy). Verifies: each author must disclose interested-party financial
  relationships and funding sources relevant to the research. Accessed 2026-06.
- **https://www.aeaweb.org/journals/policies/data-code** — AEA Data and Code Availability Policy (applies
  to all AEA journals incl. AEJ: Policy). Verifies: the **AEA Data Editor** "will assess compliance with
  this policy, including by conducting **reproducibility checks** and verifying the accuracy of the
  information provided," **before final acceptance**; the **AEA Data and Code Repository** (hosted at
  **openICPSR**) is strongly encouraged and the Data Editor has automatic access to draft deposits; for
  data that cannot be made public, authors must preserve materials ≥5 years, make code public, and
  document sources, and **"it is generally not acceptable that data be provided 'upon request' if the
  request must be approved by the authors themselves."** Accessed 2026-06.

## AEA-wide policy regime (shared across AER / AEJ family)

- **AEA Data Editor.** The AEA Data Editor (Lars Vilhuber) runs the reproducibility / data-availability
  process for AEA journals. Verifies the **pre-publication** reproducibility check and the openICPSR
  deposit workflow referenced in `aejpol-replication-package`. Accessed 2026-06.
  (Lead-editor / Data-Editor personnel can change — re-confirm.)
- **AEA submission system & double-blind review.** AEA journals use a common submission system; AEJ
  family review is **double-blind**, so the submitted manuscript must be anonymized. Verifies the
  anonymization requirements in `aejpol-submission`. Accessed 2026-06.
