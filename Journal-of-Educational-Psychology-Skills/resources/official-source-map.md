# Journal of Educational Psychology Official Source Map

Primary and authoritative sources behind the Journal of Educational Psychology (JEP)-specific facts

## Official journal & submission pages (APA)

- **https://www.apa.org/pubs/journals/edu**
  — Journal of Educational Psychology home (APA). Verifies: **publisher** is the **American Psychological
  Association (APA)**; **purpose** is to publish **original, primary psychological research pertaining to
  education across all ages and educational levels**, with **occasional** publication of **exceptionally
  important meta-analyses**; the journal **does not typically publish reliability/validity studies of
  specific tests/instruments**; **ISSN 0022-0663**, **eISSN 1939-2176**; published **eight times a year**;
  submissions handled through **Editorial Manager** (`editorialmanager.com/edu`).
- **https://www.apa.org/pubs/journals/edu/submit**
  — Journal of Educational Psychology submission guidelines (APA). Verifies: manuscripts **generally should
  not exceed 12,000 words** (≈ 40 double-spaced pages, 12-pt Times New Roman), **not including references,
  tables, figures, and appendixes**; **abstract maximum 250 words** on a separate page; **masked review for
  all submissions** (identities of authors and reviewers masked) — authors must avoid revealing identity
  via grant numbers, IRB/institution names, self-citations, or linked repositories, and use third-person
  attribution for their own work; **APA Publication Manual, 7th edition**, double-spaced; manuscripts in
  **Word (.docx)** or **LaTeX (.tex zip + PDF)**; a **Transparency and Openness** subsection states whether
  **data, materials, and analysis code** are in trusted repositories with **persistent identifiers** (or
  unavailable); **preregistration encouraged but not mandatory** (disclose status, provide links);
  **JARS** (quantitative/qualitative/mixed) and **JARS-REC** reporting standards apply; **Open Science
  Badges** (Open Data, Open Data with Protected Access, Open Materials, Preregistered, Preregistered+
  Analysis Plan) available with a **signed badge disclosure form**; **no submission fee** ("submitting a
  manuscript for peer review is free of charge").

## APA reporting standards & open science

- **https://apastyle.apa.org/jars** — APA Journal Article Reporting Standards (JARS). Verifies: the JARS
  quantitative, qualitative, and mixed-methods standards, and **JARS-REC** for reporting on race,
  ethnicity, and culture — the standards JEP asks authors to follow.
- **https://www.apa.org/pubs/journals/resources/open-science** — APA open-science / transparency framework
  (TOP-aligned). Verifies: the Transparency and Openness reporting expectations and Open Science Badges.

## Editor & owner

- **https://www.apa.org/pubs/journals/resources/editor-conversations/open-science-educational-psychology**
  — APA editor conversation. Verifies: **Panayiota Kendeou** is the *prior* Editor-in-Chief (open-science
  emphasis). The **current Editor-in-Chief is Young-Suk Kim (University of California, Irvine)** —
  **verified 2026-06-22** (manuscripts are now submitted to her editorial office, per APA). Re-confirm
  the exact term on the live APA masthead, as the editorship rotates.
- **https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Journal_of_Educational_Psychology** — Verifies: JEP is published by the
  **APA**; **founded 1910**; **8 issues per year**; ISSN 0022-0663 / 1939-2176; scope is psychological
  research on education at all ages/levels with occasional theoretical/review articles.
