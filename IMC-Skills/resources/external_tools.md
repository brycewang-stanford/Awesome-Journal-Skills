# External Tools - ACM IMC

Access date: 2026-07-09

Use these surfaces after reopening the current IMC cycle pages. IMC mechanics — the two deadline
dates, page limits, One-Shot-Revision rules, the Ethics-statement wording, and the artifact
declaration — are decided per edition, and the `conferences.sigcomm.org`/`dl.acm.org` gateway may
block direct fetches (read via search renderings and cross-check ACM DL and dblp). Do not confuse
this venue with the Leeds *International Medieval Congress*, the *Indian Management Conclave*, or
any IEEE "IMC" services track.

- IMC conference series (SIGCOMM): https://www.sigcomm.org/events/imc-conference
- IMC 2026 home (Karlsruhe, Oct 12-16, 2026): https://conferences.sigcomm.org/imc/2026/
- IMC 2026 call for papers: https://conferences.sigcomm.org/imc/2026/cfp/
- IMC 2026 submission instructions (acmart, BBL, double-blind, Ethics): https://conferences.sigcomm.org/imc/2026/submission-instructions/
- IMC 2026 committees: https://conferences.sigcomm.org/imc/2026/committees/
- IMC 2026 camera-ready instructions: https://conferences.sigcomm.org/imc/2026/camera-ready-instructions/
- IMC 2026 main-track HotCRP (cycle-specific instances): https://imc2026.hotcrp.com/
- IMC 2026 Replicability Track EoI HotCRP: https://imc2026-repro.hotcrp.com/
- IMC 2025 call (page-limit basis): https://conferences.sigcomm.org/imc/2025/cfp/

## Cross-check surfaces (when the gateway blocks the primary page)

- ACM DL IMC proceedings (per-year tables of contents; IMC '25 = 10.1145/3730567).
- dblp IMC proceedings stream: https://dblp.org/db/conf/imc/index.html — for exemplar
  verification and prior-edition cross-check.
- The IMC HotCRP `deadlines` pages for the machine-readable cutoffs the CFP paraphrases.
- The noise-lab networking-deadlines aggregator and wikicfp IMC entry for a quick cross-check of
  the current cycle dates (verify against the official call before relying on them).

## Author-side checks

- HotCRP profile, conflicts, coauthor emails, topic tags, and the **paper-registration** step
  that precedes each cycle's full-paper deadline.
- `acmart` compliance, the current page budget (IMC 2025: 13-page full / 6-page short body +
  unlimited references/appendix), the **BBL** upload, and double-blind PDF export.
- Double-blind sweep: anonymize the PDF, the dataset/code links, tool names, and — critically —
  any vantage-point or infrastructure detail that uniquely identifies your group or institution.
- The **Ethics** section/appendix (Belmont principles; IRB determination form attached and
  anonymized for human-subjects/user-data work; responsible-disclosure plan for
  vulnerability/exposure findings).
- The **artifact-availability declaration** (full / partial / none) with an honest justification
  if none.
- Cycle choice (deadline 1 vs deadline 2) and awareness of the One-Shot-Revision path across
  deadlines.
- Community Contribution Award intent: a public dataset/tool/platform by the camera-ready
  deadline.

## Do not infer

- Do not infer the deadline dates or the registration-to-submission gap between editions; IMC
  moves them each year. Confirm on the current Important Dates / call page.
- Do not assume the page limit, `acmart` variant, One-Shot-Revision action-point count, Ethics
  wording, or artifact-declaration options carry over between editions.
- Do not assume IMC awards formal ACM reproducibility badges, or that a "public review" is
  published for accepted papers, without confirming on the current edition (**待核实**).
- If pages disagree, treat the newest call, submission-instructions page, HotCRP deadline page,
  or a direct chair announcement as controlling, and record the conflict.
