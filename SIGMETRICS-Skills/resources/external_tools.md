# External Tools - ACM SIGMETRICS

Access date: 2026-07-09

Use these surfaces after reopening the current SIGMETRICS cycle pages. SIGMETRICS mechanics — the
three deadline dates, the page budget, the one-shot-revision rules, the track list, any artifact
track — are decided per edition, and the `sigmetrics.org` / `dl.acm.org` / `*.hotcrp.com` gateway
may block direct fetches (read via search renderings and cross-check ACM DL and dblp). Do not
confuse this venue with **IMC** (ACM Internet Measurement Conference), **SIGCOMM/NSDI**
(networking systems), or **SIGMOD** (databases).

## Cross-check surfaces (when the gateway blocks the primary page)

- ACM DL POMACS tables of contents (Vol N, Issue 1/2/3 map to the three rolling deadlines).
- dblp POMACS stream: https://dblp.org/db/journals/pomacs/index.html
- dblp SIGMETRICS proceedings for pre-POMACS editions and exemplar verification.
- OpenAccept SIGMETRICS statistics: https://openaccept.org/c/sys/sigmetrics/
- The specific per-deadline HotCRP `deadlines` page for the machine-readable cutoff the CFP

## Author-side checks

- Pick the **deadline** (summer / fall / winter) whose notification and POMACS issue timing fits
  your plan; register the abstract about a week before the paper deadline (the two are separate).
- Select the correct **track** (Theory / Measurement & Applied Modeling / Learning / Operational
  Systems) on the submission site; a second track only for genuinely interdisciplinary work.
- ACM **acmsmall** single-column template; the current page budget (2026: 20 pages of technical
  content including tables/figures, plus unlimited references); no margin/font/spacing edits.
- Double-anonymous sweep of the PDF, plots, self-citations, and any released code/data — except in
  the Operational Systems Track, which may name the deploying organization / system.
- A reproducibility/artifact plan aligned to ACM badging (Available / Functional / Reusable /
  Reproduced); confirm whether a formal artifact track runs this cycle.

## Do not infer

- Do not infer the deadline dates: SIGMETRICS runs three rolling deadlines a year and the exact
  dates move every cycle. Read them from the current call and the per-deadline HotCRP site.
- Do not assume the one-shot-revision target deadline, the 12-month resubmission bar, the page
  budget, the track list, or the anonymity policy of a given track carry over between editions.
- Do not assume an artifact-evaluation track exists in a given cycle, or its badge set — confirm.
- If pages disagree, treat the newest call for papers, the HotCRP deadline page, or a direct chair
  announcement as controlling, and record the conflict.
