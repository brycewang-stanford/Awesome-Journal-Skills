# Worked Example: Reframing an Introduction for Financial Management

This is a fictional example. It does not describe a real paper.

## Before

> This paper studies an important topic using a large dataset and several robustness checks. We contribute to the literature by adding new evidence and showing heterogeneous effects.

## Diagnosis

- The first sentence does not name the FM audience or the journal's corporate finance, investments, market institutions, and applied financial decision-making scope.
- The contribution could fit several siblings: Journal of Corporate Finance, Journal of Banking and Finance, JFQA, and Review of Financial Studies.
- The mechanism words that matter here are absent: corporate policy, capital structure, payout and investment, governance channel.

## After

> This paper asks how **corporate policy** changes the decision margin that FM readers care about. The design ties the estimate to **capital structure**, separates the main mechanism from **payout and investment**, and reports uncertainty in a way that makes the policy or theory implication portable beyond the study setting.
