## Verified facts used across the skills

- CHI = the ACM CHI Conference on Human Factors in Computing Systems, SIGCHI's
  flagship venue; one Papers cycle per year; submission and review run on PCS
  (Precision Conference Solutions), not OpenReview or CMT; publication runs through
  ACM TAPS into the ACM Digital Library.
- CHI 2026: April 13–17, 2026, Barcelona, Spain. CHI 2027: May 10–14, 2027,
  Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, USA.
- CHI 2027 Papers: due 2026-09-10 with videos and supplementary material on the same
  deadline; single-column ACM template for review; words-not-pages length regime
  (5,000–8,000 encouraged; <5,000 short papers; >12,000 desk-reject exposure).
- Review: 1AC + 2AC + two externals; A/ARR/RR/RRX/X; two rounds with a ~5-week
  tracked-changes revision + response letter; DR and rubric-based ADR screening
  before full review; PC meeting decides.
- Camera-ready: TAPS source upload, then a PCS publication-ready package; mandatory
  author-supplied alt text with external PDF tagging; optional 30-second video
  preview; ACM open access as of 2026-01-01.
