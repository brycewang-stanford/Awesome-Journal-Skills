## Aims & scope

- **Aims-and-scope text (confirmed via search rendering of the ScienceDirect about page):**
  *"Agricultural Systems is an international journal that deals with interactions — among the components
  of agricultural systems, among hierarchical levels of agricultural systems, between agricultural and
  other land use systems, and between agricultural systems and their natural, social and economic
  environments."* Manuscripts generally include **substantive natural-science content** (especially
  **farm- or landscape-level biology or ecology**, sometimes combined with social sciences) **and**
  substantive analysis/discussion of the **interactions** within or among agricultural-system
  components and other systems. **Preference is given to manuscripts that address whole-farm and
  landscape-level issues** via the **integration of conceptual, empirical and dynamic modelling
  approaches.** Founded **1976**; published monthly.

## Peer-review & editorial policy

- **Peer-review model (confirmed via search rendering of the journal's ScienceDirect page):** the
  journal operates **single anonymized** peer review; suitable submissions are sent to a **minimum of
  two reviewers** for independent expert assessment; the **decision to accept or reject is taken by the
  journal's editors**. **Perspective** articles undergo a **rapid review** process. 待核实 on exact
  reviewer counts and timelines on the live page.

## Research data, code & model availability

- **https://www.elsevier.com/about/policies-and-standards/research-data** — Elsevier Research Data
  policy. For Agricultural Systems, the journal applies Elsevier's **"Option C" research-data
  instructions**: authors are **encouraged/required to deposit research data in a repository**, and to
  **cite and link the dataset** in the article; **if data cannot be shared, a statement must explain
  why**. "Research data" explicitly includes **software, code, models, algorithms, protocols, and
  methods**. 待核实 on whether the journal mandates vs. encourages deposit and on any data-availability
  statement template.
- **https://www.elsevier.com/researcher/author/tools-and-resources/research-data/data-guidelines** —
  Elsevier research-data guidelines (repository options, Data in Brief, Mendeley Data, linking).
