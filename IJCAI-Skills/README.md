# IJCAI Skills

Twelve agent skills for IJCAI and IJCAI-ECAI main-track papers. The pack focuses on
Chairing Tool submission readiness, abstract and author-information deadlines, two-phase
review, concise author responses, reproducibility evidence, supplementary files,
resubmission information, LLM-use policy, and camera-ready publication.

Official basis checked on 2026-06-01: IJCAI-ECAI 2026 Main Track CFP, submissions FAQ,
Author's Response FAQ, reproducibility guidelines, Author Kit, important dates, peer-review
principles, and IJCAI conflict-of-interest policy. See `resources/official-source-map.md`
for the exact source map.

## Skills

- `ijcai-submission` - audit Chairing Tool, author information, ORCID, page limits,
  anonymity, supplementary material, resubmission information, author limits, dual
  submission, and LLM-use policy.
- `ijcai-author-response` - draft one-page IJCAI author responses for pressing questions,
  factual errors, and unethical-review escalations without new results.
- `ijcai-camera-ready` - prepare accepted-paper formatting, de-anonymization, metadata,
  copyright, registration, in-person presentation, and artifact-release work.
- `ijcai-artifact-evaluation` - package code/data/proofs as reproducibility evidence when
  there is no separate official artifact badge.
- `ijcai-reproducibility` - strengthen convincing or credible evidence for theory,
  algorithms, datasets, and computational experiments.
- `ijcai-supplementary` - organize Technical Appendix, ZIP files, and Resubmission File
  under deadline, format, size, and anonymity constraints.
- `ijcai-review-process` - reason about Phase 1 summary reject, full review, PC/SPC/AC/SAC
  roles, ethics, confidentiality, and conflicts.
- `ijcai-writing-style` - revise for broad AI significance, 7-page-body compression,
  self-contained exposition, ethics, reproducibility, and double-blind style.
- `ijcai-related-work` - position against archival work, preprints, workshops, prior
  rejected versions, and shortened prior versions.
- `ijcai-experiments` - audit baselines, ablations, statistics, hyperparameters, compute,
  datasets, ethics, and reproducibility.
- `ijcai-workflow` - manage the calendar from topic selection through abstract registration,
  author information, full upload, response, notification, camera-ready, and presentation.
- `ijcai-topic-selection` - decide whether a project is IJCAI-shaped, special-track shaped,
  or better routed to another AI venue.

## Maintenance notes

- Reopen current-cycle official sources before deadline-sensitive advice.
- Treat IJCAI-ECAI 2026 facts as historical anchors, not permanent rules.
- Track rules, page limits, submission systems, PPI fees, LLM policy, response format,
  supplementary limits, and camera-ready obligations can change by cycle and track.
