Access date: 2026-07-08

This map records the official sources behind every ICRA-specific fact in this pack.
ICRA policy is re-set for each edition by that year's organizing committee under the
IEEE Robotics and Automation Society (RAS); reopen the current year-site
(`<year>.ieee-icra.org`), its call-for-papers and final-submission pages, and the
PaperPlaza portal before giving submission-ready advice.

## Verified 2026-cycle facts used in the skills

- ICRA 2026 was held June 1-5, 2026 at Messe Wien, Vienna, Austria — the RAS
  flagship, theme "Robots for all," 8,000+ attendees.
- The live target as of 2026-07-08 is **ICRA 2027, May 24-28, 2027, COEX, Seoul,
  Republic of Korea**, whose paper deadline is not yet posted; the recent pattern
  (2023-2026 cycles) is a mid-September deadline of the preceding year.
- Submission runs on PaperPlaza (`ras.papercept.net`), deadline 23:59 Pacific time
  (not AoE) in the 2026 cycle.
- 2026 page geometry: 6 content pages + up to 2 reference-only pages at submission;
  8 pages total including references at camera-ready.
- ICRA 2026 review was double-anonymous — a change from single-blind ICRA 2025.
- Video attachments: ≤ 180 s, ≤ 20 MB, mpeg/mp4/mpg, accepted only during two
  posted windows (the second closing after the paper deadline).
- The RA-L pathway is real and current: rolling journal submission, 6 pages
  including references (+2 at USD 175/page), ~3-month first decision, and
  conference presentation via transfer within 270 days of acceptance.
- ICRA proceedings are published in IEEE Xplore; publication requires the IEEE
  electronic copyright form.
- Like every conference in this collection, ICRA has no standing editor-in-chief;
  review is run per-edition through the RAS Conference Editorial Board structure
  (Editors and Associate Editors) inside PaperPlaza, and organizing committees
  rotate annually.
