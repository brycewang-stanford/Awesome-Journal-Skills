## Verified facts
- **Editorial leadership (verified 2026-06-23):** *Annals of Mathematics* is **run by an editorial board, not a single famous Editor-in-Chief.** The board comprises managing/handling editors and associate editors. Editors include (per the official board page) Bhargav Bhatt and Helmut Hofer (both IAS / Princeton University), alongside Princeton faculty editors (e.g. Sergiu Klainerman, Fernando C. Marques, Nicholas M. Katz, Assaf Naor, Zoltán Szabó) and a roster of associate editors. **Membership rotates — confirm the current editors on the board page before relying on any name.** (#2)
- **Publisher / owner:** Jointly edited and published by the Department of Mathematics, **Princeton University**, and the **Institute for Advanced Study (IAS)** (arrangement since 1933); Princeton is the primary publisher. Distributed in partnership with the Mathematical Sciences Publishers (MSP). (#1, #5)
- **APC / fees:** **No article processing charges (APCs) and no page charges to authors.** Revenue is subscription-based. (#3, #4)
- **ISSN:** 0003-486X (print) / 1939-8980 (online). (#5)
- **Submission portal / review model:** Submit per the official submission guidelines (electronic submission to the journal). Rigorous, often lengthy single-blind expert peer review; extremely selective top-tier pure-mathematics venue. (#3)
