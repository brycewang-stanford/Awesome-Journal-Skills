# External Tools - EACL

Access date: 2026-07-09

Use these surfaces after reopening the current EACL edition's call for papers
and the live ARR pages. EACL routes through ACL Rolling Review, and both the ARR
calendar and the EACL edition move between years — nothing below is a standing
schedule.

## Official workflow surfaces

- EACL 2027 site and CFP: https://2027.eacl.org/ and https://2027.eacl.org/calls/papers/
- EACL chapter home: https://eacl.org/
- ACL Rolling Review CFP: http://aclrollingreview.org/cfp
- ARR cycle dates: https://aclrollingreview.org/dates
- Responsible NLP checklist: https://aclrollingreview.org/responsibleNLPresearch
- OpenReview (ARR is where submission and commitment happen): https://openreview.net/
- ACL Anthology (proceedings, EACL and Findings volumes): https://aclanthology.org/
- ACL Member Portal (calls and announcements): https://www.aclweb.org/portal/
- ACL Anthology copyright/licensing: https://aclanthology.org/faq/copyright/

## Author-side checks by stage

- **Before the ARR cycle deadline:** OpenReview profile, conflicts, author list,
  title, abstract, ACL template compliance, content-page count, Limitations
  section present, Responsible NLP checklist completed and consistent with the
  PDF, anonymization of paper and supplement, preprint declaration, resubmission
  link if applicable.
- **During review:** track the assigned reviews and the action editor's steer;
  prepare a focused rebuttal; do not add unsupported new claims or links that
  break anonymity.
- **At commitment:** confirm EACL is accepting commitments from this cycle,
  attach the meta-review package, and remember the author list is frozen (order
  may change, membership may not).
- **After acceptance:** de-anonymize, spend the extra content page, disclose AI
  assistance per the checklist, finalize ACL Anthology metadata (title casing,
  author affiliations, license), register at least one author, and plan the
  public artifact release.

## Do not infer

- Do not carry EACL 2027's dates, single-cycle constraint, or commitment window
  to another edition — EACL cadence changes.
- Do not assume a fallback ARR cycle exists for a given EACL edition; confirm the
  eligible cycle(s) on that edition's CFP.
- Do not assume page limits, checklist wording, Findings mechanics, or
  registration rules without reopening the current pages.
- If official pages disagree, the newest EACL CFP, ARR announcement, or direct
  chair communication is controlling; record the conflict.
