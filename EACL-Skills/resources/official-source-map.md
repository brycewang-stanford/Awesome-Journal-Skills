# Official Source Map - EACL

Access date: 2026-07-09

This map records the official sources used for EACL-specific facts in this pack.
EACL runs through ACL Rolling Review and its calendar changes every edition;
reopen the current EACL call for papers, the ARR CFP and dates pages, the
OpenReview cycle group, and the ACL Anthology before giving submission-ready
advice.

## Access-method note (gateway behavior on 2026-07-09)

Direct machine fetches of `2027.eacl.org`, `aclrollingreview.org`, and
`dblp.org` returned **HTTP 403** through the build-time network gateway. Where a
page could not be fetched directly, its contents were read through
**search-engine renderings of the exact official URL** and then **cross-checked**
against a second independent surface — the **ACL Anthology** (`aclanthology.org`,
fetchable), the **ACL Member Portal** (`aclweb.org/portal`), and the official
**@eaclmeeting** announcements. Facts that could be triangulated across at least
two such surfaces are recorded as verified for 2026-07-09; anything that could
not is marked **待核实** below. This mirrors the access-method caveat used by the
UAI and NAACL packs in this collection.

## Verified 2027 facts used in skills

- EACL 2027 is the **20th Conference of the European Chapter of the ACL**,
  scheduled for **March 9-14, 2027 in Athens, Greece**, held as a hybrid event.
- Reviewing is done through **ACL Rolling Review** on OpenReview; the conference
  makes acceptance decisions from ARR reviews and meta-reviews at commitment.
- The **only viable ARR cycle for EACL 2027 is the August 3, 2026 cycle**. The
  organizers publicly corrected an earlier date and stressed that, because EACL
  2027 is earlier than usual, this is the sole cycle — there is no fallback.
- **EACL commitment deadline: October 11, 2026.** Notification: November 12,
  2026. Camera-ready: November 26, 2026. (All ARR/commitment deadlines run
  anywhere-on-Earth; confirm the exact time zone on the live pages.)
- Long papers: up to **8 content pages**; short papers: up to **4**; one extra
  content page at camera-ready (9 / 5). References and appendices are unlimited;
  a dedicated **Limitations** section is mandatory and does not count toward the
  limit.
- The **Responsible NLP checklist** is filed at ARR submission.
- Papers not selected for the main conference are **automatically considered for
  Findings of ACL: EACL**, with simultaneous notification.
- During commitment, the **author list may not change** (no additions or
  deletions); author order may be changed.

## Leadership (verified 2026-07-09)

- EACL is a conference of the ACL's European Chapter: it has **no standing
  editor-in-chief**. The rotating analogue is the per-edition General Chair and
  Program Chairs, appointed for that edition.
- EACL 2027 General Chair: **Isabelle Augenstein** (University of Copenhagen).
  Program Chairs: **Malvina Nissim** (University of Groningen), **Roi Reichart**
  (Technion - Israel Institute of Technology), and **Sara Tonelli** (Fondazione
  Bruno Kessler). These appointments rotate every edition — re-check the current
  organization page before relying on a name.
- There is **no article-processing charge**. EACL funds the meeting through
  registration; at least one author must register and present. Accepted papers
  are open access in the ACL Anthology at no author fee.

## Prior-edition anchors (historical, not standing rules)

- **EACL 2026** was the **19th Conference**, held in **Rabat, Morocco,
  March 24-29, 2026**; program chairs Vera Demberg, Kentaro Inui, and Lluís
  Màrquez. Proceedings are in the ACL Anthology under `2026.eacl-long`,
  `2026.eacl-short`, `2026.findings-eacl`, and `2026.eacl-industry`. It ran and
  concluded before this pack's access date.
- Earlier editions used in exemplars: EACL 2024 (18th, St. Julian's, Malta),
  EACL 2021 (16th, online), EACL 2017 (15th, Valencia).
