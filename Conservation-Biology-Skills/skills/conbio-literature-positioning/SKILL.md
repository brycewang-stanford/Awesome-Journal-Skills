# Literature Positioning (conbio-literature-positioning)

*Conservation Biology* rewards **novelty and transferability**, and reviewers screen hard for whether a
paper actually advances the field. Positioning is therefore not throat-clearing — it places the work
where a **conservation audience spanning ecology, the social sciences, and practice** can see the gap
and the advance.

## When to trigger

- Drafting or revising the introduction and the "contribution" paragraph
- A reviewer said you "missed key work" or "don't engage the relevant literature"
- Your taxon/system literature is solid but the paper doesn't connect to broader conservation science
- You need to distinguish your contribution from the closest prior papers

## How Conservation Biology wants the literature engaged

1. **Engage the conservation debate, not a citation pile.** Identify the live problem, disagreement, or
   evidence gap your paper speaks to, and cite the works that *define* it — including foundational
   conservation-biology theory where relevant.
2. **Two audiences at once.** Satisfy system specialists (you know the frontier) *and* the general
   conservation reader (why it matters for biodiversity). An ecologist and a practitioner should both
   see the stakes.
3. **Name the gap precisely.** Not "little is known" — say what is contested, unmeasured, untested, or
   not yet translated into practice, and why resolving it matters for conservation.
4. **Position as an advance.** "Prior work assumes/shows X; we show it fails / is conditional on C /
   generalizes / translates into decision D."
5. **Pre-empt the obvious objection.** Reviewers are expert; name the strongest alternative explanation
   and say how your design adjudicates it (hand off to `conbio-study-design`).

## Cross-disciplinary engagement (a distinctive demand)

| If your paper is… | also engage… |
|-------------------|--------------|
| an ecological field study | the conservation-decision or management literature it informs |
| a method/SDM/modeling advance | the substantive conservation problems that will use it |
| a human-dimensions / social study | the ecological stakes and the policy literature |
| a policy / practice analysis | the underlying evidence base on what works |

## Anti-patterns

- A "literature dump" with no organizing conservation problem
- Engaging only your taxon/system and ignoring conservation science broadly
- Strawmanning prior work, or hiding the closest competitor paper
- Self-citation that breaks anonymity (the journal is double-blind — see `conbio-submission`)
- Claiming "first to study" when the contribution is incremental


## Positioning pass for Conservation Biology

Treat this skill as an executable review pass, not a prose hint. First lock the species/system threat, conservation decision, and uncertainty relevant to action; then judge whether the current manuscript answers the venue's real reader: conservation-science reviewers who ask whether evidence changes biodiversity, management, or policy action.

- **Do the pass:** Build a three-column map: incumbent conversation, unresolved tension, and this manuscript's delta; include one sibling-venue omission that would make a referee doubt the fit.
- **Return a ledger:** give `claim / evidence / risk / manuscript location` rows, so the next agent can edit rather than rediscover the issue.
- **Sibling guard:** compare against Biological Conservation for applied conservation breadth, Global Change Biology for climate/ecosystem process, Ecology Letters for theory-forward ecology; if a sibling owns the contribution, recommend re-routing before polishing format.
- **Submission-ready gate:** do not give final advice until the pack's `resources/official-source-map.md` has been checked for upload-week rules and the manuscript has one concrete fix for the largest venue-specific risk.

## Output format

```
【Problem】the live conservation gap / disagreement
【Key works】the 3-6 that define it (incl. cross-disciplinary)
【Gap】what is contested / unmeasured / untranslated
【Advance】how this paper moves the field
【Strongest rival】and how the design will adjudicate it
【Next】conbio-study-design
```
