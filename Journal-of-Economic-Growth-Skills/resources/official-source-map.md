# JEG Official Source Map

Primary and authoritative sources behind every Journal of Economic Growth (JEG)
fact injected into this skill pack. **Access date for all entries: 2026-06-20.**
Re-verify volatile items (editors, APC figures, policy wording, portal URL) before
relying on them — Springer journal pages change and the APC is revised periodically.
Items that could not be confirmed on an official page are marked **待核实** (to be verified).

## Official journal pages (Springer Nature / link.springer.com)

- **https://link.springer.com/journal/10887/submission-guidelines** — Submission Guidelines. Verifies:
  - Submission via the Springer Nature "Submit manuscript" portal (`submission.nature.com/new-submission/10887/3`).
  - Abstract must be **150 to 250 words**.
  - **LaTeX preferred** (Springer Nature LaTeX template recommended); **Word (.docx) accepted**.
  - References are **author-year in-text** citations with an **APA 7th edition** reference list.
  - Title page must carry: title, author names and affiliations, corresponding-author email, **ORCID** (16-digit, if available), abstract, **4-6 keywords**, an **appropriate number of JEL classification codes**, and a **Statements/Declarations** section.
  - **No manuscript word/length limit** is specified.
  - **Editable source files** (LaTeX source or .docx) must be provided at every submission and revision.
  - A **Data Availability Statement** is required for all original research articles.
  - **Author Contribution** and **Competing Interest** information must be entered via the submission interface at submission time.
  - **Large Language Models (LLMs), such as ChatGPT, "do not currently satisfy our authorship criteria"**; LLM use must be documented (e.g., in the Methods section). (AI-assisted copy editing does not require declaration.)
- **https://link.springer.com/journal/10887/how-to-publish-with-us** — How to publish with us. Verifies:
  - **Hybrid journal**; subscription publishing is **free to authors**; **no submission/handling fee** documented.
  - Optional open access (Open Choice) **APC: GBP 3,090.00 / USD 4,590.00 / EUR 3,590.00** (re-confirmed directly on this page 2026-06-20; subject to Springer revision).
- **https://link.springer.com/journal/10887/editorial-board** — Editorial Board. Verifies:
  - **Editor-in-Chief: Oded Galor (PhD)**, Dept. of Economics, Brown University, Providence, United States; and Hebrew University of Jerusalem, Jerusalem, Israel. *(Directly read on the official editorial-board page, 2026-06-20.)*
  - Large board of **Associate Editors** (36 listed as of access date, e.g., Daron Acemoglu (MIT), Philippe Aghion (Collège de France), Paul Krugman (Princeton); Alberto Alesina listed as deceased).
- **https://link.springer.com/journal/10887/aims-and-scope** — Aims and Scope. Verifies:
  - Principal outlet for **theoretical and empirical research in economic growth and dynamic macroeconomics**: neoclassical/endogenous growth, human capital, fertility, trade, development, financial development, migration, technological change, political economy of growth, OLG models.
- **https://www.springernature.com/gp/authors/research-data-policy** — Springer Nature research data policy. Verifies:
  - The **Data Availability Statement** requirement the journal follows (links/citations to publicly archived datasets, or an explanation and conditions for restricted data).
- **https://link.springer.com/journal/10887** — Journal home page. Verifies *(directly read 2026-06-20)*:
  - Publisher: **Springer Science+Business Media, LLC, part of Springer Nature**.
  - **ISSN 1381-4338** (print), **1573-7020** (electronic) — confirmed on the official home page (no longer Wikipedia-only).
  - Facts & figures: **submission to first decision (median) 13 days**; Journal Impact Factor 2.9 (2024), 5-year IF 3.8 (2024). *(Metrics are revised annually — re-confirm before quoting.)*

## Representative JEG-scope topics (for orientation only — NOT a citation list)

The journal's scope spans unified growth theory, endogenous and neoclassical growth,
human-capital and fertility dynamics, trade-and-growth, financial development and growth,
migration, technological change, and the political economy of growth (often via OLG models).
Confirm any specific article attribution against the journal's own contents before citing —
**do not attribute named papers to JEG without verification (待核实).**
