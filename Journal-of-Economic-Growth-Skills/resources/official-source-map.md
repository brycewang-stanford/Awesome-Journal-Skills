## Verified facts encoded in the pack

- **Publisher / identity:** Springer Science+Business Media, LLC, part of Springer Nature; officially cited as *J Econ Growth*; print ISSN 1381-4338 and electronic ISSN 1573-7020. (#1)
- **Editor-in-Chief:** Oded Galor, PhD. (#1, #4)
- **Scope:** economic growth and comparative economic development across the contemporary period and long-run history; income distribution, demographic transition, human capital formation, technological change, structural transformation, and deeply rooted geographic/cultural/institutional/human characteristics. (#1, #5)
- **Transfer boundary:** population-economics submissions judged not suitable for JEG may be transferred, with referee reports, for further consideration in *Journal of Population Economics*. (#1)
- **Submission system:** Springer Nature "Submit manuscript" route linked from the journal pages. (#1, #2)
- **Abstract:** 150-250 words. (#2)
- **File formats:** LaTeX preferred with Springer Nature template recommended; Word (.docx) accepted; editable source files required for submission and revision. (#2)
- **Title-page metadata:** title, author names/affiliations, corresponding-author email, ORCID if available, abstract, 4-6 keywords, appropriate JEL codes, and Statements/Declarations. (#2)
- **References:** author-year in-text citations and APA 7th edition reference list. (#2)
- **Data availability:** all original research articles must include a Data Availability Statement explaining access to supporting data, including links/citations to public archives or access conditions for restricted data. (#2, #6)
- **Authorship / AI:** LLMs do not satisfy Springer Nature authorship criteria; LLM use must be documented. AI-assisted copy editing does not require declaration under the wording read on 2026-06-21. (#2)
- **Open access:** hybrid journal; APC re-verified 2026-06-22 as GBP 2,890 / USD 4,290 / EUR 3,390 (down from the GBP 3,090 / USD 4,590 / EUR 3,590 figure recorded 2026-06-21); subscription route carries no APC. Confirmed via two Springer-Nature-sourced web indexes on 2026-06-22; the canonical "How to publish with us" page (#3) is login-gated to command-line fetch, so re-confirm the live amount at acceptance. (#3)
- **Metrics visible on home page:** 2024 Journal Impact Factor 2.9, 5-year IF 3.8, median submission to first decision 13 days, and 2025 downloads 320.1k. These are metrics, not stable policy rules. (#1)
