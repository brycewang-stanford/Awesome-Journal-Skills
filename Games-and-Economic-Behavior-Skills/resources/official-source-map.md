## Official journal & guide-for-authors pages

- **https://www.sciencedirect.com/journal/games-and-economic-behavior** — GEB homepage on
  ScienceDirect. Verifies: publisher = Elsevier; the gold open-access Article Publishing Charge
  (APC) of **USD 2,860 excluding tax** for accepted articles whose authors choose OA. Accessed 2026-06-01.
- **https://www.sciencedirect.com/journal/games-and-economic-behavior/publish/guide-for-authors**
  — GEB Guide for Authors. Verifies: chief editor assigns each paper to one of **seven Editors**
  with final decision authority; editors consult anonymous advisory editors and referees;
  **about 15% of submitted papers are published** and **about one-third are desk-rejected**;
  the cover letter must explain how the submission differs from any **conference version**; use of
  generative AI must be declared in a section titled **"Declaration of generative AI and
  AI-assisted technologies in the manuscript preparation process,"** placed before the reference
  list (basic grammar/spelling tools exempt); **abstract must not exceed 250 words**; no strict
  reference-formatting requirement at submission as long as the style is consistent; published
  citation style is **elsarticle-num**. Accessed 2026-06-01.
  **⚠ 待核实 (to verify):** this page repeatedly returned HTTP 403 to direct fetch; the facts above
  were obtained via search-engine snippets of this page, not a direct load. A human should confirm
  them directly on the live page.

## Society & masthead

- **https://gametheorysociety.org/games-and-economic-behavior/** — Game Theory Society official
  page for GEB. Verifies: publisher Elsevier; GEB is **affiliated with the Game Theory Society**;
  editor-in-chief **Hervé Moulin** (as of Nov 2025); **seven Editors** handling **~800 annual
  submissions** plus **~45 Advisory Editors**; each paper assigned to a **publicly known "Editor
  in Charge,"** who assigns it to an **anonymous Advisory Editor** who selects referees and
  recommends a decision. Accessed 2026-06-01.
  **⚠ 待核实:** the seven named Editors and ~45 Advisory Editors / ~800 annual submissions came from
  the GTS page summary and may have changed; verify the current editorial board before relying on
  individual names. The seven Editors reported (Bikhchandani, Bohren, Immorlica, Moulin, Serrano,
  Vespa, Vieille) are **待核实** and not used as load-bearing names in the skills.

- **https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Games_and_Economic_Behavior** — Verifies: publisher Elsevier;
  one of two official journals of the Game Theory Society; **founded 1989 by Ehud Kalai** (founding
  editor); current editor-in-chief **Hervé Moulin since 1 January 2021**; bimonthly; ISSN 0899-8256;
  scope spans game theory, economics, political science, biology, computer science, mathematics,
  psychology. Accessed 2026-06-01.
  **⚠ 待核实:** Moulin confirmed as chief editor since 2021 and still listed as of late 2025; not
  independently reconfirmed for mid-2026 — verify the masthead at submission time.

## Submission system & formatting

- **https://www.elsevier.support/publishing/answer/how-to-submit-a-latex-file-in-editorial-manager**
  — Verifies: submission via Elsevier **Editorial Manager**; editable **Word or LaTeX** files
  required; LaTeX submissions use the **elsarticle** template / `.sty` file; correspondence by email.
  Accessed 2026-06-01.

## Data / replication policy

- **https://github.com/atrisovic/econ-data-policy/blob/master/README.md** — Independent survey of
  economics-journal data policies. Verifies: GEB's data policy is **"encouraged but not mandatory"**
  — Elsevier recommends data sharing but there is **no requirement** for this journal; GEB encourages
  and enables authors to share data via repositories such as **Mendeley Data**, data statements, or a
  Data Brief. Accessed 2026-06-01.
  Note: this is a secondary aggregator; the underlying stance is Elsevier's "encourages and enables"
  research-data policy (Option C). Cross-check the journal's own Guide for Authors.
