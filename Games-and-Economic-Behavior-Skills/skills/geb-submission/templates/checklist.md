# GEB Submission Checklist

- Abstract is 250 words or fewer.
- Manuscript uses a consistent style; LaTeX/elsarticle or Word source is ready.
- Main contribution to game theory is clear in the first page and cover letter.
- Cover letter explains how the manuscript differs from any conference version, or states that none exists.
- Theorems, assumptions, game forms, equilibrium concepts, and proofs are complete.
- Experiments or simulations include design, sample, code/data plan, and robustness.
- Generative-AI declaration is included before the reference list if applicable.
- Conflict, funding, and not-under-review declarations are ready.
- Data/code sharing plan is prepared, recognizing that GEB encourages but does not mandate sharing.
- Volatile facts such as APC, editor roster, and guide wording are rechecked on the live official pages.
