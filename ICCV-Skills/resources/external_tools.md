# External Tools - ICCV

Access date: 2026-07-08

Live official surfaces for ICCV work, plus the author-side verification passes this
pack expects an agent to run. ICCV is biennial and its process is re-set per edition:
nothing below substitutes for reopening the current cycle's own pages. Note that in
the environment used to build this pack, `iccv.thecvf.com` was not directly fetchable
(HTTP 403) — see the access-method note in
[`official-source-map.md`](official-source-map.md).

## Author-side verification passes

1. **Cycle pass** — confirm from the live Dates page: registration deadline, the
   paper/supplement deadline and its **timezone** (2025 used HST, not AoE), rebuttal
   window, decision date. Never infer 2027 values from 2025.
2. **Format pass** — rebuild from the current author kit; confirm the 8-page
   inclusive cap and references-only overflow still hold; no style modifications.
3. **People pass** — every coauthor: complete OpenReview profile, conflict list,
   registered-submission count vs the per-author cap, and reviewer availability for
   the post-deadline months (every author reviews unless exempt).
4. **Anonymity pass** — PDF metadata, acknowledgements, grant IDs, repo links (the
   2025 rule: do not cite your public codebase), supplement paths, video container
   tags, image EXIF.
5. **Attribution pass** — spot-check .bib entries against dblp and CVF open access;
   upgrade stale arXiv citations to their published venues; verify every machine-
   suggested reference exists.

## Do not infer

- Do not project the 2025 deadline chain, page caps, unified supplement deadline,
  reviewer-duty wording, or Findings workshop onto 2027 — all 待核实 until the Hong
  Kong cycle publishes its pages.
- Do not treat community trackers' tier counts (orals, Highlights) as official
  statistics; label them reported.
- If official pages conflict, the newest chairs' communication controls; record the
  conflict in the source map with its access date.
