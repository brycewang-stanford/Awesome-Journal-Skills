# Worked Example: Reframing an Introduction for Journal of Financial Markets

This is a fictional example. It does not describe a real paper.

## Before

> This paper studies an important topic using a large dataset and several robustness checks. We contribute to the literature by adding new evidence and showing heterogeneous effects.

## Diagnosis

- The first sentence does not name the JFM audience or the journal's market microstructure, asset pricing, liquidity, trading, and financial-market design scope.
- The contribution could fit several siblings: Journal of Finance, Journal of Financial Economics, Review of Financial Studies, and Management Science.
- The mechanism words that matter here are absent: microstructure mechanism, liquidity measurement, trading venue design, price discovery.

## After

> This paper asks how **microstructure mechanism** changes the decision margin that JFM readers care about. The design ties the estimate to **liquidity measurement**, separates the main mechanism from **trading venue design**, and reports uncertainty in a way that makes the policy or theory implication portable beyond the study setting.
