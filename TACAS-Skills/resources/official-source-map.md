# Official Source Map - TACAS (ETAPS)

Access date: 2026-07-09

This map records the official and cross-check sources for TACAS-specific facts in this pack.
TACAS is the **International Conference on Tools and Algorithms for the Construction and Analysis
of Systems**, one of the main conferences of **ETAPS** (the European Joint Conferences on Theory
and Practice of Software). Its four paper categories, per-category anonymity, mandatory tool-paper
artifact evaluation, ETAPS Artifact Badges, LNCS gold-open-access proceedings, and hosting of
SV-COMP are decided per edition through the ETAPS joint call and the TACAS call. Reopen the
current-cycle ETAPS joint CfP and the per-conference TACAS page before giving submission-ready
advice.

## Verified 2026/2027 facts used in skills

- **TACAS 2026 is the 32nd** International Conference on Tools and Algorithms for the Construction
  and Analysis of Systems, held as part of **ETAPS 2026 (29th)** in **Turin, Italy** (University of
  Turin). ETAPS 2026 main conferences run **13-16 April 2026**; satellite events **11-12 April**.
  As of 2026-07-09 this edition has already taken place, so the live *next* submission target is
  **TACAS 2027**.
- **Four paper categories:** **regular research papers**, **case-study papers**, **regular tool
  papers**, and **tool-demonstration papers** (source 1/2).
- **Page limits (llncs.cls, excluding bibliography and appendices):** research, case-study, and
  regular tool papers **≤16 pages**; tool-demonstration papers **≤6 pages**. Additional material
  may go in a clearly marked appendix at the end and/or on a supplementary website (source 1).
- **Per-category blind model:** **regular research papers are reviewed double-blind** (names and
  institutions omitted, prior work cited in the third person, no identifying acknowledgements);
  **case-study, regular tool, and tool-demonstration papers are reviewed single-blind** (source 1).
- **Template and portal:** Springer **LNCS `llncs.cls`**; submission through the **EasyChair**
  author interface of the respective TACAS edition (source 1/2).
- **Artifact evaluation is tightly integrated and, for tools, mandatory.** Regular tool and
  tool-demonstration papers **must** submit an artifact shortly after the paper (TACAS 2026:
  paper 16 Oct 2025, mandatory artifact 30 Oct 2025); this artifact is evaluated **in parallel with
  the PC** and its outcome **feeds the acceptance decision**. For research and case-study papers,
  artifact evaluation is **voluntary and post-acceptance** (TACAS 2026 voluntary artifact deadline
  8 Jan 2026) (source 1/6).
- **ETAPS Artifact Badges:** the Artifact Evaluation Committee (AEC) assigns, per the ETAPS Artifact
  Badge guidelines, some subset of **Available / Functional / Reusable**, added to the paper's title
  page (source 6).
- **Proceedings:** Springer **LNCS** (ARCoSS subline), **gold open access under CC-BY since 2018**;
  copyright remains with the authors. TACAS 2026 appeared as **LNCS 16505 + 16506** (Proceedings
  Part I + Part II) (source 4/5/8).
- **TACAS 2026 outcome (reported):** the proceedings comprise **52 full papers, 4 short papers, and
  16 SV-COMP papers** selected from **177 submissions** (source 4 rendering; 待核实 on the exact
  split).
- **SV-COMP 2026** is the **15th International Competition on Software Verification**, hosted at
  TACAS 2026 and organized by **Dirk Beyer (LMU Munich)** and **Jan Strejček (Masaryk University)**;
  participating verifiers contribute short competition papers to the TACAS proceedings (source 7).
- **ETAPS 2027 is the 30th edition**, **Copenhagen, Denmark**, **10-15 April 2027**; TACAS 2027 is
  the **33rd** TACAS and the live next submission target; the ETAPS 2027 voluntary artifact deadline
  is **11 January 2027** (source 9).

## TACAS 2026 reported timeline (single review round; verify each cycle)

TACAS runs a **single annual review round** on the shared ETAPS schedule (it does not use ESOP's
two-round scheme). Reported dates for the TACAS 2026 cycle, cross-read from the ETAPS 2026 joint
CfP and the TACAS 2026 page:

- **Paper submission:** 16 October 2025 (AoE).
- **Mandatory artifact submission** (regular tool + tool-demonstration): 30 October 2025 (~two
  weeks after the paper).
- **Rebuttal / author response:** 8-10 December 2025.
- **Notification:** 22 December 2025.
- **Voluntary artifact submission** (accepted research + case-study papers): 8 January 2026.

All ETAPS deadlines are advertised as **firm and not extended**. Treat the exact dates as a
one-cycle snapshot; the abstract-vs-full-paper gap and any per-category variation should be
reconfirmed on the live TACAS page.

## No editor-in-chief; open access without author-facing APC

Like every conference in this collection, TACAS has **no standing editor-in-chief**; the rotating
analogue is the per-edition **PC chairs** and the **ETAPS steering committee**. The proceedings are
**gold open access (CC-BY)**; ETAPS funds this centrally so that published papers are free to
download in perpetuity, and the cost model is conference registration — do not describe it as an
author-pays journal APC.
