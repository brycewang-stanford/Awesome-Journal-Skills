# External Tools - TACAS (ETAPS)

Access date: 2026-07-09

Use these surfaces after reopening the current ETAPS cycle. TACAS mechanics — the four paper
categories, per-category anonymity, mandatory tool-paper artifact rounds, ETAPS Artifact Badges,
page limits, and the SV-COMP schedule — are set per edition through the ETAPS joint CfP and the
per-conference TACAS page, and the `etaps.org` / `link.springer.com` / `tacas.info` gateway may
block direct fetches (read via search renderings and cross-check Springer LNCS and dblp).

## Official workflow

- ETAPS series hub: https://etaps.org/ and the About-TACAS page https://etaps.org/about/tacas/
- TACAS 2026 conference page (Turin): https://etaps.org/2026/conferences/tacas/
- ETAPS 2026 Joint Call for Papers: https://etaps.org/2026/cfp/
- ETAPS 2026 home (dates, venue): https://etaps.org/2026/
- TACAS 2027 conference page (Copenhagen, next live target): https://etaps.org/2027/conferences/tacas/
- ETAPS 2027 Joint Call for Papers: https://etaps.org/2027/cfp/
- TACAS series / artifact-evaluation home: https://tacas.info/ (per-year `artifacts-NN.php`)
- ETAPS proceedings + open-access policy: https://etaps.org/about/proceedings/
- SV-COMP (hosted at TACAS): https://sv-comp.sosy-lab.org/2026/
- VerifyThis on-site competition (co-located): https://verifythis.github.io/onsite/
- Submission portal: the **EasyChair** author interface of the current TACAS edition.

## Cross-check surfaces (when the gateway blocks the primary page)

- Springer LNCS book landing pages for each TACAS volume (TACAS 2026 = LNCS 16505/16506,
  Proceedings Part I/II) to confirm the LNCS number, DOI, and gold-open-access status.
- dblp TACAS proceedings series: https://dblp.org/db/conf/tacas/index.html — for verifying a paper
  actually appeared at **TACAS** (not CAV, VMCAI, FMCAD, SPIN, or another verification venue).
- The SV-COMP results archive on Zenodo and the competition site for the participating-tool list.
- The ETAPS awards page for any best-paper / doctoral-dissertation distinctions.

## Author-side checks

- EasyChair profile, co-author records, category selection (research / case-study / regular tool /
  tool-demonstration), and topic keywords for the current TACAS edition.
- LNCS `llncs.cls` compliance and the correct page limit for your category (16 pages research /
  case-study / regular tool; 6 pages tool-demonstration; bibliography and appendix excluded).
- The **right blind mode for your category**: anonymize a **regular research** paper (no names,
  third-person self-citation, no identifying acknowledgements); a tool / case-study paper is
  **single-blind** and names its authors and tool.
- For a **regular tool or tool-demonstration** paper: prepare the **mandatory artifact** for the
  post-paper deadline — it is evaluated with the PC and gates acceptance.
- For a **research or case-study** paper: plan the **voluntary, post-acceptance** artifact if you
  want the Available / Functional / Reusable badges on the title page.
- SV-COMP participation, if applicable: register the verifier, meet the benchmark/rule format, and
  write the short competition contribution.

## Do not infer

- Do not assume TACAS uses a two-round submission scheme: **ESOP** does at ETAPS, but **TACAS runs
  a single annual round**. Confirm on the current TACAS page.
- Do not assume every category is double-blind: only **regular research papers** are; case-study,
  tool, and tool-demonstration papers are **single-blind**.
- Do not assume artifact evaluation is optional for tool papers: it is **mandatory** for regular
  tool and tool-demonstration papers and **feeds acceptance**; for research/case-study it is
  voluntary and post-acceptance.
- Do not carry page limits, badge names, or dates between editions. If pages disagree, treat the
  newest ETAPS joint CfP and the per-conference TACAS page as controlling, and record the conflict.
- Do not confuse TACAS with its verification siblings (CAV, VMCAI, FMCAD, SPIN, ATVA): a familiar
  tool or author is not proof of a TACAS placement — check dblp.
