# AA Pre-Submission Checklist (American Anthropologist)

American Anthropologist is the **four-field AAA flagship** (sociocultural, archaeology, biological,
linguistic, plus reflexive/public anthropology), published by **Wiley** for the **American
Anthropological Association**, submitted through **Research Exchange**, under **anonymous** peer review
and an explicit **ethics of care**. AA charges **no submission or publication fee** (OnlineOpen APC only
if you choose open access). Verify all volatile specifics (caps, ORCID requirement, portal, data policy
wording) on the official AA / Wiley author pages and Research Exchange before submitting.

## 1. Scope & fit (four-field flagship)

- [ ] The contribution matters to anthropology **broadly**, not only the home subfield
- [ ] The right **section** is chosen (Research Article / Essay / Commentary / Interview / Review Essay /
      Vital Topics Forum / World / Public / Multimodal Anthropologies)
- [ ] Distinguished from AAA siblings (not *American Ethnologist*, *Cultural Anthropology*, or
      *Current Anthropology* with its CA✩ comments format)
- [ ] Confirmed against the current AA aims & scope

## 2. Length & abstract

- [ ] Research Article **≤ 8,000 words** (incl. figures, tables, references, notes; excl. transcriptions
      + abstract); shorter sections within their caps (Essay 2,000–3,000; Commentary ≤ 1,500;
      Review Essay ≤ 5,000; Book Review ≤ 1,000)
- [ ] **Abstract = 200 words**, stating question, approach, and contribution
- [ ] Transcriptions clearly delimited if claiming the exclusion

## 3. Anonymization (anonymous review)

- [ ] No author names, affiliations, or acknowledgments in the manuscript body
- [ ] **File name** carries no author/institution information
- [ ] Self-citations neutral ("the author (2019)"), never "our earlier study"
- [ ] Document file properties/metadata scrubbed of author identifiers
- [ ] Separate (non-anonymous) **title page** prepared as a distinct file

## 4. House style & references

- [ ] **Chicago Manual of Style author-date** in-text and reference list (free-format accepted at
      submission, but keep one consistent style)
- [ ] **DOIs** included in references where available
- [ ] Every in-text citation is in the list and vice versa; all references real and accurate
- [ ] **Citational politics** considered — marginalized/regional/Indigenous scholarship represented
- [ ] Inclusive, care-ful language; deficit framing of communities avoided

## 5. Evidence & argument quality

- [ ] Contribution stated by the end of the introduction; argument is portable across subfields
- [ ] Positionality / reflexivity made explicit where appropriate
- [ ] Interpretive claims grounded in shown evidence; alternative readings addressed
- [ ] (Biological/archaeological) effect sizes + uncertainty reported; measures validated;
      ancestry not conflated with social race

## 6. Figures, media & permissions

- [ ] Each exhibit is self-contained (caption, labels, source, date/place) and **argues**, not decorates
- [ ] **Alt text** provided for figures; color encoding legible in grayscale where meaning depends on it
- [ ] **Permissions/credits** secured for archival, museum, and others' images/media
- [ ] People depicted **consented** to that use; identifiers handled per consent
- [ ] Sacred/heritage materials and ancestral remains handled per community authority (some withheld)

## 7. Ethics & accountability (central at AA)

- [ ] **Informed consent** obtained and ongoing; covers publication and media use
- [ ] **IRB/ethics approval** documented where applicable
- [ ] **Anonymization** strategy protects vulnerable/criminalized interlocutors; limits stated
- [ ] **Heritage / NAGPRA / repatriation** obligations met for remains, objects, and biological samples
- [ ] Competing obligations (to people, communities, scholarship, profession) weighed and explained
- [ ] **Ethics statement** included in the submission

## 8. Portal & files (Research Exchange)

- [ ] Submitted via **Research Exchange**; correct article/section type selected
- [ ] **ORCID** ready for the corresponding author
- [ ] Original, unpublished, not under review elsewhere; preprint status checked vs. Wiley policy
- [ ] No submission/publication fee due (OnlineOpen APC only if choosing open access)
- [ ] Files staged: anonymized manuscript + separate title page + figures + ethics statement + any forms
- [ ] Within portal file-size and file-type limits
