## Official journal, submission & author pages

- **https://www.americananthropologist.org/how-to-submit** — *American Anthropologist* editorial site,
  "Submit to the Journal." Verifies: submissions via the **Research Exchange** platform (since **15 April
  2025**; earlier submissions used Manuscript Central); **Research Articles up to 8,000 words** including
  figures, tables, references, and notes, excluding transcriptions and the abstract, and **extendable to
  up to ~10,500 words at the associate editors' discretion** if accepted; **Essays 2,000–3,000**;
  **Commentaries up to 1,500**; **Interviews ~2,000**; **Review Essays up to 5,000** (covering 2–5 recent
  publications); **Book Reviews up to 1,000**; **abstract 200 words**; **anonymous** review (upload the
  manuscript with **no identifying author information in the file name**; separate title page);
  **Chicago Manual of Style author-date**, with **free-format** submissions accepted and **DOIs** in
  references where available; **no submission or publication fee** (OnlineOpen open-access option
  available); editorial **ethics of care**, **land acknowledgments** encouraged where appropriate, and
  **inclusive language / citational practices** that lift marginalized voices.
- **https://anthrosource.onlinelibrary.wiley.com/hub/journal/15481433/about/author-guidelines** —
  Wiley Online Library author guidelines for *American Anthropologist* (publisher = **Wiley**;
  manuscript-preparation, ethics, data-availability, and figure/alt-text requirements). NOTE: the Wiley
  page returned HTTP 403 to automated fetching at access time; facts above are taken from the AA
  editorial site, and Wiley-only specifics (exact ORCID requirement, data-availability wording, figure
  resolution/alt-text rules) are 待核实 on the live page.
- **https://anthrosource.onlinelibrary.wiley.com/journal/15481433** — *American Anthropologist* on Wiley
  Online Library (publisher home, current issues; ISSN 0002-7294 / e-ISSN 1548-1433).

## Owner, publisher, editors, history

- **https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/American_Anthropologist** — Verifies: AA began in **1888** (original
  series), with the **New Series** from **1899**; became the official journal of the **American
  Anthropological Association** after the AAA's founding (1902); **four-field** flagship; published by
  **Wiley**; ISSN 0002-7294.
- **Editor-in-Chief (as of access date): Elizabeth Chin** (ArtCenter College of Design); a Managing
  Editor and a four-field associate-editor board. Web sources indicate **Dána-Ain Davis and Sameena
  Mulla** are slated to become editors-in-chief in **2027**. Editorial correspondence:
  `aa-eic@americananthro.org`. Confirm the current masthead and transition dates on the live editorial
  site. 待核实.
