# ES&T Official Source Map

Primary and authoritative sources behind the *Environmental Science & Technology* (ES&T)–specific
facts injected into this skill pack. Access date for all entries: **2026-06-01**. Re-verify volatile
items (exact per-type word limits and what they include, the figure/table word-equivalent rule, the
submission-system name, blinding model, current Editor-in-Chief, impact metrics, any open-access APC)
on the official page before relying on them — ACS journal pages change.

## Official author guidelines & submission

- **https://researcher-resources.acs.org/publish/author_guidelines?coden=esthag**
  — ACS Researcher Resources, ES&T Author Guidelines (page-verified). Verifies: publisher =
  **American Chemical Society (ACS)**; manuscripts submitted through the **ACS Publishing Center**
  (the modern submission entry; the peer-review/submission environment is **ACS Paragon Plus**);
  manuscript types and word limits — **Research Article 7,000**, **Feature 5,000**,
  **Review (Critical Review) 10,000**, **Perspective 4,000**, **Policy Analysis 7,000**,
  **Viewpoint 1,000 + ≤5 refs**, **Spotlight 800 + ≤5 refs**, **Letter to the Editor 500 +
  250 words of refs**, **Correspondence/Rebuttal 1,000**; **a TOC graphic is required** for Research
  Articles, Features, Reviews, Policy Analysis, and Perspectives (not for Viewpoints/Spotlights/
  Letters); **Supporting Information** is submitted simultaneously as separate file(s), is available
  to reviewers, and is free to readers on publication; **data-availability statement** and public-
  database deposition of materials/data/protocols expected; ethical requirements (COI disclosure,
  **iThenticate/CrossCheck** plagiarism screening, coauthor consent, ARRIVE/STROBE where relevant,
  AI-use disclosure, safety-hazard reporting in Methods); editor screening with a significant
  fraction declined without external review, then typically **three** expert reviewers; authors
  suggest **≥4** non-conflicted reviewers.
- **https://pubs.acs.org/page/esthag/submission/authors.html**
  — Information for Authors (ES&T) on pubs.acs.org. The canonical author page (note: may return
  403 to automated fetchers; open in a browser). Confirms scope, article types, and the TOC-graphic /
  Supporting Information requirements.
- **https://publish.acs.org/publish/author_guidelines?coden=esthag** — ES&T Author Guidelines via
  the ACS Publishing Center / **ACS Paragon Plus** submission entry.
- **https://publish.acs.org/entry.html** — **ACS Paragon Plus** submission and peer-review environment (current entry at publish.acs.org; the legacy `acsparagonplus.acs.org` address now redirects here)
  (the system referenced throughout ACS author guidance).

## Scope, aims & about

- **https://pubs.acs.org/page/esthag/about.html** — About ES&T. Verifies: ES&T "is an impactful
  environmental science and technology research journal that aims to be transformational and
  direction-setting, publishing rigorous and robust" work for a multidisciplinary audience; scope
  spans **environmental chemistry, environmental engineering, fate/transport/transformation in
  natural and engineered systems, treatment and resource recovery, ecotoxicology and environmental
  health, biogeochemical cycling, sustainability and energy, and the science–policy interface**.
- **https://pubs.acs.org/journal/esthag** — ES&T journal home (current issues, metrics).

## TOC graphic & word-limit policy history

- **https://pubs.acs.org/doi/10.1021/es103646v** — "Mandatory TOC Art for ES&T Research Articles,
  Policy Analysis, Critical Reviews, and Features" (editorial). Verifies the **TOC art is required**
  for those types and that it functions as a visual abstract that illustrates — not duplicates — the
  paper.
- **https://pubs.acs.org/doi/10.1021/es2012269** — "Changes to ES&T Word Limits." Historical context
  for the per-type word caps and the **figure/table word-equivalent** accounting; confirm the current
  numbers on the live guidelines page.

## Companion journal

- **https://researcher-resources.acs.org/publish/author_guidelines?coden=estlcu** — **ES&T Letters**
  Author Guidelines (page-verified). Verifies: ES&T Letters is the ACS companion journal for the
  **rapid disclosure of urgent and impactful results**; primary type is the **Letter (up to 3,000
  words)**; **a TOC graphic is required**; less-urgent findings should instead go to ES&T as a full
  Research Article.
- **https://pubs.acs.org/page/estlcu/about.html** — About ES&T Letters.

## Style & ethics

- **https://pubs.acs.org/doi/full/10.1021/acsguide.40303** — ACS Style Quick Guide (ACS Guide to
  Scholarly Communication). The house style for ES&T citations and conventions.
- **https://pubs.acs.org/page/4authors/index.html** — ACS author resources, including the
  **Ethical Guidelines to Publication of Chemical Research**.
