# External Tools - SOSP

Access date: 2026-07-08

Open these after re-checking the current SOSP cycle's pages; every rule below the
links can change year to year. If direct access to `sigops.org` fails (it returned
403 through the gateway used to build this pack), verify via search renderings of
the exact URL plus an ACM DL or dblp cross-check — see
[`official-source-map.md`](official-source-map.md).

## Official workflow

- SOSP 2026 call for papers: https://sigops.org/s/conferences/sosp/2026/cfp.html
- SOSP 2026 author guidelines (camera-ready): https://sigops.org/s/conferences/sosp/2026/authors.html
- SOSP 2026 HotCRP (submissions + deadlines): https://sosp26.hotcrp.com/
- Artifact evaluation (sysartifacts): https://sysartifacts.github.io/sosp2026/
- SIGOPS conference index and announcements: https://www.sigops.org/
- SOSP historical archive (all editions since 1967): https://www.sosp.org/
- Proceedings of record: https://dl.acm.org/conference/sosp
- SIGOPS Hall of Fame (exemplar mining): https://www.sigops.org/awards/hof/

## Author-side checks before upload

- HotCRP account, abstract registered before the registration gate, complete author
  list and topics, conflict list built from defensible categories.
- Current-year LaTeX format: 12 pages of technical content, references uncounted,
  10-point font on 12-point leading, 7 x 9 inch two-column block.
- Anonymized PDF with the registered paper ID in the author block; metadata scrubbed
  in both the paper and the separate supplementary document.
- Response-phase readiness: figure and invariant numbering stable enough to cite
  from a sub-500-word response.
- After acceptance: shepherd thread, ACM rights form, 13/14-page budget, sysartifacts
  registration within days of notification.

## Do not infer

- Do not project SOSP 2027 dates from the 2026 cycle; the annual cadence is
  verified, the dates are not.
- Do not assume the AE badge set, kick-the-tires phase names, or schedule from a
  prior year's sysartifacts page.
- Do not carry OSDI, EuroSys, or ATC formatting or policy details onto SOSP —
  sibling venues share culture, not rules.
- When two official pages disagree (CFP vs HotCRP vs authors page), the HotCRP
  instance controls deadlines and the newest authors page controls format; record
  the conflict in the source map.
