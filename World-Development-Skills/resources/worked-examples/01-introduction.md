# Worked Example: Reframing an Introduction for World Development

This is a fictional example. It does not describe a real paper.

## Before

> This paper studies an important topic using a large dataset and several robustness checks. We contribute to the literature by adding new evidence and showing heterogeneous effects.

## Diagnosis

- The first sentence does not name the World Development audience or the journal's development studies and development economics across poverty, institutions, sustainability, and policy implementation scope.
- The contribution could fit several siblings: Journal of Development Economics, World Bank Economic Review, Economic Development and Cultural Change, and World Development Perspectives.
- The mechanism words that matter here are absent: development intervention, local institution, poverty mechanism, implementation constraint.

## After

> This paper asks how **development intervention** changes the decision margin that World Development readers care about. The design ties the estimate to **local institution**, separates the main mechanism from **poverty mechanism**, and reports uncertainty in a way that makes the policy or theory implication portable beyond the study setting.
