## Verified on official pages (2026-06-28)

- **Editors-in-Chief: Jampel Dell'Angelo (VU Amsterdam, Amsterdam, Netherlands) and Angelika Rettberg (University of the Andes, Bogota, Colombia).** The editorial-board page also lists 51 editors and editorial board members in 13 countries/regions. The prior "Editor-in-Chief: Arun Agrawal (since 2013)" is stale; the official page now lists Agrawal under previous Editors-in-Chief, so do not use the old leadership claim.
  - https://www.sciencedirect.com/journal/world-development/about/editorial-board
- **Journal status / metrics shown on ScienceDirect:** World Development supports open access; the public journal chrome shows 13.1 CiteScore and 4.8 Impact Factor. Treat metrics as live-page values and refresh before using in promotional or selection claims.
  - https://www.sciencedirect.com/journal/world-development
- **Scope anchor:** the guide describes World Development as a multi-disciplinary monthly journal of development studies focused on improving standards of living and the human condition through development problems and potential solutions.
  - https://www.sciencedirect.com/journal/world-development/publish/guide-for-authors
- **Peer review / anonymity anchor:** World Development follows double anonymized peer review. Submit the title page and anonymized manuscript separately; the anonymized manuscript should omit identifying information.
  - https://www.sciencedirect.com/journal/world-development/publish/guide-for-authors
- **Format anchors:** regular papers are 4,500 to approximately 10,000 words; Development Reviews are 4,500 to approximately 15,000 words, with the Development Review notes also saying 10,000 to 15,000 words and up to 150 references. Use the guide and portal instructions together at submission time.
  - https://www.sciencedirect.com/journal/world-development/publish/guide-for-authors
- **Abstract / keyword / highlight anchors:** the guide requires a concise factual abstract, 3-6 keywords, and 3-5 highlights. Because the page contains both a general 250-word abstract instruction and World Development-specific notes with a 300-word manuscript-file abstract maximum, default drafting should target the stricter limit unless the submission portal indicates otherwise.
  - https://www.sciencedirect.com/journal/world-development/publish/guide-for-authors
- **Data / replication anchors:** for this journal, Option C research-data instructions apply; revised empirical, simulation, or experimental papers must provide replication materials unless editors accept a constraint raised at submission.
  - https://www.sciencedirect.com/journal/world-development/publish/guide-for-authors
- **Open-access APC: USD 4750** excluding taxes for all article types, with personalized APC calculation and open-access agreements possible during submission. The same page lists a 24-month embargo for subscription articles.
  - https://www.sciencedirect.com/journal/world-development/publish/open-access-options
