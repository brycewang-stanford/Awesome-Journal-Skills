## Submission route, article types, length & style

- **https://www.journals.uchicago.edu/journals/ca/instruct** — CA "Instructions for Authors" (UChicago
  Press). Verifies (cross-checked via search-indexed excerpts; live page returned HTTP 403 on direct
  fetch 2026-06 — confirm wording live):
  - **Major Articles** make theoretical or methodological interventions, provide synthetic foundations,
    and set the terms of future research; **6,000–10,000 words** (references and endnotes included);
    **abstract ≤ 200 words**; up to **12 figures and tables**; they receive the **CA✩ Treatment**.
  - **Reports** present key findings, provocations, new frameworks, or analytic insights; **3,000–5,000
    words** (references and endnotes included); **abstract ≤ 200 words**; up to **4 figures and tables**.
  - **Discussion items** respond to publications from roughly the prior **8 months**; **≤ 800 words**
    including references.
  - The journal also publishes **Forums** and **Commentaries**.
  - **File formats:** submit a word-processing file (**`.doc` or `.rtf`, not PDF**) as a single file with
    all text and tables; submit figures (graphs and images) as **separate TIFF (`.tif`) or EPS (`.eps`)**
    files.
  - **Style:** AAA/UChicago-style **free-format** submission is accepted; references should include
    **DOI** where available; final accepted files follow **Chicago Manual of Style author-date** with
    references listed **alphabetically** and **not embedded in endnotes**.
  - **Submission system:** **Editorial Manager**; have the manuscript, title-page information (title,
    short title, authors/affiliations, corresponding-author contact), and abstract ready.
- **https://www.journals.uchicago.edu/journals/ca/prep-gen** — CA "Manuscript Preparation — General."
  Use for current formatting specifics (spacing, endnotes, file preparation). *(Live page returned HTTP

## Pack-level guardrails

- Do **not** confuse CA with *American Anthropologist*, *American Ethnologist*, or *Cultural
  Anthropology*. CA is **Wenner-Gren-sponsored**, published by **UChicago Press**, and is defined by the
  **CA✩ comment-and-reply Treatment** — none of the siblings apply published Comments + Reply.
- The **CA✩ Treatment applies to Major Articles**, not Reports/Forums/Discussion items. Do not promise an
  author published Comments on a Report.
- Editor names are **volatile** (the 2025 co-editor transition is recent) — confirm the live masthead.
- The UChicago Press CA pages blocked automated fetch (HTTP 403) at the access date; several facts here
  rest on search-indexed excerpts of those official pages plus Wenner-Gren/encyclopedic cross-checks.
  Treat caps, file rules, and copyright wording as **verify-on-the-live-page** before a real submission.
- Acceptance rate (~10%) and manuscript volume (~230/yr) are approximate and year-dependent.
