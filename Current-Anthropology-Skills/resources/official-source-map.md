# CA Official Source Map

Primary and authoritative sources behind the Current Anthropology (CA)-specific facts injected into this
skill pack. Current check date: **2026-06-21**. UChicago Press pages are the canonical current policy
source; non-browser automated requests received a Cloudflare challenge during this check, so the pack
keeps UChicago URL anchors and records which facts were read from official UChicago search snippets.
Before a live submission, open the UChicago pages in a normal browser and re-check portal prompts,
article-type caps, file formats, rights wording, and the editorial roster.

## Official journal, scope, sponsor & history

- **https://wennergren.org/article/current-anthropology-editor-in-chief-search-2/** — Wenner-Gren
  editor-search page (**2023-08-04**), directly readable 2026-06-21. Verifies: Wenner-Gren founded CA
  in **1959** and continues to sponsor its publication through **University of Chicago Press**; CA covers
  **all branches** of anthropology and addresses a worldwide audience; it publishes major articles,
  reports, forums, and other content; **Major Articles** receive the **CA Treatment**, defined as
  simultaneous publication of multiple commentaries with the author's reply. Also verifies: six core
  issues per year plus one open-access supplementary issue; core issues include five-to-six major
  articles plus reports/other content; approximate operating figures at the time of the search were
  around **230 manuscripts/year**, about **50%** receiving full peer review, and about **10%** accepted;
  UChicago Press and CA use **Editorial Manager** for online submission/review.
- **https://www.journals.uchicago.edu/journals/ca/about** — CA "About the Journal" page (UChicago
  Press). Canonical current journal page. Official UChicago search snippets checked 2026-06-21 show:
  ISSN **0011-3204**, E-ISSN **1537-5382**, six issues/year, and the current editors. Use this page for
  current scope wording, ISSN/metrics, masthead pointers, and links to author instructions.
- **https://www.journals.uchicago.edu/toc/ca/current** — current CA table-of-contents page (UChicago
  Press). Official snippets checked 2026-06-21 show the editors as **Caroline Schuster** and
  **Catherine J. Frieman**, plus ISSN **0011-3204** and E-ISSN **1537-5382**.

## Submission route, article types, length & style

- **https://www.journals.uchicago.edu/journals/ca/instruct** — CA "Instructions for Authors"
  (UChicago Press). Canonical current author-instruction page. Official UChicago search snippets checked
  2026-06-21 verify these pack facts:
  - **Major Articles**: theoretical/methodological/synthetic interventions and the flagship article type;
    **6,000-10,000 words** including references/endnotes; **abstract <= 200 words**; up to **12 figures
    and tables**; undergo the **CA Treatment**.
  - **Reports**: **3,000-5,000 words** including references/endnotes; **abstract <= 200 words**; up to
    **4 figures and tables**.
  - **Discussion items**: **<= 800 words**; text, references, and endnotes count toward the word limit.
  - **File formats**: submit a word-processing file (**.doc or .rtf, not PDF**) and figures as separate
    **TIFF (.tif) or EPS (.eps)** files; GIF/JPEG figure files are not accepted in the snippet.
  - **Submission system**: Editorial Manager, corroborated by the Wenner-Gren editor-search page.
  - **Copyright**: assignment to the Wenner-Gren Foundation for major articles, reports, forums, and
    other CA contribution types; see rights page below for current wording.
- **https://www.journals.uchicago.edu/journals/ca/checklist** — CA "Checklist for CA Authors"
  (UChicago Press). Official snippet checked 2026-06-21 corroborates the **<= 800-word** discussion-item
  limit and that text, references, and endnotes all count toward the limit. Use this checklist as the
  final local preflight companion to `skills/curranthro-submission/templates/checklist.md` if a template
  is later added.
- **https://www.journals.uchicago.edu/journals/ca/prep-gen** — CA "Manuscript Preparation — General"
  (UChicago Press). Use for current formatting specifics such as spacing, endnotes, title pages, and
  file preparation.

## Rights, open access & supplementary material

- **https://www.journals.uchicago.edu/journals/ca/jrnl_rights** — CA / UChicago Press authors' rights.
  Canonical current rights/licensing page. Pair this page with the Instructions for Authors before
  describing the exact copyright assignment or open-access option for a live submission.
- **https://wennergren.org/article/current-anthropology-editor-in-chief-search-2/** — also verifies the
  journal's open-access posture at the time of the 2023 search: CA is a subscription journal with a
  commitment to open access; the editor could select one article for open-access availability in each
  core issue; green open access is provided for all articles; gold open access is available for authors
  with qualifying funder requirements. It also notes online supplementary material can include data,
  methodological details, photographs, video/audio clips, animations, and similar material.

## Pack-level guardrails

- Do **not** confuse CA with *American Anthropologist*, *American Ethnologist*, or *Cultural
  Anthropology*. CA is **Wenner-Gren-sponsored**, published by **UChicago Press**, and is defined by the
  **CA Treatment**: Major Articles are published with commentaries and the author's reply.
- The **CA Treatment applies to Major Articles**, not Reports/Forums/Discussion items. Do not promise an
  author published Comments on a Report.
- Editor names, article-type caps, portal prompts, rights wording, and acceptance figures are live
  submission facts. Re-check the UChicago pages and Editorial Manager immediately before submission.
- Acceptance rate (~10%) and manuscript volume (~230/year) come from the 2023 Wenner-Gren editor-search
  page and are year-dependent operating figures, not a standing promise.
