# External Tools - ICDM

Access date: 2026-07-09

Open these after reopening the current ICDM edition's pages. ICDM rules are re-set every year by
a new committee, and 2026 introduced two new tracks (Applied, Blue Sky), so a link that worked
last edition may point at a different regime this one. Note the gateway 403 on the host domain
(see `official-source-map.md`): if a direct fetch fails, open the page in a browser.

## Official workflow

- ICDM 2026 conference site: https://icdm2026.neu.edu.cn/
- ICDM 2026 Research Track call: http://icdm2026.neu.edu.cn/CallforResearchTrack/list.htm
- ICDM 2026 Applied Track call: http://icdm2026.neu.edu.cn/CallforAppliedTrackPapers/list.htm
- ICDM 2026 Blue Sky Track (CCC): https://cccblog.org/2026/04/07/call-for-papers-ccc-sponsored-blue-sky-track-at-icdm-2026/
- IEEE ICDM awards (impact / best-paper history): https://icdm.zhonghuapu.com/Awards/Awards-All.shtml
- IEEE Xplore ICDM proceedings: https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/ (search "IEEE International Conference on Data Mining")
- dblp ICDM index (cross-check): https://dblp.org/db/conf/icdm/index.html

## Author-side checks

- Correct **track** chosen: Research (triple-blind) vs Applied (single-blind) vs Blue Sky vs
  Demo/Workshop — each has its own call, anonymity regime, and evidence bar.
- IEEE two-column template applied without margin, font, or spacing edits; PDF exported for IEEE.
- **10-page cap counting references and appendices** met before upload; over-length is an
  automatic no-review reject.
- Triple-blind sweep for the Research Track: no author names, affiliations, acknowledgements,
  grant numbers, self-referential phrasing, or identifying dataset/system names anywhere in the
  body, figures, or citations.
- Reproducibility and supplementary evidence packaged inside the anonymity regime of the chosen
  track.
- Abstract registered by the abstract deadline; full paper uploaded by the paper deadline (AoE).
- Post-acceptance: register at least one author, prepare IEEE camera-ready and copyright forms,
  and handle a possible **short-paper acceptance** (a full-length draft compressed to the
  short-paper camera-ready length).

## Do not infer

- Do not carry ICDM dates, chairs, or fees forward from a prior edition.
- Do not assume double-blind: the Research Track is **triple-blind**, and the 2026 Applied Track
  is **single-blind** — the anonymity regime depends on the track.
- Do not assume a rebuttal exists this edition; ICDM's tradition is no rebuttal, and any response
  window must be confirmed on the current call.
- Do not assume a separate reference or appendix page budget: at ICDM everything lives inside the
  single 10-page cap.
- If the official call and an IEEE Xplore or committee page disagree, use the newest official
  instruction and record the conflict.
