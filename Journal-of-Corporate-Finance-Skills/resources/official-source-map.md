# JCF Official Source Map

Primary and authoritative sources behind the Journal of Corporate Finance (JCF) facts injected into this skill pack.
Access date for all entries: **2026-06-01**. Re-verify volatile items (fee, editors, abstract cap, data policy wording, review model) before relying on them — journal pages change, and several Elsevier/ScienceDirect pages block automated fetches.

## Official journal pages (Elsevier / ScienceDirect)

- **https://www.sciencedirect.com/journal/journal-of-corporate-finance/publish/guide-for-authors** — JCF Guide for Authors. Verifies:
  - Publisher = **Elsevier**; the journal publishes original manuscripts **or shorter format papers** in theoretical and empirical corporate finance. *(accessed 2026-06-01)*
  - **Abstract ≤ 250 words**; references should be avoided in the abstract; non-standard abbreviations defined at first mention; **1–7 keywords** required. *(accessed 2026-06-01)*
  - Each new submission must be accompanied by a **US$340.00 submission fee**; the paper is considered only after the fee is paid; the fee is **non-refundable**, and **desk-rejected articles are not refunded**. *(accessed 2026-06-01)*
  - **Single anonymized (single-blind) review**; suitable submissions are sent to a **minimum of two independent reviewers**; an active **desk-rejection policy** rejects a non-trivial fraction without full review. *(accessed 2026-06-01)*
  - In-text citations are **author-date (Harvard-style)** — name and year in parentheses, e.g., (Campbell and Peden, 2005). At initial submission, **"your paper, your way"** — references may be in any consistent style provided all required elements are present; DOIs encouraged; formal Elsevier reference styling applies at revision/acceptance. *(accessed 2026-06-01)*
  - **Research data = Elsevier Option C**: state data availability at submission; deposit/cite/link research data (including software, code, models, algorithms) in a relevant repository, or provide a statement explaining why data cannot be shared. *(accessed 2026-06-01)*
  - **Free integrated SSRN preprint posting** offered at submission (manuscript made public with a preprint DOI and a link to the version of record). *(accessed 2026-06-01)*
  - Mandatory **generative-AI disclosure** at submission with human-oversight requirements; required **competing-interest declaration** via Elsevier's declarations tool. *(accessed 2026-06-01)*
  - Submission is via **Editorial Manager**, reached from the ScienceDirect "Submit your article" link. *(accessed 2026-06-01)*

## Scope (from Guide for Authors and journal description)

- Field focus = **corporate finance (empirical and theoretical)**: financial structure, governance, payout, financial contracting, risk management, innovation, M&A, and international corporate finance, including work at the intersection of corporate finance and macroeconomics, asset pricing, household/behavioral finance, fintech/blockchain, law, financial intermediation, and market microstructure. Source: ScienceDirect journal homepage + Guide for Authors. *(accessed 2026-06-01)*
