## Research transparency & data policy

- **https://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/page/journal/14680491/homepage/forauthors.html** (author guidelines)
  and Wiley's standard data-sharing policy pages. Verifies: a **Data Availability Statement is required**
  (the statement declares whether replication materials are available and how to access them); a
  **DOI-citable data repository is recommended**; an **anonymized pre-analysis plan may be supplied as
  supplementary material**. Note: the statement is **mandatory**, but — unlike APSR — *Governance* does
  **not** run a verified pre-publication reproduction of the manuscript's tables and figures as a
  condition of acceptance. Treat this as a transparency *statement* requirement, not a reproduction
  gate. Confirm exact wording on the live page.
