# Worked Example: Reframing an Introduction for Entrepreneurship Theory and Practice

This is a fictional example. It does not describe a real paper.

## Before

> This paper studies an important topic using a large dataset and several robustness checks. We contribute to the literature by adding new evidence and showing heterogeneous effects.

## Diagnosis

- The first sentence does not name the ETP audience or the journal's entrepreneurship theory, new ventures, founder teams, entrepreneurial finance, ecosystems, and family business scope.
- The contribution could fit several siblings: Journal of Business Venturing, Strategic Entrepreneurship Journal, Research Policy, and Academy of Management Journal.
- The mechanism words that matter here are absent: venture formation, founder team, entrepreneurial ecosystem, opportunity process.

## After

> This paper asks how **venture formation** changes the decision margin that ETP readers care about. The design ties the estimate to **founder team**, separates the main mechanism from **entrepreneurial ecosystem**, and reports uncertainty in a way that makes the policy or theory implication portable beyond the study setting.
