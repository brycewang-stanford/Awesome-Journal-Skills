# Entrepreneurship Theory and Practice Skills

<p align="center"><img src="assets/cover.svg" width="200" alt="Entrepreneurship Theory and Practice Skills 封面"></p>

[English](README.md) | 简体中文

面向 **Entrepreneurship Theory and Practice（ETP）** 投稿的 12 个 agent skills。本包围绕 entrepreneurship theory, new ventures, founder teams, entrepreneurial finance, ecosystems, and family business 设计，帮助稿件区别于 Journal of Business Venturing, Strategic Entrepreneurship Journal, Research Policy, and Academy of Management Journal，并强调 entrepreneurship theory with credible venture-level evidence and boundary conditions。

**官方依据核验日期：2026-06**（投稿前需复核易变细节）：见 [`resources/official-source-map.md`](resources/official-source-map.md)。

## 为什么需要单独的技能栈？

| ETP 约束 | 对稿件的要求 |
|-------------------|--------------|
| 范围 | 主张必须服务于 entrepreneurship theory, new ventures, founder teams, entrepreneurial finance, ecosystems, and family business |
| 同门边界 | 说明为什么不是 Journal of Business Venturing, Strategic Entrepreneurship Journal, Research Policy, and Academy of Management Journal |
| 证据标准 | 设计、模型、综述或质性证据必须匹配 entrepreneurship theory with credible venture-level evidence and boundary conditions |
| 来源纪律 | 当前流程事实必须有来源，或明确标记 待核实 |

## 快速开始

```text
/plugin marketplace add ./Entrepreneurship-Theory-and-Practice-Skills
/plugin install entrepreneurship-theory-and-practice-skills
```

手动使用：先打开 [`skills/etp-workflow/SKILL.md`](skills/etp-workflow/SKILL.md)。

## 默认工作流

```text
etp-workflow → etp-topic-selection → etp-theory-development → etp-literature-positioning → etp-methods → etp-data-analysis → etp-contribution-framing → etp-tables-figures → etp-writing-style → etp-submission → etp-review-process → etp-rebuttal
```
