## VERIFIED facts injected into the skills (with source #)

- **Field/scope:** entrepreneurship and new venture creation — antecedents, mechanisms, and consequences of the entrepreneurial phenomenon; venture creation, entrepreneurial cognition/behavior, financing, teams, social/sustainable/international entrepreneurship, ecosystems. (#1, #2)
- **Publisher / identifiers:** Elsevier; subscription/hybrid; print ISSN 0883-9026; eISSN 1873-2003; bimonthly; English. (#1, #6, #7)
- **History:** established 1985 by Ian MacMillan. (#1)
- **Multidisciplinary grounding:** explicitly grounded in economics, psychology, and sociology, welcoming anthropology, geography, history, and multiple functional areas. (#1, #2)
- **Editor-in-Chief:** Jeffery S. McMullen (Dale M. Coleman Chair in Entrepreneurship, Kelley School of Business, Indiana University; mcmullej@indiana.edu); editorial office at the Johnson Center for Entrepreneurship & Innovation. (#2, #4)
- **Editorial routing:** field editors (handling editors) manage manuscripts under the EIC and curate their own trusted reviewer pools; managing/handling contact Sarasa Subramony (SubramonyS@Darden.virginia.edu). (#2, #4)
- **Review model:** double-anonymized (double-blind); suitable manuscripts sent to a minimum of two reviewers; anonymized manuscript required (no author names, affiliations, or acknowledgements). (#3)
- **Submission system:** Elsevier Editorial Manager (editorialmanager.com/jbvi); fully online stepwise file upload; co-submission to Data in Brief / MethodsX on the "Attach files" page. (#4)
- **First-submission format:** "Your Paper Your Way" — single file, any reasonable layout; flexible reference formatting provided each reference includes author(s), title, year, journal/book, volume, pages/article number. (#3)
- **Abstract:** concise and factual, not exceeding 250 words. (#3)
- **Declarations:** declaration of competing interest and a generative-AI disclosure statement required at submission. (#3)
- **Data policy:** Elsevier research-data framework — authors encouraged to deposit/cite data and to include a data availability statement (or state why data cannot be shared); statement published on ScienceDirect. No verified hard full-replication-package mandate in the public guide. (#3)
- **Open access / fees:** no submission fee; hybrid title; optional open access carries an APC of USD 4,280 (excluding taxes); choice does not affect peer review or acceptance. (#5)
- **Status:** FT50 journal; top-tier entrepreneurship outlet; Best Paper Award since 2014. (#1)
- **Metrics:** CiteScore 14.6; SNIP 4.53; Q1 Business & International Management — treat as **approximate / 待核实** (third-party aggregator). (#6)

- **Mandatory full replication/code package** — 待核实: public materials indicate Elsevier's "encourages data sharing" regime plus a required data statement, not a verified hard replication-archive mandate.
