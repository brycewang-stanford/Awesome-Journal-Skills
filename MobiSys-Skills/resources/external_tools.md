# External Tools - MobiSys

Access date: 2026-07-09

Use these surfaces after reopening the current MobiSys cycle pages. MobiSys rules change by
edition, and the whole calendar hangs on one December deadline, so verify before advising.

If a direct fetch returns HTTP 403 (as sigmobile.org and hotcrp.com did on 2026-07-09), read
the exact URL through a search-engine rendering and cross-check the ACM DL and dblp before
relying on any number.

## Author-side verification passes (run each cycle)

- **Clock pass.** Confirm the single paper-registration and paper-submission dates on the
  current `mobisys<yy>.hotcrp.com/deadlines` page, and the exact time-zone/AoE framing. There
  is one hard December cutoff per year; a miss costs a full cycle.
- **Budget pass.** Confirm the page cap is still 12 double-column pages including figures and
  tables, and that references and appendices remain outside it. Re-read the ACM template
  version named in the CFP.
- **Blindness pass.** Confirm double-blind is in force and sweep the PDF, supplement, and any
  demo media for identity leaks — including device serial numbers, account handles in
  screenshots, internal codenames, and repository owners in trace paths.
- **Status pass.** Confirm which round you are in and what the notification means: an early
  rejection after round 1 is terminal for this cycle; a round-2 outcome precedes the rebuttal.
- **Rebuttal-scope pass.** Confirm the current rebuttal length limit and the rule on new
  results (directly-responsive only, or promised for camera-ready), and whether any revision
  channel exists for this edition.
- **Artifact pass.** Confirm the three ACM badge names, the artifact deadline, and the
  single-blind script-first evaluation model on the current Artifact Evaluation page.

## Do not infer

- Do not infer future-cycle dates, host, chairs, or page caps from MobiSys 2026.
- Do not assume a MobiCom-style rolling two-deadline calendar or a MobiCom/NSDI-style one-shot
  revision channel; MobiSys ran one December deadline with rebuttal-only response in 2026.
- If official pages disagree, use the newest CFP, HotCRP deadlines page, or direct chair
  communication as controlling, and record the conflict.
