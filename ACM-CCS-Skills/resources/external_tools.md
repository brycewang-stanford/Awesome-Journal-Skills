# External Tools - ACM CCS

Access date: 2026-07-08

Use these tools after reopening the current CCS cycle pages. CCS rules are set per cycle and
per review cycle (A and B), so a fact verified for one cycle may not hold for the next.

## Official workflow

- CCS 2026 Call for Papers: https://www.sigsac.org/ccs/CCS2026/call-for/call-for-papers.html
- CCS 2026 Call for Artifacts: https://www.sigsac.org/ccs/CCS2026/call-for/call-for-artifacts.html
- CCS 2026 home and organization: https://www.sigsac.org/ccs/CCS2026/
- Cycle A HotCRP portal and deadlines: https://ccs2026a.hotcrp.com/ (see /deadlines)
- Cycle B HotCRP portal and deadlines: https://ccs2026b.hotcrp.com/ (see /deadlines)
- SIGSAC CCS landing and Test-of-Time Award: https://www.sigsac.org/ccs.html and /award/tta.html
- ACM Digital Library CCS proceedings: https://dl.acm.org/conference/ccs

Note: `www.sigsac.org` returned HTTP 403 to direct automated fetches on 2026-07-08. Read the
pages in a browser or via search-engine renderings of the exact URLs, and cross-check against
the HotCRP sites and the ACM DL.

## Author-side checks before upload

- HotCRP account and profile, conflicts, topics, author list, title, and the mandatory
  abstract registered before the registration deadline.
- The unaltered ACM `sigconf` template, the 12-page main-body limit, appendix and
  bibliography placement, and PDF export.
- Anonymized paper: third-person self-citation, no identifying system names, no author
  metadata, and anonymized artifact links.
- Ethics considerations section for human-subjects, user-data, or real-vulnerability work,
  with disclosure timeline and harm-minimization reasoning.
- Threat model stated up front; adaptive-attack evaluation present for any defense claim.
- Artifact-availability statement, and a justification for anything withheld.
- Camera-ready package: ACM `sigconf` final build, ACM rights form, badge placement,
  registration, and in-person presentation.

## Do not infer

- Do not infer future-cycle dates or the CCS 2027 host city from the 2026 cycle.
- Do not assume the same page limit, submission cap, decision categories, ethics-section
  placement, or artifact-badge set without reopening the current CFP and call for artifacts.
- If official pages disagree, the newest CFP, HotCRP announcement, or direct chair
  communication controls; record the conflict.
