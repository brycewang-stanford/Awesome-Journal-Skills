## Official journal & policy pages

- **https://www.sciencedirect.com/journal/journal-of-development-economics/publish/guide-for-authors**
  — JDE Guide for Authors (Elsevier / ScienceDirect). Verifies: data used must be clearly and
  precisely documented and readily available to any researcher for replication; authors of accepted
  papers with empirical, simulation, or experimental work must provide data, programs, and
  computational details sufficient for replication prior to publication; data/programs may be
  requested at the review stage; roughly 1,300 papers submitted per year, only about a quarter sent
  for review, ~6-8% published; an author may not submit more than three papers within any 12-month
  period; manuscripts in either American or British English, not a combination. **Note:** page
  returned HTTP 403 to automated fetch at access time; facts above are from web-search renderings of
  this page and are high-confidence but not verbatim page-confirmed (see UNVERIFIED notes).
- **https://data.mendeley.com/journal/03043878** — JDE on Mendeley Data. Verifies: JDE partners with
  Mendeley Data to host research data, displayed next to the article on ScienceDirect. ISSN 0304-3878.
- **https://www.elsevier.com/journals/journal-of-development-economics/0304-3878** — Elsevier journal
  home (publisher confirmation). Verifies: publisher = Elsevier; field = development economics. 待核实
  on the exact open-access Article Publishing Charge from this page (see UNVERIFIED).

## Pre-results review / Registered Reports track

- **https://www.bitss.org/publishing/jde/** — Berkeley Initiative for Transparency in the Social
  Sciences (BITSS) page on the JDE pre-results review track. Verifies: pre-results review /
  Registered Reports is a **permanent** track (piloted 2018-2019 with BITSS); Stage 1 proposal
  includes background/motivation, hypotheses, study procedures, statistical analysis plan, power
  analysis, and pilot data if applicable; in-principle acceptance is granted before results are
  known; the published article carries a footnote noting pre-results submission and includes the
  pre-specified research plan in the supplementary online appendix.
- **https://afosterri.org/jdepreresults/author-guidelines/** — JDE pre-results review author
  guidelines (Andrew Foster). Verifies: track administered with BITSS; Lead Editor **Andrew Foster**
  and Editor **Dean Karlan**; submission via **Editorial Manager**; mandatory replication policy with
  data posted on the JDE website; Stage 1 proposal must not exceed **60 pages**; abstract maximum
  **150 words** (for Stage 1 proposals); a cover page is required with JEL codes, hypotheses,
  analysis plan, power analysis, and ethics/funding/conflict-of-interest disclosures.

## Short-paper (limited-revision) track

- **https://blogs.worldbank.org/en/impactevaluations/new-short-paper-track-journal-development-economics-guest-post-andrew-foster**
  — World Bank Development Impact blog, guest post by Andrew Foster announcing the short-paper track.
  Verifies: short-paper limited-revision track follows **AER: Insights** guidelines — at most **6,000
  words**, at most **5 exhibits** (tables or figures), online appendix up to **20 pages**; decisions
  generally within **4-6 weeks**; **4 weeks** to revise a conditionally-accepted manuscript; a single
  revision round; resubmissions are **not** returned to referees.

## Editors, history, scope, metrics

- **https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Journal_of_Development_Economics** — Verifies: publisher = Elsevier;
  ISSN **0304-3878**; field = development economics; Editor / Lead Editor **Andrew Foster** (since
  **2016**); widely described as the **leading field journal in development economics**.
