# External Tools - IEEE VIS

Access date: 2026-07-09

Use these surfaces after reopening the current IEEE VIS cycle pages. VIS mechanics — the
abstract/paper/supplemental deadlines, the 9+2 page budget, the two-phase review and its
second-round timing, the area model, whether double-blind is optional — are decided per edition, and
the `ieeevis.org`/`ieeexplore.ieee.org`/`precisionconference.com` gateway may block direct fetches
(read via search renderings and cross-check IEEE Xplore/TVCG and dblp). Do not confuse IEEE VIS with
its sibling VGTC conferences **IEEE VR** or **ISMAR** — separate calls on the same PCS society.

## Cross-check surfaces (when the gateway blocks the primary page)

- IEEE Xplore / TVCG conference-issue tables of contents for the VIS special issues.
- dblp IEEE VIS / TVCG streams: https://dblp.org/db/journals/tvcg/index.html and the VIS proceedings.
- The official `@ieeevis` announcements for machine-readable deadline reminders.
- Community deadline trackers (e.g. the visualization deadlines list) for a quick cross-check —
  never as the primary source.

## Author-side checks

- PCS profile, conflicts, coauthor list (final by the abstract deadline), primary area, and keywords.
- VGTC/TVCG template compliance and the current 9+2 page budget (9 content + 2 references/acks).
- Author-optional double-blind sweep (if opted in): anonymize the PDF, the open-materials links,
  system names, the supplemental **video**, and any visible repository owner.
- Supplemental package — video, code, data — prepared for its own later deadline (the extra week).
- Open Practices intent: preprint, preregistration (for studies), open code/data, and — separately —
  the Graphics Replicability Stamp on its own track after acceptance.

## Do not infer

- Do not infer the exact dates, the second-round timeline, or whether double-blind is mandatory this
  year: confirm on the current call and review instructions.
- Do not assume the page budget, template revision, area boundaries, reviewer-count rule
  (primary/secondary/external/student), or Open-Practices wording carry over between editions.
- Do not assume short papers publish in TVCG or share the full-paper cycle — verify (**待核实**).
- If pages disagree, treat the newest Call for Full Papers, Review Instructions, or a direct chair
  announcement as controlling, and record the conflict.
