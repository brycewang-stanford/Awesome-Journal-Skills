# Validation set — `jaar-submission`

Held-out researcher queries (the skill text does not contain these) plus the
verified ground-truth fact sheet the judge scores against. Ground truth is drawn
from `Journal-of-the-American-Academy-of-Religion-Skills/resources/official-source-map.md`
(OUP Information to Authors, Why-Publish, Book Reviewer Guidelines, and JAAR Style
Sheet, accessed 2026-06).

## Held-out queries

**Q1 (timeline + acceptance).**
> "How long does JAAR take to reach a first decision, and what is the acceptance
> or rejection rate?"

**Q2 (book review eligibility).**
> "I'm a graduate student. Can I submit a book review to JAAR?"

**Q3 (citation style + open access / fee).**
> "What citation style does JAAR use, and is there an open-access option or any
> submission fee?"

## Ground-truth fact sheet

- **Decision timeline.** Average time to a **first decision ≈ 106 days** (as of
  2022); a final decision may take **up to ~6 months**. → Q1.
- **Rejection rate.** About **90%** (≈ eight articles per issue, ≈ 32/year). → Q1.
- **Book reviews.** JAAR does **not** accept unsolicited reviews **and** does
  **not** publish reviews from **graduate students**; reviews are **commissioned**
  via the Book Review Editor. So a grad student cannot submit one. → Q2.
- **Citations.** **In-text author-date**, NOT footnote citations; footnotes for
  substance only; alphabetized year-keyed reference list. For issues the JAAR style
  sheet does not cover, consult the **Chicago Manual of Style** (the sheet has cited
  the **15th ed.**; edition is volatile — CMOS is now in its 18th). → Q3.
- **Fees / open access.** **No submission fee**; an **optional OA license** carries
  an OA charge alongside the standard license, plus OUP **Read & Publish**
  agreements. → Q3.
- **Length / abstract / framing.** ~8,000–12,000 words incl. references and
  footnotes; abstract ≤ 150 words; must address a question of broad and fundamental
  interest to the study of religion or it is returned for reframing before review.
- **Portal.** ScholarOne / Manuscript Central (`mc.manuscriptcentral.com/jaarel`),
  Main Document (anonymous) + separate Title Page. Double-blind.

## Volatile — defer to a live check (rubric §5)

- Current **editor(s)** and editorial term.
- Exact **CMOS edition** referenced by the current style sheet.
- Exact **OA APC** amount and current Read & Publish coverage.
- Exact **word range** inclusions (working figure ~8,000–12,000 incl. notes/refs).
