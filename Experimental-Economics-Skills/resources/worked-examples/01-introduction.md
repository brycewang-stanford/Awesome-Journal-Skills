# Worked Example: Reframing an Introduction for Experimental Economics

This is a fictional example. It does not describe a real paper.

## Before

> This paper studies an important topic using a large dataset and several robustness checks. We contribute to the literature by adding new evidence and showing heterogeneous effects.

## Diagnosis

- The first sentence does not name the Experimental Economics audience or the journal's laboratory, field, online, and artefactual experiments in economics scope.
- The contribution could fit several siblings: JEBO, Games and Economic Behavior, Management Science, and Journal of Risk and Uncertainty.
- The mechanism words that matter here are absent: experimental protocol, incentive compatibility, pre-analysis plan, treatment contrast.

## After

> This paper asks how **experimental protocol** changes the decision margin that Experimental Economics readers care about. The design ties the estimate to **incentive compatibility**, separates the main mechanism from **pre-analysis plan**, and reports uncertainty in a way that makes the policy or theory implication portable beyond the study setting.
