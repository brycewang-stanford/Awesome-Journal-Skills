## Author-side checks

- OpenReview account, profile, conflicts, author list, subject areas, and track.
- Current AAAI author kit and formatting files.
- Anonymous PDF, no acknowledgments in review copy, no identity-revealing supplemental files.
- Reproducibility checklist appended after references when current instructions require it.
- Supplementary ZIPs for technical appendix, multimedia appendix, and code/data.
- Rebuttal draft within the current character limit, with no URLs if the current FAQ forbids them.
- Camera-ready source package, copyright transfer, registration, and in-person presentation plan.

## Do not infer

- Do not infer next-cycle dates from AAAI-26.
- Do not assume the same page limits, two-phase review structure, AI review pilot, rebuttal length,
  supplementary-material rules, AI-use policy, or presentation obligations without reopening the
  current official pages.
- If official AAAI pages disagree, use the newest author kit or direct chair communication as the
  controlling source and record the conflict.
