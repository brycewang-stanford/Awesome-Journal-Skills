# Editor & Reviewer Strategy (revedres-editor-strategy)

## When to trigger

- The review is drafted and you need to see it as RER's editors and reviewers will
- You are unsure whether the contribution will read as "advance" or "competent summary"
- You want to pre-empt the predictable reviewer objections before they are written
- You are weighing RER against a sibling venue and need the fit call

## How RER is refereed (and what that means for you)

RER reviews are **submitted and peer-reviewed by AERA/SAGE** — there is no commissioning editor pre-clearing your topic, so the manuscript must win on its own. The co-editors (the most-recent-listed team based at Penn State — Boveda, Ford, Frankenberg, López; publicized as the 2022–2025 team, with no 2026+ successor team announced as of 2026-06-23 web search; 检索于 2026-06-23，以官网为准) desk-screen for fit and rigor before assigning masked reviewers. Two questions decide the manuscript:

1. **Is it genuinely integrative and advancing?** RER explicitly does not want annotated bibliographies or thin stock-takes. Reviewers ask: does the framework reorganize the field and move theory or policy?
2. **Is the review itself rigorous?** For systematic reviews/meta-analyses: documented exhaustive search (PRISMA), reliable coding, defensible models, risk-of-bias, and sensitivity analysis. Process rigor is not negotiable.

Consult the **Guidelines for Reviewers** RER publishes — they state the criteria your manuscript is judged on; write *to* them.

## The predictable reviewer objections (pre-empt each)

| Objection | Where it is answered |
|---|---|
| "This is a summary, not an integration." | `revedres-organizing-framework` — name the spine and the advance up front |
| "The search is incomplete / not reproducible." | `revedres-literature-synthesis` + PRISMA flow |
| "Coding reliability is unstated." | `revedres-transparency-and-reproducibility` — report κ/ICC |
| "The pooled effect ignores heterogeneity / bias." | `revedres-comprehensiveness-and-balance` — I², moderators, funnel, sensitivity |
| "Conflicting findings are vote-counted." | weigh by estimand + design quality, steelman camps |
| "The author over-cites their own program." | bracket self-work; emphasis identity-blind |
| "This belongs at AERJ / *AERA Open*." | confirm it is a review, not original empirical research |

## Scope and contribution framing

- **Lead with the advance, not the gap.** "No one has reviewed X" is weak; "the field disagrees because studies estimate different objects, and this taxonomy reconciles them" is strong.
- **Right-size the scope** so the search is provably exhaustive yet the contribution matters across education.
- **Name the audience** — RER's broad, cross-disciplinary education readership — and write the contribution in their terms.

## Sibling-venue fit (route honestly)

| Venue | Take it there instead when… |
|---|---|
| **AERJ / *AERA Open*** | the core is original empirical research, not a synthesis |
| ***Educational Researcher*** | it is a short commentary, policy essay, or methodological brief, not a comprehensive review |
| ***Review of Research in Education*** | it fits a commissioned thematic volume, not an unsolicited integrative review |
| ***Psychological Bulletin*** | it is a psychology review with no central bearing on education |
| **Annual Reviews / discipline handbooks** | the piece is an invited, non-systematic overview |

## Checklist

- [ ] Contribution framed as an *advance* (reconciles/reframes), not just "no prior review"
- [ ] Manuscript written to RER's published Guidelines for Reviewers
- [ ] Each predictable objection (summary / search / coding / heterogeneity / vote-count / self-cite) pre-empted in the text
- [ ] Scope right-sized: provably exhaustive search *and* cross-education importance
- [ ] Audience named and addressed (broad, cross-disciplinary education readership)
- [ ] Sibling-venue fit checked; RER is the right home (not AERJ / ER / RRE / Psych Bulletin)

## Anti-patterns

- Pitching the gap ("nobody has done this") instead of the advance
- Submitting a competent summary and hoping reviewers see integration the text does not state
- Ignoring the Guidelines for Reviewers and being surprised by the criteria
- Writing for a sub-specialty when RER's readership is broad
- Sending original empirical research to a review journal (a desk-reject)
