# Review of Educational Research (RER) Official Source Map

Primary and authoritative sources behind every RER-specific fact injected into this
skill pack. **Access date for all entries: 2026-06.** RER is published by **SAGE** on
behalf of the **American Educational Research Association (AERA)**; submission runs
through **Manuscript Central / ScholarOne** (`mc.manuscriptcentral.com/rer`).
Re-verify volatile items (current editors, contact email, submission fee, exact
length limit, policy effective dates) before relying on them — SAGE/AERA pages
change. Items that could not be confirmed from a primary fetch are marked
**待核实 (to be verified)**.

## Official SAGE / AERA RER pages (publisher / editorial authority)

- **https://journals.sagepub.com/author-instructions/rer** — RER Submission
  Guidelines (SAGE). Verifies: RER publishes **comprehensive reviews of literature
  related to education** and **does not publish original empirical research** unless
  it is incorporated in a broader integrative review; the text **conforms to APA
  style (7th ed.)**; an **abstract of no more than 150 words** with keywords (placed
  separately and on the second page after the BLINDED title page); submission is in
  **Microsoft Word** (not PDF); upload (a) a separate **unblinded title page** with an
  APA-7 **Author Note**, (b) the **main manuscript** with a **BLINDED title page**,
  abstract + keywords, and body including tables and figures, and (c) **Author Bios**;
  manuscripts are **anonymized for masked review**, and anonymizing means removing
  **only limited-circulation** self-citations (forthcoming, in press, unpublished),
  not ordinary published self-citations; individuals submitting **systematic reviews
  or meta-analyses** should consult **The PRISMA Statement** and the **"Reporting
  Standards for Research in Psychology"** article in *American Psychologist* (the JARS
  family, incl. MARS); authors should consult the **Guidelines for Reviewers**.
  Accessed 2026-06. (Page is partly JS-rendered; load-bearing facts corroborated via
  web search of the same page — see 待核实 for items not captured as static text.)
- **https://journals.sagepub.com/home/rer** — RER journal home (SAGE). Verifies: RER
  is an AERA journal published by SAGE; its remit is critical, integrative reviews of
  research bearing on education. Accessed 2026-06.
- **https://www.aera.net/Publications/Journals/Review-of-Educational-Research** — RER
  page (AERA). Verifies: RER publishes **critical, integrative reviews of research
  literature bearing on education**, welcomes interdisciplinary submissions (e.g.
  psychology, sociology, history, economics) **provided they address educational
  issues**, **does not publish original empirical research** unless incorporated in a
  broader integrative review, is published **bimonthly (six issues/year)**, and was
  **established in 1931**; it occasionally publishes **solicited but refereed**
  analytic reviews of special topics. Accessed 2026-06.
- **http://mc.manuscriptcentral.com/rer** — RER submission portal (ScholarOne /
  Manuscript Central). Verifies: manuscripts are submitted electronically to the
  editorial team via this URL. Accessed 2026-06.
- **https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Review_of_Educational_Research** — secondary
  background. Corroborates (not primary): RER founded 1931; published by SAGE for
  AERA; high impact in education. Used only to corroborate founding year and
  publisher; venue facts are anchored to the SAGE/AERA pages above. Accessed 2026-06.

## Reporting standards RER points to (external authorities)

- **PRISMA Statement** (`prisma-statement.org`) — the systematic-review/meta-analysis
  reporting checklist and flow diagram. RER's submission guidance directs authors of
  systematic reviews and meta-analyses to consult it. Accessed 2026-06.
- **APA "Reporting Standards for Research in Psychology"** (*American Psychologist*,
  2008, 63, 839–851) — the JARS family, including **MARS** for meta-analyses. RER's
  submission guidance cites this article for systematic reviews/meta-analyses.
  Accessed 2026-06.
- **PROSPERO / OSF** — protocol registration platforms for systematic reviews; used
  with **PRISMA-P** for protocol reporting. Background standard (not RER-specific
  mandate), referenced in the protocol skill. Accessed 2026-06.
