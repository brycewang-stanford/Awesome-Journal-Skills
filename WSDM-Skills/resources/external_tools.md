## Per-cycle re-check list

Before deadline-sensitive advice, confirm on the current edition's site:

1. Abstract and paper deadlines (long and short tracks), AoE convention.
2. Page budget and what counts inside it - the appendix-inclusive rule and the
   uncounted references/ethics-section rule both changed shape between 2025
   and 2026 and can change again.
3. The live EasyChair (or successor) instance and its required fields.
4. Anonymization wording, the ethics-section requirement, and any new
   generative-AI/LLM authorship policy.
5. Camera-ready deadline, allowance, and ACM rights/TAPS instructions.
6. Registration, in-person presentation obligations, and fees.

## Conflict rule

If two sources disagree, precedence order: the current edition's official site
or chairs' email > EasyChair instance instructions > the official social
account > any tracker or mirror. Record the disagreement and its resolution
date in your submission log.
