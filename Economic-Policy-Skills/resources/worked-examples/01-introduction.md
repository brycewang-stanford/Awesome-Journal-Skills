# Worked Example: Reframing an Introduction for Economic Policy

This is a fictional example. It does not describe a real paper.

## Before

> This paper studies an important topic using a large dataset and several robustness checks. We contribute to the literature by adding new evidence and showing heterogeneous effects.

## Diagnosis

- The first sentence does not name the Economic Policy audience or the journal's policy-relevant economics papers written for an expert but broad policy audience scope.
- The contribution could fit several siblings: AEJ Economic Policy, Journal of Public Economics, IMF Economic Review, and World Bank Economic Review.
- The mechanism words that matter here are absent: policy question, European policy debate, CEPR audience, welfare implication.

## After

> This paper asks how **policy question** changes the decision margin that Economic Policy readers care about. The design ties the estimate to **European policy debate**, separates the main mechanism from **CEPR audience**, and reports uncertainty in a way that makes the policy or theory implication portable beyond the study setting.
