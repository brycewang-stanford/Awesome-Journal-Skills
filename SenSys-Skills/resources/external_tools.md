## Author-side verification passes

Run these against the live pages, not from memory:

1. **Round + clock pass** — which of the two deadlines is open, whether it is round one or
   round two, and the **AoE** cutoff converted to each coauthor's local time. Confirm on the
   edition HotCRP `/deadlines` page, not the CFP prose.
2. **Format pass** — 12-page full / 6-page short body in the current ACM double-column
   template; references and appendices unlimited and outside the cap; font/geometry from the
   class file, not resized by hand.
3. **Blindness pass** — no author identity in text, PDF metadata, acknowledgments, or
   file paths; self-citations in third person; **every artifact URL blinded** (anonymized
   GitHub/GitLab/Zenodo, no lab or company domain).
4. **Resubmission pass** — if this is a second-deadline entry of a first-deadline reject,
   confirm the substantive revision and the "Response to Reviewers" mapping are attached where
   the CFP requires them.
5. **Artifact + deployment pass** — decide which of the three ACM badges to pursue and whether
   a hardware-optional evaluation path exists for AEC members without your testbed.

## Do not infer

- Do not infer 2027 dates, the second-round deadline, or notification dates from 2026.
- Do not assume the merged-venue scope, page limits, anonymity mechanics, resubmission rule,
  artifact-badge deadlines, ethics/AI-use wording, camera-ready forms, or presentation
  obligations without reopening the current pages.
- If official surfaces disagree, the newest CFP, HotCRP announcement, or a direct chair
  communication controls — record the conflict and cite the source you followed.
