# GCB Official Source Map

Primary and authoritative sources behind the *Global Change Biology* (GCB) facts injected into this
skill pack. Access date for all entries: **2026-06-01**. Re-verify volatile items (exact word /
abstract caps, accepted article types, fee / APC, data-policy wording, peer-review model) on the
official page before relying on them — journal pages change, and several Wiley pages return HTTP

  — *Global Change Biology* Author Guidelines (Wiley Online Library). Verifies: publisher = **Wiley**;
  a **data availability statement is mandatory**; **data and code must be archived in a publicly
  accessible repository with a persistent identifier (DOI)** as a condition of publication; a
  **graphical abstract is required**; abstract and keyword expectations. (Page intermittently returns

- **https://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/page/journal/13652486/homepage/productinformation.html**
  — Overview / product information. Verifies the aims-and-scope statement (impacts of, and biological

  biome; aquatic or terrestrial, managed or natural systems).
- **https://mc.manuscriptcentral.com/gcb** — **ScholarOne Manuscripts / Manuscript Central** submission
  portal for GCB. Verifies the submission system.

## Article types & length

- **https://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/10.1111/gcb.15500** — "Announcing GCB reviews" editorial.
  Verifies that **GCB Reviews are commissioned / by invitation**, while the **Research Reviews** section
  is open for unsolicited submission.
- Article types observed across the guidelines and editorials: **Primary Research Articles, Technical
  Advances, Reviews / GCB Reviews (invited), Research Reviews (open), Mini Reviews, Opinions (≈3,000–5,000
  words, multiple figures/tables), Perspectives (≈1,500 words, one figure), Research Reports**, plus
  short editorial formats (Letters, Responses, Research Highlights). 待核实 on the current full list.
- **Research-article word ceiling: up to ~15,000 words including references** per the 2026 submission
  guide rendering, with the count excluding figure legends and supplementary material. A separate
  rendering frames Research Articles at **up to ~8,000 words** for the main body (Introduction–Methods–
  Results–Discussion–Acknowledgements). These figures conflict; treat the limit as **article-type- and
  era-dependent** and confirm on the live guidelines page. 待核实.
- **Abstract: ~300 words** (one rendering states 250); **6–10 keywords**; **graphical abstract
  required**, depicting the mechanistic driver-to-response link (not a site map or phylogeny). 待核实 on
  exact abstract cap.

## Peer review & editorial policy

- **https://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/page/journal/13652486/homepage/forauthors.html** (peer-review
  section) and Wiley's general peer-review policy. Verifies: expert peer review with typically **2–3
  reviewers** for research articles; high desk-rejection rate; authors must make **data accessible to
  reviewers on request** during evaluation. The review model is generally **single-anonymous** (author
  identities visible to reviewers), consistent with Wiley ecology titles — confirm the current model on
  the live page. 待核实.

## Research data, code & open-data policy

- **https://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/page/journal/13652486/homepage/forauthors.html** (data policy
  section), aligned with Wiley's "Expects Data Sharing" tier. Verifies: **primary and secondary data
  supporting the results must be archived in a publicly accessible repository with a persistent
  identifier (e.g., DOI) before acceptance/publication**; **code, software and documentation must be
  archived in an appropriate public repository (e.g., Zenodo) with a DOI** following acceptance; a
  **data availability statement is required**, and **"available on request" is not accepted** as a
  substitute for deposit; data must carry **sufficient metadata for third-party interpretation**.
- **https://datadryad.org/** and **https://zenodo.org/** — repositories commonly used for GCB
  ecological/biogeochemical data and code (DOI-minting, persistent).
