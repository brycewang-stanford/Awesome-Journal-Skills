# External Tools - NAACL

Access date: 2026-07-08

The surfaces below are where NAACL work actually happens. Because the chapter
skips years and per-edition sites appear with little lead time, the first tool
is always the calendar check, not the style file.

## Live official surfaces

- Chapter home and news: https://naacl.org/
- Next edition site: https://2027.naacl.org/ (dates/venue TBA as of the
  access date; watch for the CFP and feeding-cycle announcement)
- Historical anchors: https://2025.naacl.org/ and https://2024.naacl.org/
- ARR portal and policy: https://aclrollingreview.org/ (CFP, dates, author
  guide)
- OpenReview: the ARR cycle groups (`aclweb.org/ACL/ARR/{year}/{month}`)
  for submission and, later, the NAACL commitment venue the edition names
- ACL Anthology NAACL record: https://aclanthology.org/venues/naacl/
- Findings volumes: https://aclanthology.org/venues/findings/
- AmericasNLP workshop series: https://turing.iimas.unam.mx/americasnlp/

## Author-side verification passes

Run these in order before an upload aimed at NAACL:

1. **Calendar pass.** Does any live ARR cycle name a NAACL edition? Record
   the answer and its date. If no, decide wait / sibling-venue / submit-and-
   hold explicitly (see `../skills/naacl-workflow/SKILL.md`).
2. **Format pass.** Rebuild with the current *ACL templates fetched fresh;
   confirm content-page budget, Limitations section presence, and that
   references and appendices sit outside the count.
3. **Compliance pass.** Responsible NLP checklist answered from the PDF's
   actual contents; preprint declaration matching reality; resubmission
   linked with a change summary if applicable.
4. **Anonymity pass.** PDF text, PDF metadata, supplement archives, and
   every linked artifact checked from a logged-out session; community and
   consultant acknowledgements held back for camera-ready.
5. **Citation pass.** Every bibliography entry resolves to a real Anthology
   page or DOI; Findings and workshop papers cited with archival status
   intact.

## Do not infer

- Do not project NAACL 2025's dates, page budgets, or commitment mechanics
  onto NAACL 2027; only the live CFP and ARR dates page govern.
- Do not assume NAACL exists in a given year; check the edition site and
  the chapter news first.
- Do not treat ARR review scores as an acceptance decision — acceptance
  happens at commitment, inside the edition's own process.
- Where official surfaces disagree, prefer the newest edition-site posting,
  then the ARR CFP/dates pages, then portal announcements; record any
  conflict in the source map.
