# Official Source Map - NAACL

Access date: 2026-07-08

This map records the official sources behind every NAACL-specific fact in this
pack. NAACL — since 2024 the **Nations of the Americas Chapter of the
Association for Computational Linguistics** — publishes per-edition sites
(`{year}.naacl.org`), reviews exclusively through ACL Rolling Review (ARR), and
does not meet every year. Both the chapter's edition calendar and ARR mechanics
change between cycles: reopen the live edition site, the ARR CFP, and the ARR
dates page before giving any deadline-sensitive advice.

## Access-method note (read first)

The network gateway used while building this pack returned HTTP 403 for direct
fetches of `naacl.org`, `2025.naacl.org`, `2027.naacl.org`,
`aclrollingreview.org`, `aclanthology.org`, and `raw.githubusercontent.com`
(the mirror route for ARR's site source). Facts were therefore verified
through **web-search renderings of the exact official URLs**, cross-checked
against each other and against secondary official surfaces: ACL Member Portal
announcements (`aclweb.org/portal`), the chapter's official conference account
posts, and the ACL Anthology venue listing as rendered in search results.
Anything that could not be pinned through at least one official-domain
rendering is marked **待核实** below. With direct access, prefer opening the
official URLs themselves and update the access dates here.

## Verified facts used in the skills

- **Name.** Since the September 2024 constitutional amendment, NAACL expands
  to the **Nations of the Americas Chapter of the ACL**; the acronym was kept.
  NAACL 2024 ran under the old "North American" name; NAACL 2025 was the
  first edition under the new one (sources 1, 2, 3, 4).
- **Edition rhythm.** NAACL does not convene in years when the ACL main
  meeting is hosted in the Americas: no NAACL 2023, and no NAACL 2026 (ACL
  2026 was in San Diego, July 2-7, 2026). The next edition is **NAACL 2027**,
  with dates and venue unannounced as of 2026-07-08 (sources 6, 7, 8).
- **Review machinery.** NAACL accepts papers exclusively through **ACL
  Rolling Review**: reviews and a meta-review are produced inside an ARR
  cycle on OpenReview; authors then **commit** the finished package to a
  NAACL edition, whose senior area chairs and program chairs decide
  acceptance into the main conference or **Findings of NAACL** (sources 3,
  9, 10).
- **NAACL 2025 timeline anchor.** ARR October 2024 submission October 15,
  2024 → reviews December 12, 2024 → final versions February 8, 2025 →
  conference April 29 - May 4, 2025, Albuquerque (source 3).
- **Format gates** (owned by ARR, not by NAACL): 8/4 content pages,
  unlimited references, mandatory Limitations section, Responsible NLP
  checklist, anonymized submissions without a preprint embargo, AoE
  deadlines (source 9).
- **Americas identity in practice.** Theme tracks "Languages of Latin
  America" (2024) and "NLP in a Multicultural World" (2025); AmericasNLP
  co-location; the renaming itself (sources 2, 4, 5, 11).

## Still cycle-volatile

Everything in the 待核实 list, plus: ARR cycle cadence and month set, page
budgets and checklist wording, preprint-declaration incentives, supplement
upload formats, venue-preference form fields, and the chapter's theme-track
choices. Treat the NAACL 2024/2025 facts above as historical anchors that
show the machine's shape — never as the next edition's rules.
