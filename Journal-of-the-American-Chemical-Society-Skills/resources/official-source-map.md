## Verified facts
- **Editor-in-Chief (verified 2026-06-23):** Erick M. Carreira, Professor of Organic Chemistry, ETH Zurich; EiC of *JACS* since 2021. (#2, #3)
- **Publisher / owner:** American Chemical Society (ACS), ACS Publications. Weekly peer-reviewed journal, established 1879. Broad chemistry scope. (#1)
- **APC / fees:** *JACS* is **hybrid** — the default subscription route carries **no required author fee**. Optional **CC-BY open access** APC is in the approximate **$5,000–$6,000** range; discounts apply under ACS read-and-publish / transformative agreements and special-country pricing. **Confirm the live figure on the ACS OA pricing page — fees change yearly.** (#1, #4)
- **ISSN:** 0002-7863 (print) / 1520-5126 (online). (#5, #6)
- **Submission portal / review model:** Submit via ACS Paragon Plus; single-blind peer review handled by the EiC and a board of associate editors. (#1)
