## Verified facts
- **Editor-in-Chief (verified 2026-06-23):** Richard Horton. Editor-in-Chief of The Lancet since 1995; active in 2026 (e.g., One Health Summit panels, signed editorials). (#1, #2, #3)
- **Publisher / owner:** Elsevier (The Lancet Group); UK-based, owned by Elsevier since 1991. (#3, #5)
- **APC / fees:** Hybrid journal — subscription route carries no author publication fee. Optional gold open access is available; Elsevier applies a personalized/Geographical Pricing for Open Access (GPOA) model for Lancet titles (papers accepted after Oct 2025), so the figure varies by country/institution/agreement and is computed during production. Reported headline OA charge is in the multi-thousand USD range — confirm the personalized figure on the live charges page rather than relying on a fixed number. (#4)
- **ISSN / portal / review model:** ISSN 0140-6736 (British edition). External peer review with editorial oversight; online manuscript submission via Elsevier's Lancet submission system. (#4, #5)
