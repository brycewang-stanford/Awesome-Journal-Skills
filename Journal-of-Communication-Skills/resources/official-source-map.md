# JoC Official Source Map

Primary and authoritative sources behind the JoC-specific facts injected into this skill pack.
Access date for all entries: **2026-06-02**. Re-verify volatile items (editors and editorial term,
exact page/abstract caps, fee/APC, open-science wording, accepted formats) on the official page before
relying on them — journal pages change.

## Official journal, submission & author pages

- **https://academic.oup.com/joc/pages/general_instructions**
  — Journal of Communication Author Guidelines / General Instructions (Oxford Academic). Verifies:
  submission via **Manuscript Central** (`mc.manuscriptcentral.com/jcom`); **double-anonymous** review,
  with all **author-identifying information removed** from the main document and supplemental materials;
  **self-citations written in the third person**; main document a **maximum of 35 pages** (including the
  **abstract, main text, references, tables, figures, and endnotes**); **abstract ≤ 150 words**; a
  separate **title page** with contact information and a brief biographical statement; **JoC Forum**
  contributions of **3,000–6,000 words**; formatting in **12-point Times New Roman, double-spaced,
  1.0-inch margins**, submitted as **Microsoft Word (.docx)** (not PDF); references in **APA 7th
  edition**; a **Data Availability Statement is required**; **Open Science Badges** are supported for
  open data, open materials, and preregistration; preregistration is **encouraged, not required** and
  should be noted in the cover letter.
- **https://academic.oup.com/joc/pages/publish-with-us**
  — Publishing with the Journal of Communication. Verifies: **flagship journal of the International
  Communication Association**; concentrates on **communication research, practice, policy, and theory**;
  publishes **original research articles and occasional special issues**; **double-anonymized** review;
  **hybrid open-access** option (authors may pay an OA fee to make the accepted manuscript freely
  available).
- **https://academic.oup.com/joc**
  — Journal of Communication home (Oxford Academic). Verifies: publisher = **Oxford University Press**;
  society = **International Communication Association (ICA)**; ISSN **0021-9916** (print) / **1460-2466**
  (online); six issues per year; **Editors-in-Chief (verified 2026-06-22): David R. Ewoldsen
  (Michigan State University), Natascha Just (University of Zurich), Chul-joo "CJ" Lee (Seoul National
  University), and Keren Tenenboim-Weinblatt (Hebrew University of Jerusalem)** — re-confirm the slate
  on the live OUP JoC Editorial_Board page, as the masthead rotates.

## Society, publisher & ICA journals

- **https://academic.oup.com/ica** — **ICA Journals on Oxford Academic** (publisher portfolio).
  Verifies: JoC is one of the ICA journals published by **Oxford University Press**.
- **https://academic.oup.com/ica/pages/publish-with-us** — Publish in an ICA Journal. Verifies:
  ICA/OUP author-facing policies and Read-and-Publish context.
- **https://www.icahdq.org/** — **International Communication Association** (society home). Verifies:
  ICA is the owning society; the world's leading association for communication research.

## Open access, charges & transparency

- **https://academic.oup.com/pages/open-research/open-access/charges-licences-and-self-archiving**
  — OUP open-access charges, licences, and self-archiving. Verifies: standard vs. open-access license
  options; APCs apply to OA articles; Read-and-Publish agreements may cover charges.
- **https://academic.oup.com/pages/open-research/open-access/charges-licences-and-self-archiving/apc-waiver-policy**
  — OUP APC waiver and discount policy. Verifies: **Research4Life (Tier 3) APC waivers** and
  discretionary waivers for authors unable to pay.
