# External Tools - The Econometrics Journal

Access date: 2026-06-01

Use these tools after reopening the current RES/OUP pages.

## Official workflow

- OUP EctJ instructions: https://academic.oup.com/ectj/pages/general_instructions
- RES EctJ page: https://res.org.uk/journals/the-econometrics-journal/
- RES submissions page: https://res.org.uk/submissions/
- Editorial Express EctJ portal: https://editorialexpress.com/cgi-bin/e-editor/e-submit_v18.cgi?dbase=ectj
- RES review process: https://res.org.uk/review-process/
- RES submission fee policy: https://res.org.uk/submission-fee-policy/
- RES replication policy: https://res.org.uk/replication-policy/

## Author-side checks

- RES/EctJ LaTeX template and separate online-appendix template.
- Summary length, printed-paper length, proof placement, empirical application, and simulation
  presentation.
- Editorial Express metadata, fee payment, cover-letter purpose, conflict or exemption notes,
  and replication restrictions disclosed at first submission.
- Acceptance-stage replication README, software versions, data documentation, code, simulations,
  seeds, and OUP Supporting Information package.

## Do not infer

- Do not infer peer-review blinding model unless the current official page states it.
- Do not reuse The Economic Journal's tiered fee policy for EctJ.
- Do not point EctJ replication packages to the Journal of Applied Econometrics Data Archive.
