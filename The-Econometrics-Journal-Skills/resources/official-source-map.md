# The Econometrics Journal — Official Source Map

Primary and authoritative sources behind every EctJ-specific fact injected into this
skill pack. **Access date for all entries: 2026-06-01.** Re-verify volatile items
(Editor-in-Chief, fee amount, page limit, policy wording) before relying on them —
journal pages change. Items not confirmed on an official page are handled in the
live-check rules below.

## Official journal & society pages

- **https://academic.oup.com/ectj/pages/general_instructions** — Oxford Academic general
  instructions. Verifies: published by **Oxford University Press on behalf of the Royal
  Economic Society (RES)**; journal **established 1998**; scope emphasises original
  contributions of direct or potential **applied value** and **leading-case
  (path-breaking)** articles rather than exhaustive treatment; **conformance-rejection
  rule** (submissions not conforming to guidelines are rejected routinely); editorial
  office contact **ectj@res.org.uk**.
- **https://res.org.uk/journals/the-econometrics-journal/** — RES journal page. Verifies:
  **Editor-in-Chief Dennis Kristensen** (appointed **13 April 2026**); publisher OUP;
  society RES; **rapid review** (screening within one week, decision target ~three
  months); established 1998. 2024 metrics appear here only as context, not
  load-bearing submission policy.
- **https://res.org.uk/submissions/** — RES submission guidance. Verifies: page limit
  **not normally exceeding 20 pages including the printed appendix**; **summary/abstract
  no more than 150 words**; PDF in journal style via the **RES/EctJ LaTeX template**;
  **empirical application required** even for theory; simulation results summarised
  **within ~one page** in the main text; **mathematical proofs in main text/printed
  appendix, not the online appendix**; cover letter only for conflicts / exemptions /
  prior reports; submission via **Editorial Express**.
- **https://editorialexpress.com/cgi-bin/e-editor/e-submit_v18.cgi?dbase=ectj** —
  EctJ EditorialExpress e-editor submission portal. Verifies: submission system is
  **Editorial Express**.
- **https://res.org.uk/review-process/** — RES review-process page. Verifies: papers
  conforming to guidelines reviewed by the **Editor-in-Chief (and possibly an Editor)**;
  within **one week** either desk-rejected or assigned to an editor; **decision within
  three months**; **resubmission normally within one month (sometimes three)**;
  replication package required **conditional on acceptance**.
- **https://res.org.uk/submission-fee-policy/** — RES submission-fee policy. Verifies:
  **flat £75 submission fee for all authors per new submission, plus VAT at 20%**; **NO
  reduced fee for RES members** (contrast: The Economic Journal has tiered member-
  discounted fees); fee introduced **spring 2024**; **non-refundable except for genuine
  payment errors**; desk rejections are not refunded.
- **https://res.org.uk/replication-policy/** — RES replication policy. Verifies:
  replication package required **conditional on acceptance**; package must contain a
  **README** describing contents and **software versions**, **data with complete variable
  documentation (ASCII files if proprietary formats are used)**, and **all code to
  reproduce tables, figures, supplements, appendices, and simulations (with seeds set)**;
  posted alongside the article as **Supporting Information**; **exemption / usage-
  restriction requests must be stated clearly at first submission** and the editorial
  board decides whether the submission can be considered; board **may refuse publication
  for non-compliance**. Cross-journal reproducibility coordination via the **Data Editors
  of the AEA, Review of Economic Studies, Canadian Journal of Economics, the Econometric
  Society, and The Economic Journal & The Econometrics Journal** (**Florian Oswald** named
  as the EJ/EctJ Data Editor in cross-journal coordination announcements; current
  dedicated EctJ data-editor page should be re-opened before naming a current person).

## Bounded live-check rules

- **Peer-review blinding model** (single-blind vs double-blind / anonymous): the official
  OUP and RES author pages document a screening + ~3-month-decision rapid-review process
  but **do not name the anonymity model**. Skills do not claim a blinding model.
- **Exact citation / reference style name** (e.g., Harvard author-date): not stated in
  plain text on the official author pages; the mandated LaTeX template presumably encodes
  it, but the style name itself is not load-bearing. Skills defer to the template.
- **Keyword count and mandatory JEL classification codes**: not explicitly required on the
  official author pages reviewed (the OUP page lists JEL categories only for browsing).
  Skills do not assert a required keyword/JEL rule.
- **Current dedicated EctJ data-editor page / name**: Florian Oswald is named as the
  Economic Journal / Econometrics Journal Data Editor in cross-journal coordination
  materials, but a standalone EctJ data-editor page was not independently re-confirmed
  this session. Re-open the current RES/OUP page before naming the person.
- **2024 metrics (IF 7.0, 5-Year 4.6, Article Influence 3.933)**: surfaced on the RES
  journal page but not central to submission norms and not independently re-verified
  against Clarivate. Context only.

## Explicitly NOT applicable (checked, to avoid misattribution)

- The **JAE Data Archive** (Journal of Applied Econometrics) is a *different* journal's
  archive and is **NOT** referenced by The Econometrics Journal. EctJ posts replication
  packages as **OUP Supporting Information** and coordinates via the **EJ/EctJ Data
  Editor**. Do not point EctJ authors to the JAE Archive.
- **The Economic Journal's tiered, member-discounted fees** do **NOT** apply to EctJ;
  EctJ charges a **flat £75 (+VAT) with no member discount**.
