# Perspectives on Psychological Science Exemplar Library

Access date for this library: **2026-06.** Verified entries were checked against the SAGE DOI page; they
illustrate the PoPS form (broad meta-science, theoretical statement, integrative review). Unfilled slots
are marked **待核实** — fill them only after checking the official archive or DOI page. **Sibling guard:**
do not import a paper from *Psychological Science*, *Psychological Bulletin*, *Annual Review of
Psychology*, or *Current Directions* and present it as a PoPS exemplar; verify the venue first.

> Why these fit the PoPS form: each carries a **superordinate message about the field** (its
> reproducibility, its incentives, its credibility) rather than reporting a single new effect — exactly
> the boundary in `ppsych-topic-selection`. The 2012 replicability special section is also a model of the
> **cooperative/special-issue** route in `ppsych-proposal-and-commissioning`.

## Sibling-venue contrast (do NOT cite as PoPS)

| Paper | Actual venue | Why it is the contrast |
|-------|--------------|------------------------|
| Simmons, Nelson & Simonsohn (2011). False-positive psychology… | **Psychological Science** (not PoPS) | a methods/empirical demonstration in the sister journal — illustrates the venue boundary |
| A formal, comprehensive meta-analysis with no superordinate message | typically **Psychological Bulletin** | belongs in the meta-analysis journal unless it carries a distinct metatheoretical message |
| An invited, exhaustive subfield survey | typically **Annual Review of Psychology** | invited specialist survey, not a submitted broad perspective |
