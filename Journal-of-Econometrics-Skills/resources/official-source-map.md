# Journal of Econometrics — Official Source Map

Primary and authoritative sources behind the JoE-specific facts injected into this skill pack.
**Access date for all entries: 2026-06-01.** Re-verify volatile items (editors, fees, page/abstract
limits, portal routing, data policy) before relying on them — journal pages change, and several
official Elsevier pages returned HTTP 403 to automated fetches (see UNVERIFIED notes).

## Official journal pages

- **https://sites.google.com/view/journalofeconometrics/guidelines** — The journal's own
  (self-administered) author guidelines. Verifies: all submissions are made through **Editorial
  Express**; the three submission categories are **Regular Issues, Annals Issues, and Themed
  Issues**; **proof of payment of the submission fee must be uploaded before a paper can be
  submitted**.
- **https://sites.google.com/view/journalofeconometrics/themes** — Themed (Theme) Issues page.
  Verifies: Themed Issues go through the regular editorial process; proposal organizers may serve as
  **Guest Associate Editors** who make recommendations to the Co-Editors.
- **https://editorialexpress.com/cgi-bin/e-editor/e-submit.cgi?dbase=je** — JoE submission portal on
  **Editorial Express** (database `je`). Verifies: main text should be **around 40 pages at least
  1.5 line spacing and 11pt font**; manuscripts must be uploaded in **PDF format only**; six-step
  submission process.
- **https://www.elsevier.com/journals/journal-of-econometrics/03044076/guide-for-authors** —
  Elsevier Guide for Authors. Verifies: **USD $75 nonrefundable submission fee** for new submissions
  and resubmissions over one year old; proof of payment must be uploaded; VAT added for European
  authors; no discounts/refunds; submissions via Editorial Express. (Page returned **HTTP 403** to
  direct fetch; fact captured from official guide content surfaced via search snippets — see
  UNVERIFIED.)
- **https://www.sciencedirect.com/journal/journal-of-econometrics/publish/guide-for-authors** —
  ScienceDirect Guide for Authors. Verifies: **abstract must not exceed 250 words** and references
  cited in the abstract must be given in full; Elsevier reference style and the **elsarticle** LaTeX
  document class; data references tagged **[dataset]** (author(s) / title / repository / version /
  year / persistent identifier); optional **Data in Brief / MethodsX** co-submission via **Editorial
  Manager**. (See UNVERIFIED: the same page references Editorial Manager for the journal's own
  submissions, which the journal's own site contradicts.)
- **https://www.elsevier.com/journals/journal-of-econometrics/0304-4076/guide-for-authors** —
  Elsevier Guide for Authors (ISSN-keyed URL). Verifies: peer-review model is **single anonymized
  review**; editors screen first, then a **minimum of two independent reviewers**; editors make the
  final decision. (Page returned **HTTP 403** to direct fetch.)

## History, scope, editors, metrics

- **https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Journal_of_Econometrics** — Verifies: publisher **Elsevier**;
  **founded 1973**; Co-Editors-in-Chief **Michael Jansson (UC Berkeley)** and **Aureo de Paula
  (University College London)**; peer-reviewed; **"Fellow of the Journal of Econometrics"**
  designation for authors who publish four or more articles in the journal. Scope covers
  econometric theory and applied methodology.

## Scope summary (used as load-bearing framing)

- **Field focus:** econometric methodology spanning theory and applied methods — identification,
  estimation, testing, decision, and prediction problems arising in economic research, plus
  applications of econometric techniques to economics. Source: scope statements on the Elsevier /
  ScienceDirect journal homepage and Wikipedia (above), accessed 2026-06-01.
