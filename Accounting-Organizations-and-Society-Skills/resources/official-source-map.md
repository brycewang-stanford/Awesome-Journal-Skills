## VERIFIED facts injected into the skills (with source #)

- **Publisher / identity:** Accounting, Organizations and Society, Elsevier Ltd (UK), ISSN 0361-3682. (#1, #5)
- **Founding:** established at Pergamon Press and launched in **1976** by **Anthony Hopwood**, who edited the journal until 2009; the founding project was to study accounting's social and organizational — not merely technical — operation. (#5, #6)
- **Editor succession:** Hopwood (to 2009) → Christopher Chapman (2010–2015) → Keith Robson (2015 onward) → the current **joint Editors-in-Chief: Marcia Annisette (Schulich School of Business, York University), Martin Messner (University of Innsbruck), Hun-Tong Tan (Nanyang Technological University)**. Mastheads change — re-verify on the ScienceDirect editorial-board page before any editor-specific step. (#5, #1, #8)
- **Scope:** the behavioral, organizational, social, institutional and political aspects of accounting — how accounting shapes and is shaped by human behaviour, organizational processes, and social/political environments. The journal publishes qualitative field studies, experimental/behavioral work, surveys, historical and critical scholarship, and theory-carrying archival work; it is not a capital-markets outlet. (#1, #5)
- **Submission system:** Editorial Manager at **editorialmanager.com/AOS**; submission proceeds fully online; the system converts uploaded source files into the single review PDF. (#3, #2)
- **Review model:** **double-anonymized** peer review — anonymized manuscript plus a separate identified title page; declarations (competing interests, generative-AI use) collected per Elsevier policy. (#2)
- **Length:** no fixed word or page cap was found in the accessible Guide for Authors excerpts; long interpretive papers are an established feature of the journal. Treat length as norm-governed and **re-check the current guide** before advising a number. (#2)
- **Open access:** hybrid journal — subscription publication is free of author charges; optional gold OA carries a journal-specific APC that Elsevier personalizes at submission (country, institution, agreements). The exact dollar figure is not published on the OA options page: confirm in the submission system before electing OA. (#4)
- **Special tracks:** the journal runs conference/special-issue submission types inside Editorial Manager; the 50-years-of-AOS anniversary conference used a dedicated "Conference submission" option with a 28 February 2026 deadline. (#7)
