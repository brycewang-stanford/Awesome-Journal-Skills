# Contribution Framing (aos-contribution-framing)

## When to trigger

- Findings are solid but the discussion section restates them instead of arguing from them
- The current contribution reads as "first evidence from setting X" or "extends literature Y"
- You cannot answer, in one sentence, what a reader of the AOS canon now understands differently
- A reviewer asks what the paper offers beyond the case / beyond the experiment

## What counts as a contribution at AOS

The currency is **conceptual**: after your paper, readers should think differently about how accounting operates in organizations or society. Empirical novelty, clean identification, or an exotic setting are inputs, not contributions. Strong AOS contribution types:

| Type                     | Shape of the claim                                                            |
|--------------------------|-------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| New concept              | Names and demonstrates a phenomenon the field lacked words for                |
| Mechanism                | Shows *how* an accounting practice produces its organizational/social effect  |
| Boundary condition       | Shows where a received account holds, fails, or reverses                      |
| Reconceptualization      | Recasts a familiar practice (budgeting, audit, risk metrics) as doing different work than assumed |
| Process evidence         | (Experimental) locates the psychological process behind a known accounting effect |
| Genealogy                | Shows how a present practice became possible and what it forecloses           |

## Build the claim from the findings

- Draft the contribution as **one falsifiable-in-spirit sentence**: "This paper shows that [accounting practice] [does what] through [mechanism/process], which [changes what] in [which conversation]."
- Tie it to the crack identified in `aos-literature-positioning`: the contribution is the answer to that specific disagreement, not a free-floating insight.
- Test transferability honestly: what about the claim travels beyond the site or the lab, and via what logic (theoretical generalization for field work; process generality for experiments)? Say so explicitly — overselling a single case is as damaging as underselling it.
- Locate the implications where AOS expects them: for **theory** first; for practice, regulation, or policy second and only as far as the evidence carries.

## The discussion section that delivers it

1. Restate the puzzle and the answer in two or three sentences — conceptual language, not variable names.
2. Spell out what each nearest prior paper must now concede or refine.
3. Develop the theoretical implications: what the lens itself gains from the case (AOS values papers that give back to the imported theory, not just borrow from it).
4. Bound the claim: scope conditions, site particularities, design limits — stated as analysis, not ritual humility.
5. Open genuine next questions the reconceptualization makes visible.

## Checklist

- [ ] Contribution stated in one sentence, conceptual in kind
- [ ] Claim answers the specific crack the front end opened
- [ ] Each nearest prior paper's required revision is named
- [ ] Transferability argued through theory/process, not asserted through sample
- [ ] The imported theory receives something back
- [ ] Practice/policy implications sized to the evidence

## Anti-patterns

- **Inventory framing**: "we contribute to three literatures" followed by lists.
- **Setting-as-payoff**: the exotic case presented as the point rather than the lens's occasion.
- **Managerialism**: converting an interpretive study into consulting advice AOS never asked for.
- **Theory borrowed, nothing returned**: heavy conceptual machinery with no reflection on what the case teaches the theory.
- **Contribution inflation**: claims the evidence register or the design cannot back.

## Output format

```
【Contribution】one sentence: practice / effect / mechanism / conversation changed ...
【Type】concept / mechanism / boundary / reconceptualization / process / genealogy
【Concessions owed】nearest paper 1 / 2 / 3 must now ...
【Give-back】what the imported theory gains ...
【Scope】what travels, through what logic; what stays local ...
【Next step】aos-tables-figures
```
