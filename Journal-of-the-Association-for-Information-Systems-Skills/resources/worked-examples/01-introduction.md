# Worked Example: Reframing an Introduction for Journal of the Association for Information Systems

This is a fictional example. It does not describe a real paper.

## Before

> This paper studies an important topic using a large dataset and several robustness checks. We contribute to the literature by adding new evidence and showing heterogeneous effects.

## Diagnosis

- The first sentence does not name the JAIS audience or the journal's information systems theory, digital innovation, sociotechnical systems, methods, and cumulative IS scholarship scope.
- The contribution could fit several siblings: MIS Quarterly, Information Systems Research, JMIS, and Management Science.
- The mechanism words that matter here are absent: sociotechnical system, IS theory, digital innovation, methodological pluralism.

## After

> This paper asks how **sociotechnical system** changes the decision margin that JAIS readers care about. The design ties the estimate to **IS theory**, separates the main mechanism from **digital innovation**, and reports uncertainty in a way that makes the policy or theory implication portable beyond the study setting.
