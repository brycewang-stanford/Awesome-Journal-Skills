# Demography Official Source Map

Primary and authoritative sources behind the Demography-specific facts injected into this skill pack.
Access date for all entries: **2026-06-01**. Re-verify volatile items (editor and editorial term,
exact word/abstract caps, fee amounts and waivers, the Subscribe-to-Open threshold and which volume
is open this year, data-policy wording, submission portal) on the official page before relying on
them — journal pages change.

## Official journal, author & submission pages (PAA)

- **https://www.populationassociation.org/demography/about/aims-scope**
  — Demography Aims & Scope. Verifies: the journal is about **how populations change, the measurement
  of population composition and change, and their components (mortality, fertility, migration)**, and
  the broader **population studies** question of why changes occur and with what consequences; draws
  on **anthropology, biology, economics, epidemiology, geography, history, psychology, public health,
  sociology, and statistics**; welcomes a **wide variety of methodological approaches** and research
  from around the globe, past to present.
- **https://www.populationassociation.org/demography/for-authors/preparing-manuscripts**
  — Preparing Manuscripts. Verifies: **Research Articles <= 8,000 words** main text (excluding
  abstract, keywords, footnotes, acknowledgments); **Research Notes <= 4,000 words**; **Commentaries
  <= 2,000 words**; **abstract <= 200 words**; **up to 5 keywords/phrases**; **3-5 highlights** (each
  <= 85 characters incl. spaces); reference style **loosely APA**; Merriam-Webster spelling;
  Research Articles **<= 8 tables/figures** (recommend **<= 75 references**); Research Notes **<= 4
  tables/figures** (recommend **<= 15 references**); Commentaries **<= 4 tables/figures**.
- **https://www.populationassociation.org/demography/for-authors/submit**
  — Submit. Verifies: **from May 31, 2024 submissions are handled in ScholarOne Manuscripts** (the
  journal moved off Editorial Manager).
- **https://www.populationassociation.org/demography/for-authors/editorial-policy**
  — Editorial Policy. Verifies: article types **Research Articles / Research Notes / Commentaries**
  (regular and invited); "**to preserve anonymity, authors and referees should avoid self-identifying
  references**"; disciplinary breadth as above.
- **https://www.populationassociation.org/demography/for-authors/review-process**
  — Review Process. Verifies: review is **double-blind** (authors are not told reviewer names and
  reviewers are not told author names); **all manuscripts pass a pre-review** with the Editor or a
  Deputy Editor; manuscripts may be **desk-rejected** when inappropriate for the journal or unlikely
  to receive favorable external reviews; **Deputy Editors** specialized in major topic areas help
  select reviewers, synthesize reviews, and recommend to the Editor.
- **https://www.populationassociation.org/demography/for-authors/ethics-disclosures**
  — Ethics & Disclosures (data availability). Verifies: **accepted manuscripts must include a data
  availability statement**; provide **URL links with persistent identifiers** (DOIs/accession
  numbers); for restricted data give a **description of access conditions**; authors are **encouraged
  to provide reproducible code** with software versions and informative comments; **Demography does
  not maintain its own repository** — authors choose a FAIR repository (guidance to FAIRsharing.org
  and re3data.org).
- **https://www.populationassociation.org/demography/for-authors/fees-funding**
  — Fees & Funding. Verifies: **$35 USD submission fee paid at submission**; **$1,000 editorial-
  management fee collected via ScholarOne at acceptance**, explicitly **not an open-access APC** and
  not to cover Duke University Press expenses (it supports PAA editorial costs); **waivers** available
  for authors without institutional or grant support (request **after acceptance**, contact the
  managing editor).

## Open-access model

- **https://www.dukeupress.edu/open-access/frequently-asked-questions-(faqs)-about-demography**
  — FAQs about Demography's funding model. Verifies: **Subscribe to Open (S2O)** business model;
  **2021, 2022, 2023 volumes remain fully open access**; the **2026 volume is open access regardless
  of author affiliation**; from the **2024 volume**, a year's content opens **if the subscription
  threshold is met by March 31**; authors may post the accepted manuscript without embargo.
- **https://dukeupress.edu/open-access/demography-open-access-funding** — Demography Open-Access
  Funding (S2O details, subscribing-institution support).
- **https://cpha.duke.edu/news-events/news/demography-population-association-americas-flagship-journal-under-auspices-duke**
  — Announcement that Demography became openly available to all (context for the OA transition).

## Publisher, owner, history

- **https://read.dukeupress.edu/demography** and **https://read.dukeupress.edu/demography/pages/About**
  — Demography on Duke University Press (publisher home, current issues, About). Verifies: published
  by **Duke University Press**; **bimonthly**; flagship journal of the **Population Association of
  America**; broad disciplinary scope.
- **https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Demography_(journal)** — Verifies: founded **1964**; flagship
  journal of the PAA; published by Duke University Press **since 2021**.
