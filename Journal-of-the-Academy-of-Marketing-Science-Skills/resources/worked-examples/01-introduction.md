# Worked Example: Reframing an Introduction for Journal of the Academy of Marketing Science

This is a fictional example. It does not describe a real paper.

## Before

> This paper studies an important topic using a large dataset and several robustness checks. We contribute to the literature by adding new evidence and showing heterogeneous effects.

## Diagnosis

- The first sentence does not name the JAMS audience or the journal's marketing strategy, consumer behavior, channels, branding, innovation, and marketing theory scope.
- The contribution could fit several siblings: Journal of Marketing, Journal of Marketing Research, Marketing Science, and Journal of Consumer Research.
- The mechanism words that matter here are absent: marketing strategy, customer response, brand mechanism, managerial relevance.

## After

> This paper asks how **marketing strategy** changes the decision margin that JAMS readers care about. The design ties the estimate to **customer response**, separates the main mechanism from **brand mechanism**, and reports uncertainty in a way that makes the policy or theory implication portable beyond the study setting.
