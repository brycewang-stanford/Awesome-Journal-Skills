# Journal of Applied Psychology Official Source Map

Primary and authoritative sources behind the Journal of Applied Psychology (JAP)-specific facts injected
into this skill pack. Access date for all entries: **2026-06** (检索于 2026-06；以官网为准). Re-check
volatile items (editor, length caps, abstract limit, exact TOP level/wording, accepted article types,
any open-access APC) on the official APA page before relying on them — journal policies change.

## Official submission & policy pages (APA)

- **https://www.apa.org/pubs/journals/apl** — Journal of Applied Psychology home (APA). Verifies:
  publisher is the **American Psychological Association (APA)**; **ISSN 0021-9010 / e-ISSN 1939-1854**;
  scope is **original investigations in applied psychology in work and organizational settings** —
  cognitive, motivational, affective, and behavioral phenomena at individual, group, organizational,
  and cultural levels across business, education, health, government, and military contexts; article
  types include **empirical investigations, meta-analyses, theory-development articles, integrative
  conceptual reviews, and qualitative research**; submission via **Editorial Manager**
  (`www.editorialmanager.com/apl/`). **Editor-in-Chief Mo Wang** (University of Florida), who **began
  the editorship in January 2026**, succeeding outgoing Editor **Lillian T. Eby** (web-verified
  2026-06-22 via UF Warrington announcement, APA new-editor-appointments page, and Wikipedia; re-verify
  before relying).
- **https://www.apa.org/pubs/journals/apl** (Submission Guidelines section) — Verifies: manuscripts
  prepared per the **Publication Manual of the APA, 7th edition**; **masked (anonymized) review** with
  author identities withheld throughout consideration; completion of the **APA-published submission
  checklist** is required at upload and signifies agreement to follow the guidelines; full submissions
  enter as **Feature Articles** with length **commensurate with contribution**, and authors may be asked
  to reduce a manuscript to a **Research Report** during review; **proposals** (where applicable) are
  prepared in APA style and limited to **1,000 words** (exclusive of title, references, figures).
- **https://www.apa.org/pubs/journals/features/apl-manuscript-checklist.pdf** — Manuscript Preparation
  Instructions / submission checklist (APA). Verifies: required preparation and masking steps; the
  checklist gating submission. Open the live PDF when preparing the final upload packet.
- **https://www.apa.org/pubs/authors/data-availability-statement** — APA data-availability statement
  guidance (APA). Background for the data-availability/transparency statement JAP expects under TOP.

## Open science (TOP framework)

- Effective **November 1, 2021**, empirical research **including meta-analyses** submitted to JAP must,
  **at a minimum, adhere to the TOP (Transparency and Openness Promotion) Levels** — data, materials,
  and analysis-code availability with persistent identifiers, a data-availability statement, and
  preregistration weighed in evaluation. Confirm the **current TOP level** and exact statement wording
  on the official APA author page.
- **https://www.cos.io/initiatives/top-guidelines** — Center for Open Science, TOP Guidelines.
  Background on the TOP framework that APA journals (including JAP) adopt.

## Owner, publisher, history

- **https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Journal_of_Applied_Psychology** — Verifies: JAP is a peer-reviewed
  academic journal published by the **American Psychological Association**, founded **1917**, and a
  leading outlet in **industrial and organizational (I-O) psychology**.

## Sibling-venue boundary (avoid mis-targeting)

- **Personnel Psychology** (Wiley) — I-O with a personnel selection/assessment/validation center of
  gravity; JAP leads with theoretical mechanism and micro/individual measurement.
- **Academy of Management Journal (AMJ)** — macro/meso management and organizational outcomes; JAP is
  micro/individual + measurement-focused.
- **Organizational Behavior and Human Decision Processes (OBHDP)** — judgment/decision processes and
  experimental micro-OB; JAP emphasizes I-O theory plus field generalizability and measurement.
- **Journal of Organizational Behavior (JOB)** — broad OB with overlapping micro topics; JAP holds a
  stronger measurement/quantitative-rigor bar.

## Bounded live-check rules

- Treat Mo Wang (University of Florida) as the Editor-in-Chief handling new submissions from January 2026,
  with Lillian T. Eby as outgoing editor (web-verified 2026-06-22 via UF Warrington announcement + APA
  new-editor-appointments page); re-open the APA masthead before naming the full term window.

  rule is that Feature Article length should be commensurate with contribution.
- Do not quote a generic APA 250-word abstract limit as a JAP-specific rule without checking the live
  journal page.
- TOP adherence and data-availability statements are load-bearing, but the exact statement template
  should be copied from the live APA page during submission.
- Do not assert a submission fee or APC in checklist logic; check APA open-access charges only if the
  author chooses that publication route.
- Do not use third-party acceptance-rate estimates as policy.
