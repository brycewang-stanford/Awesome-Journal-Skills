# External Tools - CIKM

Access date: 2026-07-08

Open these after reopening the current edition's site. CIKM hands its web presence to a
new local host every year, so the operative URLs move annually even when the series page
stays put. Direct fetches of several official domains were gateway-blocked at check time
(see the access-method note in `official-source-map.md`); verify in a normal browser.

## What to re-check on each surface

- Important dates: whether any track deadline was extended after the CFP first posted,
  and the notification / camera-ready pair for the track you are on.
- Track call: page budget, what counts inside it (CIKM 2026 counts appendices inside),
  template pointer, and the anonymity wording for that track.
- Policies page: the current GenAI Usage Disclosure requirements, the arXiv-declaration
  rule, the dual-submission ban, and the authorship-freeze wording.
- Submission hub: which EasyChair track label to pick and where the author-reviewer
  nomination field lives — a missed nomination is a stated desk-reject at CIKM 2026.
- ACM DL / dblp: proceedings lineage, edition numbering, and whether a paper you plan
  to cite as "a CIKM paper" actually appeared at CIKM rather than a sibling venue.

## Do not infer

- Do not carry CIKM 2026 dates, page budgets, track names, or the EasyChair choice into
  2027 — CIKM has changed platforms and track lineups between editions before.
- Do not assume the GenAI-disclosure section format, the reviewer-nomination rule, or
  the arXiv-declaration mechanics survive unchanged; each is one host-committee
  decision away from rewording.
- Do not treat deadline aggregators (wikicfp, paperpilot, ai-deadlines) as controlling;
  when they disagree with the host site — as they did on the 2026 full-paper date —
  the host site's important-dates page wins, and a direct read of it settles the matter.
- If the host site and the ACM DL disagree about proceedings facts, the DL record is
  authoritative for anything already published; the host site is authoritative for the
  cycle still in flight.
