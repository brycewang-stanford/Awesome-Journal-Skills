# Official Source Map - CIKM

Access date: 2026-07-08

This map records the official sources behind every CIKM-specific fact used in this pack.
CIKM is re-hosted by a different local team every year (the 2026 site lives under a
Sapienza University of Rome domain), and every edition rewrites its track lineup, page
budgets, deadlines, and policy text. Reopen the current edition's per-track calls and
its Submission Policies page before giving any deadline- or policy-sensitive advice.

**Access-method note (2026-07-08):** the network gateway of the build environment
returned HTTP 403 on direct fetches of `cikm2026.diag.uniroma1.it`, and
`dl.acm.org` / `dblp.org` pages were likewise not directly fetchable. The contents of
the URLs below were therefore verified through web-search renderings of those exact
official pages, cross-checked against the ACM Digital Library proceedings records,
dblp entries, the SIGWEB events calendar, and machine-readable deadline registries
(huggingface/ai-deadlines). Open the primary URLs directly before relying on any
specific date, page count, or fee.

## Verified 2026-cycle facts used in the skills

- CIKM 2026 is the **35th International ACM Conference on Information and Knowledge
  Management, November 7-11, 2026, Rome, Italy**, hosted on a Sapienza University of
  Rome domain (sources 1, 13).
- The 2026 submission lineup is **five proceedings tracks**: Full Research (≤10 pages
  including appendices + ≤2 reference pages), Short Research (≤4 pages), Applied
  Research (≤7 pages), Resource (≤4 pages), and Demonstration (≤4 pages) — in every
  track the **appendix counts inside the page budget**, while the GenAI Usage
  Disclosure section and references sit outside it (sources 3-7).
- 2026 timetable: full abstract **May 16**, full paper **May 23**; short/resource/demo
  abstract **May 30**, papers **June 6**; **notification August 7**; **camera-ready
  August 20**; conference November 7-11 — all AoE (source 2). As of 2026-07-08 every
  submission deadline has passed: the live cycle is in review, heading to notification.
- Submission and review run on **EasyChair**, one named track selection per paper, and
  **every track requires nominating at least one author as a reviewer in EasyChair —
  skipping the nomination is a desk reject** (sources 3, 9).
- Review is **double-blind**: anonymized PDF, third-person self-citations, and a
  mandatory declaration on the submission form if a **non-anonymized version exists
  online (e.g., arXiv)** or will appear before notification; silent non-disclosure
  risks desk rejection (sources 3, 8).
- Every submission carries a mandatory **"GenAI Usage Disclosure" section** — placed
  after the content/appendix, before the references, uncounted — declaring generative-AI
  use in the research, the code and data, and the writing; automated compliance checks
  are reserved, and reviewers are barred from AI-written reviews (source 8).
- Format is the **ACM proceedings template** (two-column `acmart`/sigconf family, per
  https://www.acm.org/publications/proceedings-template) (source 3).
- **Dual/concurrent submission to any other conference or journal is prohibited**, and
  authorship is frozen at the abstract deadline under the ACM authorship policy
  (source 8).
- CIKM proceedings are published by **ACM in the Digital Library** (CIKM '25 volume DOI
  10.1145/3746252), so acceptance leads into ACM e-rights and TAPS production
  (source 10).
- CIKM is co-sponsored by **ACM SIGIR and ACM SIGWEB**, and the venue's scope spans
  information retrieval, data mining, databases, and knowledge management — the
  tri-community identity the topic-selection skill is built around (sources 1, 13).
- Verified exemplar papers (see `exemplars/library.md`): Ferragina & Scaiella CIKM 2010
  (TAGME); Huang et al. CIKM 2013 (DSSM); Cao, Lu & Xu CIKM 2015 (GraRep); Guo, Fan,
  Ai & Croft CIKM 2016 (DRMM); Wang et al. CIKM 2018 (RippleNet, DOI
  10.1145/3269206.3271739); Sun et al. CIKM 2019 (BERT4Rec) — each pinned to a dblp
  record and edition-checked (19th/2010 Toronto, 22nd/2013, 24th/2015, 25th/2016,
  27th/2018 Torino, 28th/2019 Beijing).
