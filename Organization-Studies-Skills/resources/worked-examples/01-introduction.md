# Worked Example: Reframing an Introduction for Organization Studies

This is a fictional example. It does not describe a real paper.

## Before

> This paper studies an important topic using a large dataset and several robustness checks. We contribute to the literature by adding new evidence and showing heterogeneous effects.

## Diagnosis

- The first sentence does not name the OS audience or the journal's organization theory, institutional theory, critical management, qualitative research, and process studies scope.
- The contribution could fit several siblings: Administrative Science Quarterly, Organization Science, Journal of Management Studies, and Academy of Management Review.
- The mechanism words that matter here are absent: institutional process, qualitative depth, organization theory, critical reflexivity.

## After

> This paper asks how **institutional process** changes the decision margin that OS readers care about. The design ties the estimate to **qualitative depth**, separates the main mechanism from **organization theory**, and reports uncertainty in a way that makes the policy or theory implication portable beyond the study setting.
