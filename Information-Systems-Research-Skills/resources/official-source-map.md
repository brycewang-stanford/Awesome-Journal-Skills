## VERIFIED facts injected into the skills (with source #)

- **Publisher/cadence:** published by INFORMS (Institute for Operations Research and the Management Sciences, Catonsville, MD); **quarterly**; hosted on INFORMS PubsOnline; member of the IS field's **Senior Scholars' Basket of Eight**. (#1, #8)
- **Scope (sociotechnical, intradisciplinary):** receptive to phenomena on the **design, management, use, valuation, and impacts of IT** at **multiple levels of analysis** (individuals, groups, firms, networks, societies, nations); explicitly **sociotechnical and intradisciplinary**, integrating **behavioral, design-science, economic, and organizational** perspectives across cognitive psychology, economics, computer science, operations research, design science, organization theory/behavior, sociology, and strategic management. (#2, #8)
- **Dual co-equal genres:** ISR houses **both** rigorous behavioral/empirical sociotechnical research **and** analytical economic/econometric and design-science modeling as co-equal first-class genres; the EIC has formalized a **multimethod-research framework** (ISR 36(2), 2025). (#2, #7)
- **Length:** manuscripts **not more than 32 (8.5x11-inch) pages of text** and **no more than 38 pages including all material** (references, appendixes, tables, figures); overflow (proofs, psychometric/measurement detail) goes to an **online appendix / electronic companion** published if accepted. (#3)
- **Abstract:** **300 words maximum**. (#3)
- **Formatting:** files **double-spaced**, standard font **at least 11 points**, **one-inch margins** on all four sides; INFORMS author-date reference style. (#3)
- **Review model:** **double-anonymized (double-blind)**; a **Senior Editor** manages review, an **Associate Editor** solicits 2-3 reviewers and integrates recommendations, the Senior Editor makes the final decision after an initial editorial **fit** assessment with the EIC. (#3, #4)
- **Mandatory contribution statement:** a **~500-word contribution statement** is required in every cover letter (effective **June 1, 2023**), articulating the novel, rigorous original contribution to IS. (#3)
- **Editor:** current Editor-in-Chief **Suprateek Sarker** (McIntire School of Commerce, University of Virginia); inaugural EIC editorial ISR **34(1):1-4**, Feb 27, 2023; past EICs include Alok Gupta, Ritu Agarwal (2011-2016), V. Sambamurthy (2005-2010). (#6, #8, #9)
- **Submission system:** INFORMS ScholarOne / Manuscript Central. (#5)
- **Open access:** optional post-acceptance INFORMS **Open Option** fee of **US$3,000**. (#11)
