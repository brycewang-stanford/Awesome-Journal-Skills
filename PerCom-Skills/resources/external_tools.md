# External Tools - IEEE PerCom

Access date: 2026-07-09

Use these surfaces after reopening the current PerCom cycle pages. PerCom mechanics — deadline
dates, page budget, rebuttal window, whether an artifact/reproducibility track runs — are decided
per edition, and the `percom.org` gateway may block direct fetches (read via search renderings and
cross-check IEEE Xplore and dblp). Do not confuse PerCom with ACM **UbiComp/ISWC** and its
**IMWUT** journal — a different sponsor (ACM SIGCHI/SIGMOBILE), a rolling quarterly deadline, and a
journal publication model.

## Cross-check surfaces (when the gateway blocks the primary page)

- IEEE Xplore / IEEE Computer Society Digital Library (CSDL) PerCom and PerCom Workshops tables of
  contents for edition-to-year mapping and exemplar verification.
- dblp PerCom stream: https://dblp.org/db/conf/percom/index.html
- dblp PerCom Workshops stream for WristSense, PerVehicle, CoMoRea, DIGITA, etc.
- CFP aggregators (wikicfp, clocate) for the machine-readable deadline the CFP paraphrases —
  treat as secondary, always reconcile with the official Dates page.

## Author-side checks

- HotCRP profile, conflicts, coauthor emails, topic tags, and the mandatory **paper-registration**
  step (title/abstract/authors) that precedes the full-paper deadline by one week.
- IEEEtran compliance: `\documentclass[10pt, conference, compsocconf]{IEEEtran}`, the **9 pages
  technical content + 1 page references** budget, US-letter, two-column.
- Double-blind sweep: anonymize the PDF, the dataset/repository links, sensor-testbed and lab
  names, and cite your own prior work in the **third person** (do not omit it).
- A dataset-availability / open-data plan for the human-subjects sensing data, with IRB/ethics
  and privacy handling stated.
- Awards intent where relevant (Mark Weiser Best Paper, Best WiP) and the separate WiP/demo/
  industry/workshop deadlines.

## Do not infer

- Do not infer the rebuttal is a revise-and-resubmit: it is a **bounded, single-round** reply that
  answers explicit reviewer questions and does not expect new experiments. Papers with no positive
  review are **early-rejected** before the rebuttal opens.
- Do not assume the page budget, IEEEtran option, rebuttal length, early-reject wording, or any
  reproducibility-badge / AI-disclosure policy carry over between editions.
- Do not assume PerCom shares a calendar or model with ACM UbiComp/IMWUT; they are distinct venues.
- If pages disagree, treat the newest Call for Papers, the HotCRP deadline page, or a direct chair
  announcement as controlling, and record the conflict.
