# External Tools - ACM CoNEXT

Access date: 2026-07-09

Use these surfaces after reopening the current CoNEXT cycle pages. CoNEXT mechanics — the two
cycles' dates, page budget, one-shot revision timing, reproducibility badges — are decided per
edition, and the `conferences.sigcomm.org`/`dl.acm.org` gateway may block direct fetches (read via
search renderings and cross-check ACM DL PACMNET editorials and dblp). Do not confuse this venue
with Schneider Electric "Conext" products.

## Official workflow

- CoNEXT conference series hub: https://www.sigcomm.org/events/conext-conference
- CoNEXT 2026 home (Utrecht, Dec 7-11, 2026): https://conferences.sigcomm.org/co-next/2026/
- CoNEXT 2026 call for papers (two cycles): https://conferences.sigcomm.org/co-next/2026/#!/cfp
- CoNEXT 2025 CFP (mechanics cross-check): https://conferences.sigcomm.org/co-next/2025/#!/cfp
- PACMNET journal home: https://dl.acm.org/journal/pacmnet
- PACMNET how-to-submit: https://dl.acm.org/journal/pacmnet/how-to-submit
- ACM Artifact Review and Badging (reproducibility badges): https://www.acm.org/publications/policies/artifact-review-and-badging-current
- CoNEXT Best Paper Award: https://www.sigcomm.org/events/conext-conference/conext-best-paper-award
- CoNEXT HotCRP (per cycle, e.g. June 2025): https://conext25-june.hotcrp.com/ — replace with the
  live CoNEXT 2026 per-cycle URLs from the CFP (**待核实**).

## Cross-check surfaces (when the gateway blocks the primary page)

- ACM DL PACMNET editorials by issue (e.g. Vol 4 CoNEXT1 March 2026; Vol 3 CoNEXT4 December 2025)
  — the authoritative record of which cycle mapped to which PACMNET issue.
- dblp PACMNET stream and CoNEXT proceedings: https://dblp.org/db/conf/conext/index.html
- The RIPE RACI mailing-list re-posting of the CFP for the machine-readable two-cycle dates.
- The CoNEXT reproducibility-committee page for the badge set, opt-in deadline, and one-page
  artifact-description format.

## Author-side checks

- Cycle decision: December vs. June — pick the nearer honest deadline and confirm both cutoffs.
- HotCRP profile, conflicts, coauthor emails, topic tags, and the mandatory **paper-registration**
  step that precedes each cycle's full-paper deadline.
- ACM `acmart` template compliance and the current page budget (long ≤16 + ≤4 appendix; short
  ≤10 + ≤2 appendix; references unlimited) — **verify the 2026 numbers**.
- Double-anonymous sweep: anonymize the PDF, testbed/deployment names, repository owners, dataset
  hosts, and — critically — the one-shot major-revision response letter itself.
- Reproducibility **opt-in before the submission deadline**, then the one-page artifact description
  within a week of acceptance.

## Do not infer

- Do not infer a single deadline: CoNEXT runs **two cycles a year** (December + June). Confirm both
  on the current CFP.
- Do not assume the page budget, `acmart` variant, one-shot-revision window, badge set, or
  public-review/shepherding procedure carry over between editions.
- If pages disagree, treat the newest CoNEXT CFP, HotCRP deadline page, or a direct chair
  announcement as controlling, and record the conflict.
