# JBES Official Source Map

Primary and authoritative sources behind the JBES-specific facts injected into this skill
pack. **Access date for all entries: 2026-06-01.** JBES's own instructions-for-authors page
on tandfonline.com was Cloudflare-blocked (HTTP 403 / "Just a moment...") with no Wayback
capture during this session, so several operational specifics could not be confirmed from the
journal's own page and are marked **待核实 (to be verified)** below. Re-verify volatile items
(editors, fees, length limits, review model, exact reproducibility rules) on the live official
page before relying on them.

## Official journal / society pages

- **https://www.tandfonline.com/journals/ubes20** — JBES homepage on Taylor & Francis Online.
  Verifies: published by Taylor & Francis Group **on behalf of the American Statistical
  Association (ASA)**; scope is high-quality **methodological** contributions oriented toward
  applications in microeconomics, macroeconomics, business, and finance — including the
  development/improvement of statistical and econometric methods, the **adaptation of methods
  from machine learning and data science**, and **computational improvements** — with theory
  required to have **clear empirical relevance**; offered as **Open Select** (hybrid open
  access, optional APC). Accessed 2026-06-01.
- **https://www.tandfonline.com/action/authorSubmission?show=instructions&journalCode=ubes20**
  — Taylor & Francis author-instruction entry point for JBES. The live page (Cloudflare-blocked
  this session) is the authoritative source for submission portal, file requirements, ethics,
  data policy wording, abstract/page limits, review model, and citation style — recheck here
  before submission. Accessed 2026-06-01 (blocked).
- **https://www.amstat.org/publications/q-and-as/asa-journal-policies-on-data-sharing-and-reproducibility**
  — ASA journal policies on data sharing and reproducibility (applies to ASA-owned journals
  published with Taylor & Francis). Verifies: ASA **strongly encourages** authors to include
  data sets and code as **supplementary material** demonstrating their results, to make the
  underlying data **publicly available**, and to include a **data availability statement**;
  individual ASA journal editors may set their own (possibly stricter) policies under this
  principle; authors may request a **waiver** for confidentiality/security reasons. Accessed
  2026-06-01.

## Editors, history, identifiers

- **https://as.vanderbilt.edu/economics/atsushi-inoue-appointed-co-editor-of-prestigious-journal/**
  — Vanderbilt official announcement. Verifies: **Atsushi Inoue (Vanderbilt)** appointed
  **Co-Editor of JBES for the 2022–2024 term**, with co-editor **Ivan Canay (Northwestern)**;
  confirms the **multi-Co-Editor (no single Editor-in-Chief)** model. Accessed 2026-06-01.
- **https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Journal_of_Business_%26_Economic_Statistics** — Verifies:
  established **1983**; discipline **econometrics**; publisher **Taylor & Francis Group**;
  ISSN **0735-0015** (print) / **1537-2707** (online). NOTE: the Wikipedia infobox "editor"
  field and RePEc/IDEAS historical editor listings conflict with each other and with the
  academic record; they were treated as unreliable/stale and **NOT used** as facts in this
  pack. Accessed 2026-06-01.
