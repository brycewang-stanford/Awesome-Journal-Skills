## Verified facts encoded in the pack

- **Field / scope:** information systems research across behavioral science, design science, economics, and organizational approaches to information technology. (#10, #12)
- **Publisher / sponsor:** published by the Management Information Systems Research Center (MISRC), Carlson School of Management, University of Minnesota; official journal of the Association for Information Systems; quarterly issues in March, June, September, and December. (#10)
- **Editor-in-Chief:** Susan A. (Sue) Brown, University of Arizona; term January 2024 - December 2028. (#7, #8, #9)
- **Submission system:** ScholarOne Manuscripts is the accepted route; manuscript submitted as a Word file where required by the guide; no fee to submit. (#1, #5, #10)
- **Categories and page limits:** Research Article 50 pages, Theory Development 55 pages, Theory-Generative Research Synthesis 65 pages; Research Notes roughly half a Research Article; all maximum lengths include text, tables, figures, references, and appendices. (#3)
- **First page / keywords:** first manuscript page includes title, abstract, and keywords; keywords should be 5-10 specific terms. (#6)
- **Formatting / style:** follow MISQ Style Guide; APA 7th edition citations/references; official heading conventions in the formatting guide. (#5, #6)
- **Review model:** double-anonymous review; author/reviewer identities are not shared with reviewers; Senior Editor and Associate Editor initial review followed by external reviewers; Senior Editor owns the decision. (#2, #10)
- **Publication policies:** no reject-and-resubmit decision; work already published or under review elsewhere cannot be submitted; submissions must comply with the AIS Code of Research Conduct. (#10)
- **Research transparency:** genre-appropriate transparency policy; transparency commitment at submission; materials should document design/data/analysis and provide procedures and/or code sufficient to support replication where appropriate. (#1, #4)
- **Reciprocal service:** submitting authors agree to review up to three papers per year for the journal if invited. (#1)
- **Design-science genre:** MISQ is a canonical venue for design-science research, anchored by the 2004 Hevner et al. guidelines. (#11)
