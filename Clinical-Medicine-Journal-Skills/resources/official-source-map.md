# Official Source Map — Specialty Clinical-Medicine journals

Use these as **first-stop** source families, then drill down to the specific
journal's current instructions-for-authors page or submission system. Always prefer
the publisher's own domain or the submission system over any aggregator copy. Access
notes reflect checks made while building this bundle (2026-06); re-verify the live
page before any submission-ready advice. These are venue-fit aids only — not clinical,
diagnostic, or regulatory advice.

## Cross-cutting policy anchors (check for almost every clinical submission)

- **ICMJE** — Recommendations for the Conduct, Reporting, Editing, and Publication of
  Scholarly Work in Medical Journals: `https://www.icmje.org/`. Authorship,
  disclosures, trial registration, and overlapping-publication rules.
- **Trial registration** — prospective registration (e.g. ClinicalTrials.gov, a WHO
  primary registry) is required by ICMJE journals for clinical trials; the
  registration number must appear in the manuscript.
- **EQUATOR Network reporting guidelines** — `https://www.equator-network.org/`:
  CONSORT (RCTs), PRISMA (systematic reviews/meta-analyses), STROBE (observational),
  STARD (diagnostic accuracy), ARRIVE (animal research), and others; attach the
  completed checklist the journal requires.
- **Ethics / consent** — IRB/ethics-committee approval and informed-consent
  statements; patient-image and identifiable-data consent.

## Publisher / society author centers

- **JAMA Network** — instructions for authors for JAMA Internal Medicine, JAMA
  Oncology, JAMA Cardiology, JAMA Neurology, JAMA Pediatrics, JAMA Psychiatry, and
  JAMA Surgery at `https://jamanetwork.com/` (per-journal "Instructions for Authors").
- **The Lancet** — The Lancet Respiratory Medicine, Diabetes & Endocrinology, Public
  Health, and Psychiatry at `https://www.thelancet.com/` (information for authors);
  Elsevier Editorial Manager submission.
- **Nature Portfolio** — Nature Reviews Clinical Oncology at `https://www.nature.com/`
  (largely commissioned reviews; propose topics to the editors).
- **Elsevier** — Annals of Oncology (ESMO), Journal of Hepatology (EASL), the American
  Journal of Obstetrics and Gynecology, and European Urology (EAU) on
  `https://www.sciencedirect.com/journal/<journal>` with Guide for Authors.
- **Wiley** — Hepatology (AASLD) and Arthritis & Rheumatology (ACR) on
  `https://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/`.
- **Society / publisher direct** — American Journal of Respiratory and Critical Care
  Medicine (ATS, `https://www.atsjournals.org/`); Journal of the American Society of
  Nephrology (ASN, `https://jasn.asnjournals.org/`); Kidney International (ISN,
  Elsevier); Journal of Clinical Endocrinology and Metabolism (Endocrine Society, OUP,
  `https://academic.oup.com/jcem`); Diabetologia (EASD, Springer); American Journal of
  Gastroenterology (ACG, `https://journals.lww.com/ajg/`); Journal of Allergy and
  Clinical Immunology (AAAAI, Elsevier); Brain (Guarantors of Brain, OUP,
  `https://academic.oup.com/brain`); Stroke (AHA/ASA, `https://www.ahajournals.org/journal/str`);
  Radiology (RSNA, `https://pubs.rsna.org/journal/radiology`); Anesthesiology (ASA,
  `https://pubs.asahq.org/anesthesiology`); Critical Care Medicine (SCCM,
  `https://journals.lww.com/ccmjournal/`).

## Operational rule

1. Open the journal-specific official page above; do not infer rules from a sibling
   title in the same family.
2. Re-check article type, length/abstract limits, the applicable reporting checklist,
   trial registration, ethics/consent, data-sharing, ICMJE disclosures, preprint, and
   open-access terms.
3. If the live official instructions conflict with a skill, the official instructions
   win.
