# PiHG Submission Self-Check

A pre-submission checklist for a *Progress in Human Geography* review, intervention, or commissioned
progress report. Re-confirm every volatile item (检索于 2026-06；以官网为准) on the SAGE PiHG author
pages before relying on it. PiHG does **not** publish empirical results or detailed case studies.

## Route, strand & intake
- [ ] Route chosen: **commissioned progress report** (invited by editors) **or** **submitted review/intervention**
- [ ] For a submission, the content strand is identified (Perspectives / Reviews / Opinions / Biography / Key Publication)
- [ ] No attempt to self-submit a progress report cold (progress reports are commissioned)
- [ ] No original empirical results or detailed cases included (scope)

## Manuscript
- [ ] Anonymized manuscript prepared (double-anonymized review) + separate title page with author details
- [ ] Self-citation cues stripped from the anonymized file
- [ ] Unstructured abstract (~100–150 words — exact limit 待核实) stating purpose, argument, conclusions
- [ ] An explicit **conceptual argument / spine**, not an annotated bibliography or neutral roundup
- [ ] The close names a concrete **forward research agenda**, not "more research is needed"
- [ ] References complete and accurate (SAGE/PiHG house style applied at production)
- [ ] Within the chosen strand's word limit (exact limits 待核实 — confirm)

## Exhibits
- [ ] Debate-mapping table(s) ordered by framework cell; comparable positions only; tradition + limit noted
- [ ] Conceptual/framework figure renders the spine and is self-contained
- [ ] No original-estimate, original-data, or fieldwork exhibits
- [ ] Figure specs met; reproduced exhibits have permission + attribution

## Transparency & reflexivity
- [ ] Coverage account documented (databases, dates, terms/traditions, selection logic, saturation)
- [ ] Tradition coverage and prior PiHG progress reports accounted for
- [ ] Positionality / reflexivity stated where the standpoint shapes the appraisal
- [ ] PRISMA used only if a genuine systematic review warrants it (otherwise a narrative account)

## Declarations
- [ ] ORCID iDs supplied for authors
- [ ] COPE/ICMJE: funding stated, conflicts of interest declared, AI-tool use disclosed
- [ ] AI not listed as an author; all listed authors meet authorship criteria
