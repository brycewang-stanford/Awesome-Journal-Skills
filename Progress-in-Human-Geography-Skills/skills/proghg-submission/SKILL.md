# Submission Preflight (proghg-submission)

## When to trigger

- "Submitting tomorrow" — last check before uploading a review or progress report to the SAGE portal
- Unsure which strand/route and which files the portal expects
- Confirming abstract/word limits, ORCID, disclosures, and SAGE style compliance
- Verifying the transparency obligations for a critical review that discusses empirical work

## Process facts (检索于 2026-06；以官网为准 — re-confirm on the SAGE PiHG pages)

- **Publisher / venue.** PiHG is published by **SAGE**, founded **1977** (ISSN 0309-1325 print / 1477-0288 online), as a bimonthly **review / state-of-the-art** journal in human geography, distinct from Annals of the AAG (primary research), Transactions of the IBG (primary + some review), and Antipode (radical geography). Submission is via the SAGE **ScholarOne Manuscripts** portal (检索于 2026-06：https://mc.manuscriptcentral.com/pihg；以官网为准).
- **Does not publish empirical work.** PiHG **does not publish the results of empirical work or accounts of detailed cases** — it publishes critical reviews and conceptual interventions. A piece reporting original data/fieldwork is out of scope (检索于 2026-06；以官网为准).
- **Two routes.** (1) **Commissioned progress reports** — short, invited by the editors (you do not self-submit cold). (2) **Submitted reviews/interventions** in the journal's content strands — **Perspectives, Reviews, Opinions, Biographies, Key Publications** (检索于 2026-06；以官网为准). Choose the strand before submitting.
- **Word & abstract limits.** Limits differ by strand and are **volatile (待核实)** — confirm on the author pages. Reported guides (检索于 2026-06): Reviews ~8,000 words; Perspectives/Opinions ~3,000; Biographies/Key Publications ~5,000; progress reports short (~1,500–4,000 by report type); **unstructured abstract ~100–150 words**. Treat all as 待核实 and re-confirm.
- **Anonymized (double-blind) review.** PiHG uses **double-anonymized peer review** (检索于 2026-06；以官网为准): prepare an anonymized manuscript plus a separate title page with author details; strip identifying self-citation cues.
- **ORCID.** ORCID iDs are required/expected for authors at submission (检索于 2026-06；以官网为准).
- **Ethics & disclosures.** SAGE/PiHG follow **COPE**; authorship and disclosure follow **ICMJE**-style norms — declare funding, conflicts of interest, and any **AI-tool use**; AI may not be listed as an author (检索于 2026-06；以官网为准).
- **Transparency of the review.** A pure review reports no new data. The obligation bites on the **scholarly apparatus, positionality, and coverage account** (`proghg-transparency-and-reproducibility`) — not a replication package.
- **Open access.** SAGE offers a hybrid OA option (**Sage Choice**) with an APC; OA is not required and the APC is **volatile (待核实)** — do not assert an amount; confirm on the SAGE page.
- **Style.** Follow the **SAGE / PiHG house style** for manuscript format and references; the production editor applies house style at acceptance.

## Preflight checklist

### Route, strand & files
- [ ] Route chosen: **commissioned progress report** (invited) **or** **submitted review/intervention**
- [ ] Content strand identified (Perspectives / Reviews / Opinions / Biography / Key Publication) for a submission
- [ ] Within the strand's word limit (exact limits 待核实 — confirmed on author pages)
- [ ] Anonymized manuscript + separate title page prepared (double-anonymized review)

### Front matter
- [ ] Unstructured abstract (~100–150 words, limit 待核实) in review style — not an empirical-paper abstract
- [ ] Keywords and complete author affiliations on the title page; ORCID iDs supplied
- [ ] An explicit conceptual argument / spine, not an annotated bibliography
- [ ] The close names a concrete forward research agenda

### Declarations (incomplete without these)
- [ ] COPE/ICMJE: funding stated, conflicts of interest declared, AI-tool use disclosed (AI not an author)
- [ ] Coverage account / positionality prepared (`proghg-transparency-and-reproducibility`)
- [ ] Confirmed not under review elsewhere; no original empirical results/cases included (scope)

### Volatile re-confirms
- [ ] Strand word limits, abstract limit, current editors, portal URL, OA/APC, style details — re-checked on the SAGE PiHG pages (检索于 2026-06)

## Anti-patterns

- Submitting an empirical paper or detailed case study — PiHG does not publish empirical results
- Self-submitting a "progress report" cold — progress reports are commissioned by the editors
- Choosing the wrong strand or overrunning its word limit (different limits per strand — 待核实)
- Leaving identifying self-citation cues in a double-anonymized manuscript
- Asserting an APC, word limit, or editor name from memory rather than the live SAGE page
- Omitting COPE/ICMJE disclosures (funding, conflicts, AI-tool use)
