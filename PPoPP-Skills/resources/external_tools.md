## Author-side checks

- HotCRP profile, conflicts against **both** the PC and the **External/Extended Review Committee**,
  coauthor emails, topic tags, and the 100-400 word abstract that drives TPMS matching.
- `acmart` `sigplan` two-column template compliance, the 10-page text+figures budget with unlimited
  references, and full author lists in citations (no "et al.").
- Double-blind sweep: scrub PDF metadata; describe machines architecturally (not by named
  cluster/facility); remove SLURM accounts, grant numbers, and personal-repo links.
- Reproducibility record captured at run time: exact CPU/GPU/topology, pinning policy, seeds,
  compiler/driver versions, warm-up protocol.
- Artifact-evaluation intent — Functional/Reusable + Results Reproduced (NOT "Replicated"),
  Available via the publisher — and its separate post-acceptance deadline.

## Do not infer

- Do not infer the **number of submission rounds**: the 2027 "summer" HotCRP hints at a possible
  fall round; confirm on the current Important Dates page (待核实).
- Do not assume a **revise-and-resubmit / "Revision" decision category** exists — not confirmed for
  the 2026/2027 cycles.
- Do not assume the page budget, template revision, rebuttal length/format, artifact badge set, or
  any AI-disclosure wording carry over between editions.
- Do not state an **acceptance rate** without ACM DL / dblp confirmation for that specific edition.
- If pages disagree, treat the newest research-papers call, the HotCRP deadline page, or a direct
  chair announcement as controlling, and record the conflict.
