# Official Source Map - PPoPP

Access date: 2026-07-09

This map records the official and cross-check sources used for PPoPP-specific facts in this pack.
PPoPP is the **ACM SIGPLAN Symposium on Principles and Practice of Parallel Programming**, held
inside the co-located **HPCA/CGO/PPoPP/CC** week and sponsored by ACM SIGPLAN. Its calendar,
submission-round count, page budget, and artifact process are decided per edition and are entangled
with co-located CGO. Reopen the current-cycle call, the Important Dates page, the HotCRP deadlines
page, and the artifact-evaluation page before giving submission-ready advice.

## Access method note

## Verified 2026/2027 facts used in skills

- **PPoPP 2026 is the 31st ACM SIGPLAN Symposium on Principles and Practice of Parallel
  Programming**, held in **Sydney, Australia, 31 January - 4 February 2026**, co-located with
  **HPCA/CGO/CC 2026**. As of the 2026-07-09 access date this edition has concluded, so the live
  *next* target is PPoPP 2027 (sources 1, 2, 4).
- **PPoPP 2026 calendar:** full-paper submission **1 September 2025**; author-response period
  **27-29 October 2025**; notification **10 November 2025**; artifact submission to the AE committee
  **17 November 2025**; artifact notification **5 January 2026**; final paper **9 January 2026**
  (source 3).
- **Format:** two-column ACM Conference Format (`acmart`, `sigplan` proceedings mode), template
  `ppopp-acmart-sigplanproc-template.tex`; **10 pages** of text and figures; **references excluded
  and unlimited**; all author names spelled out (no "et al."); PDF printable on A4 and US letter;
  **abstract 100-400 words** (sources 1, 2).
- **Review is double-blind**, with a **Program Committee** plus an **External/Extended Review
  Committee (ERC)**, a scheduled **author-response (rebuttal)** period, and possible **TPMS**
  reviewer matching (sources 1, 4, 7).
- **Poster auto-consideration:** submissions not accepted for a regular presentation are
  automatically considered for the poster track, with **two-page summaries in the proceedings**
  (source 4).
- **Artifact evaluation** is post-acceptance and shared in culture with co-located CGO. PPoPP
  awards **Artifacts Evaluated - Functional OR Reusable** plus **Results Reproduced**; it
  **does not award the "Results Replicated" badge**; the **Artifacts Available** badge is granted by
  the publisher from a deposited-artifact link with **no formal audit** (source 6).
- **PPoPP 2027 is the 32nd edition**, Salt Lake City, Utah, **20-24 March 2027**, co-located with
  **HPCA/CGO/CC 2027**; a **summer-round** submission on **3 August 2026** via
  `ppopp27-summer.hotcrp.com`, author response **6-8 October 2026**, notification **26 October
  2026** (source 8).

## The co-located week (why PPoPP's calendar is not standalone)

PPoPP runs inside the **HPCA/CGO/PPoPP/CC** umbrella event, so registration, venue, artifact
timing, and even some committee logistics move with the co-located conferences (especially CGO,
with which PPoPP shares an artifact-evaluation culture). Confirm each cycle which surfaces are
run jointly before treating a PPoPP date as independent (source 11).

## No editor-in-chief; conference model

Like every conference in this collection, PPoPP has **no standing editor-in-chief**; the rotating
analogue is the per-edition General/Program Chairs appointed by ACM SIGPLAN. PPoPP papers are
conference proceedings papers (ACM DL), not journal articles; the cost model is conference
registration and at least one author presents.
