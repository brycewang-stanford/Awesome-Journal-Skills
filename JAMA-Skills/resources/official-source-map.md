## Verified facts
- **Editor-in-Chief (verified 2026-06-23):** Kirsten Bibbins-Domingo, PhD, MD, MAS (UCSF; Lee Goldman Endowed Professor; chair, Dept. of Epidemiology and Biostatistics). 17th EiC of JAMA and the JAMA Network; tenure began July 1, 2022. (#1, #2)
- **Publisher / owner:** American Medical Association (JAMA Network). (#2)
- **APC / fees:** Default route carries no author publication fee (subscription model with delayed free access). Optional open access is available; the OA fee is set by the publisher (reported around the mid-thousands USD) — confirm the exact figure on the live JAMA charges/OA page before relying on a number. (#3)
- **ISSN / portal / review model:** ISSN 0098-7484. International peer-reviewed general medical journal; online manuscript submission with external peer review and editorial oversight. (#3, #4)
