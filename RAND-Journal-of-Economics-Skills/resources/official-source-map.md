## Official journal pages (rje.org / Wiley)

- **https://www.rje.org/** — RJE homepage. Verifies: purpose/scope = "to support and encourage research in the behavior of regulated industries, the economic analysis of organizations, and more generally, applied microeconomics"; formerly the **Bell Journal of Economics**; published by **RAND in partnership with Wiley**. Accessed 2026-06-01.
- **https://www.rje.org/submissions.html** — Submissions page. Verifies: submission via **Wiley's Research Exchange portal** at `https://wiley.atyponrex.com/journal/RAND`; manuscripts screened by an assigned Editor and, if not returned, sent to **two anonymous referees** with further review by the Editor; **abstract <=100 words**; **main text <=40 pages** exclusive of appendices/references; **total <=50 pages**; files in **Word or LaTeX**; supporting information discouraged; **submission fees no longer refunded as of January 1, 2026**. Accessed 2026-06-01.
- **https://www.rje.org/styleguide.html** — RAND Journal of Economics Style Guide. Verifies: **author-date in-text citations with no page numbers** (e.g., "Jones, 1991"); references **alphabetical, unnumbered, no issue numbers** in journal cites; abstract <=100 words; total 50 / main text 40 pages; footnotes ~50 words / 3 typeset lines; **sections numbered consecutively, subsections unnumbered**; equations numbered flush right with **(1a)/(1b)** for multi-part; house usage rules (**because/as** vs *since*; **whereas/although** vs *while* outside temporal contexts; **"article"** not *"paper"*); directs authors to the **Chicago Manual of Style**. Accessed 2026-06-01.
- **https://www.rje.org/editorial-board.html** — Editorial board. Verifies: **Editor-in-Chief Craig A. Bond (RAND)**; Editors include Gary Biglaiser (UNC Chapel Hill), Joyee Deb (Yale), Ying Fan (Michigan), Alessandro Gavazza (LSE), Jean-François Houde (Wisconsin–Madison), Julian Wright (NUS), Jidong Zhou (Yale). Accessed 2026-06-01.
- **https://www.rje.org/sup-mat.html** — Supplementary / supporting material page. Verifies: supplementary material is **hosted via author-maintained links (1999–2007) or through Wiley (2007–present)**; the journal does **not** host materials directly; provision of supporting information **is not encouraged as a general rule**. Accessed 2026-06-01.
- **https://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/page/journal/17562171/homepage/forauthors.html** — Wiley "For Authors" guidelines. Verifies: **$100 submission fee per article**; as of **April 23, 2025** all new manuscripts go through the **Research Exchange** platform; abstract <=100 words; main text <=40 pages; total <=50 pages. (Page returned HTTP 402 to automated fetching; read via search snippets — see 待核实 below.) Accessed 2026-06-01.

## Scope, history, ownership

- RJE is **owned and sponsored by the RAND Corporation** (a non-university research institution), editorial office in **Santa Monica, CA**; it is the **continuation of the Bell Journal of Economics**. Source: rje.org/ homepage. Accessed 2026-06-01.
- Field focus: **applied microeconomics with an industrial-organization core** — regulated industries, antitrust/competition, regulation, market structure and firm strategy, the economic analysis of organizations, and related law-and-economics; both theoretical and empirical work welcomed; widely regarded as the field's **flagship IO journal**. Source: rje.org/ and rje.org/submissions.html. Accessed 2026-06-01.
