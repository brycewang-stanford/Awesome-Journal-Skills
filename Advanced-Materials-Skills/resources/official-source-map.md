## Verified facts

- **Publisher:** Wiley-VCH. Advanced Materials is the flagship of Wiley's "Advanced" materials-science
  portfolio (which also includes Advanced Functional Materials, Advanced Energy Materials, Advanced
  Science, Small, and Advanced Materials Interfaces). (#1, #5)
- **ISSN:** 0935-9648 (print) / 1521-4095 (online). The online ISSN `15214095` is the journal's
  Wiley URL identifier. (#4, #5)
- **Frequency:** weekly, peer-reviewed. (#1, #5)
- **Established:** 1988–1989 (originally launched in association with *Angewandte Chemie*, then
  independent). (#5)
- **Scope:** materials science across materials chemistry, physics, nanotechnology, energy materials,
  electronics/optoelectronics, ceramics, metallurgy, and biomaterials. (#1, #5)
- **Article types:** Communications, Research Articles, and Reviews/Progress Reports (and related
  formats). Communications are short (~4 typeset pages) with the opening paragraph serving as the
  abstract; Research Articles are longer (~10 typeset pages) with a short abstract (~200 words) and
  3–7 keywords. **Re-check exact current limits on #2.** (#2)
- **Color figures:** free for both online and print across article types. **Re-check on #2.** (#2)
- **Open-access APC (OnlineOpen):** on the order of **US$6,000+** per article (a search-reported figure
  near US$6,250); may be covered under Wiley transformative/DEAL agreements for eligible institutions.
  **Confirm the live figure on #3.** (#3)
- **Impact factor:** approximately **29** (2025 Journal Impact Factor, released in the 2026 Journal
  Citation Reports; earlier releases reported ~26–27). Treat as volatile. (#5, secondary metrics sources)
- **Editors:** the journal is run by a professional in-house Wiley-VCH editorial team led by named
  Editors-in-Chief (search-reported: Irem Bayindir-Buchhalter and Esther Levy). **Re-check the current
  masthead on #1/#2** — editorial staff change.
- **Submission portal:** Wiley's online submission system; Wiley has been migrating journals to the
  **Research Exchange (ReX)** platform. **Confirm the current portal on #2** before submitting.
