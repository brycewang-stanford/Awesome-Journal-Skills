# JEEA Official Source Map

Primary and authoritative sources behind every JEEA-specific fact injected into
this skill pack. **Access date for all entries: 2026-06.** JEEA is owned by the
**European Economic Association (EEA)** and published by **Oxford University Press
(OUP)**; the EEA runs membership and submission, OUP hosts and distributes the
content. Re-verify volatile items (editors, fee, policy effective dates, portal)
before relying on them — Association and publisher pages change. Items that could
not be confirmed from a primary fetch are marked **待核实 (to be verified)**.
All volatile facts should be read as **(检索于 2026-06；以官网为准)**.

## Official EEA pages (owner / membership / submission authority)

- **https://eeassoc.org/journal** — EEA's JEEA landing page. Verifies: JEEA is the
  EEA's flagship general-interest economics journal, published by OUP. Accessed 2026-06.
- **https://eeassoc.org/submit-your-paper** — EEA "Submit your paper" page.
  Verifies: the submitting author must be an EEA member to submit and to resubmit
  a revised paper; submitters are prompted to pay the applicable submission fee;
  review follows the single-blind principle; initial review by a co-editor with a
  desk-reject possibility; fast-decision target (about 8 weeks where possible).
  Accessed 2026-06.
- **https://eeassoc.org/jeea-submission-fee** — EEA JEEA submission-fee page.
  Verifies: a standard submission fee of €100 applies as of February 1, 2026;
  waived if the submitting author and all coauthors are based in low- and
  middle-income countries (World Bank classification). Accessed 2026-06.
  **待核实:** exact fee tiers / student or member variations beyond the standard
  €100 were not fully captured.
- **https://eeassoc.org/jeea-data-editor-frequently-asked-questions-faqs** — EEA
  JEEA Data Editor FAQ. Verifies: the JEEA Data Editor checks data and programs to
  confirm results replicate; an empirical paper can be formally accepted only
  after the Data Editor completes this verification. Accessed 2026-06.
- **https://eeassoc.org/news/changes-jeea-editorial-board** — EEA news on JEEA
  editorial-board changes. Verifies the editorial team changes; the current full
  roster is **待核实** (see Unverified below). Accessed 2026-06.

## Official OUP pages (publisher / hosting / author guidelines)

- **https://academic.oup.com/jeea** — JEEA journal home on Oxford Academic.
  Verifies: JEEA is published by Oxford University Press; current editorial team
  managed by Romain Wacziarg (as of 2026). Online ISSN 1542-4774. Accessed 2026-06.
- **https://academic.oup.com/jeea/pages/Author_Guidelines** — JEEA Instructions
  to Authors. Verifies (per the EEA/OUP guidance): the data-availability policy
  (papers published only if data are clearly documented and available for
  replication; authors of accepted empirical/simulation/experimental papers must
  provide data, programs, and computation details before publication). **待核实
  partial:** the page returned HTTP 403 to automated fetches on 2026-06, so exact
  word/page/abstract limits and the precise format rules could not be quoted
  verbatim; confirm length/abstract/format details directly on the live page.
  Accessed 2026-06.
- **https://academic.oup.com/jeea/pages/Editorial_Board** — JEEA editorial-board
  page on Oxford Academic. Verifies the board structure (Managing Editor,
  co-editors, Data Editor, associate editors). Accessed 2026-06. (Full roster is
  JS-rendered / volatile — **待核实**; see Unverified.)

## Data & code availability standard (DCAS)

- **https://datacodestandard.org/2024/02/01/jeea.html** — DCAS announcement that
  JEEA endorses the Data and Code Availability Standard. Verifies: JEEA endorses
  DCAS and applies it from February 1, 2024; all replication packages are posted
  publicly through JEEA's Community at Zenodo. Accessed 2026-06.
- **https://zenodo.org/communities/jeea_replication/records** — the JEEA
  replication community on Zenodo. Verifies: JEEA replication packages are posted
  to this public Zenodo community. Accessed 2026-06.
