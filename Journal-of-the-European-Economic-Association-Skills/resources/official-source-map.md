# JEEA Official Source Map

Primary official sources behind every JEEA-specific fact injected into this skill pack.
Access date for all entries: **2026-06-20**. Live-check volatile facts before submission because
Association and publisher pages can change.

## Official EEA pages

- **https://eeassoc.org/submit-your-paper** — EEA "Submit your paper" page. Confirms that JEEA invites
  submissions in all areas of economics; members submit new papers and revised manuscripts by logging
  into the member area; after payment a "Submit a paper to JEEA" link appears; papers should be in
  English; author guidelines and replication-package FAQ are linked.
- **https://eeassoc.org/jeea-submission-fee** — EEA JEEA submission-fee page. Confirms that submission
  fees apply to JEEA from **February 1, 2026**; the submitter must be an EEA member; the standard fee is
  **EUR 100**; the fee is waived when the submitting author and all coauthors are based in low- or
  middle-income countries as defined by the World Bank; the fee covers one manuscript through final
  editorial decision; submission fees are non-refundable; payment is made through the EEA membership
  profile at submission. **Re-verified 2026-06-22:** the EUR 100 fee is now in force (the Feb 1, 2026
  start date has passed); EEA-membership-of-submitter, the LMIC waiver, and non-refundability are
  unchanged. Re-confirm the live page before paying.
- **https://eeassoc.org/jeea-data-editor-frequently-asked-questions-faqs** — EEA JEEA Data Editor FAQ.
  Confirms confidential-data replication paths, including temporary access to restricted data, remote
  access to restricted data, and synthetic/simulated data for code checks when exact replication is not
  possible; confirms approved replication packages are uploaded to the JEEA Zenodo community and then
  reviewed by the Data Editor.

## Official OUP pages

- **https://academic.oup.com/jeea/pages/About** — Oxford Academic "About the Journal" page. Confirms
  the journal's stated scope: high scientific quality in theoretical and empirical economics for a
  global audience; lists OUP-hosted journal metrics, Online ISSN **1542-4774**, Print ISSN
  **1542-4766**, and copyright by the **European Economic Association**.
- **https://academic.oup.com/jeea/pages/Author_Guidelines** — JEEA Instructions to Authors. Confirms
  membership and fee rules; manuscript-submission path through the EEA members-only section; English
  papers; alt text for images, graphs, and tables; peer review by co-editor first read; rejection
  without peer review for poor fit; eight-week decision target where possible; single-blind review;
  comments policy; AI disclosure; supplementary-material and data policy; replication package
  contents; data exemptions and proprietary-data handling; pre-registration guidance; disclosure
  policy; contribution statements; open access route; final-manuscript formatting and figure rules.
- **https://academic.oup.com/jeea/pages/Editorial_Board** — JEEA Editorial Board. Confirms Managing
  Editor **Romain Wacziarg**; Co-Editors **Matthias Doepke**, **Pierre Dubois**, **Jonas Hjort**,
  **Thierry Mayer**, **Jessica Pan**, **Christopher Roth**, **Alp Simsek**, and **Vasiliki Skreta**;
  Data Editor **Maia Güell**; new Associate Editor appointments for 2026; and the Associate Editors
  section. **Re-verified 2026-06-22:** Wacziarg remains the sitting Managing Editor (OUP board page +
  the JEEA vol. 24, issue 1 masthead). The co-editor slate rotates — a 2026 search snippet listed
  Doepke, Dubois, Hjort, Mayer, Roth, Simsek, Skreta plus **Andrea Weber** (CEU); treat any single
  co-editor name as volatile and confirm on the live board page.

## Data & code availability standard

- **https://datacodestandard.org/2024/02/01/jeea.html** — DCAS announcement. Confirms that JEEA
  endorses the Data and Code Availability Standard and applies it from **February 1, 2024**.
- **https://zenodo.org/communities/jeea_replication/records** — JEEA replication community on Zenodo.
  Confirms the public community for supplementary replication material for articles published in JEEA.
