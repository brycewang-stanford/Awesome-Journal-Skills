# JEEA Pre-Submission Self-Check (8 sections)

A last-pass checklist for a *Journal of the European Economic Association* (JEEA)
manuscript before submitting via the EEA submission system. Verify volatile
specifics (fee, membership, portal, editors, policy effective dates) on the
official EEA / Oxford University Press JEEA pages (source map refreshed 2026-06-20).

## 1. Fit & contribution (beats desk rejection)
- [ ] General-interest lesson stated up front; a non-specialist sees why it matters
- [ ] Marginal contribution vs. the closest 2–3 frontier papers is explicit and defensible
- [ ] The paper is a question/result, not a method demonstration or subfield-only depth

## 2. Eligibility & fee
- [ ] Submitting author is a current European Economic Association (EEA) member
- [ ] Submission fee ready (€100, effective Feb 1, 2026) OR all coauthors based in low-/middle-income countries (exemption)
- [ ] Confirmed: required again for any resubmission of a revised paper

## 3. Format & front matter
- [ ] Manuscript follows the current JEEA author guidelines (length / abstract / format per the live OUP page)
- [ ] Title page: title, abstract, keywords, JEL codes, author affiliations
- [ ] Online appendix (if any) holds auxiliary results/proofs; main paper is self-contained

## 4. House statistical presentation
- [ ] No asterisks or boldface for statistical significance anywhere
- [ ] Standard errors / confidence sets reported beneath estimates
- [ ] Each table/figure note self-contained (sample, units, estimator, inference, N)

## 5. Identification, theory & robustness
- [ ] Empirical: design-appropriate diagnostics; heterogeneity-robust estimator where staggered timing applies
- [ ] Theory: driving assumptions named; result shown not knife-edge
- [ ] Robustness organized by threat; headline magnitude stable in sign and size

## 6. Experimental / own-data
- [ ] Detailed experimental instructions / survey transcripts included
- [ ] Own-data studies pre-registered (recognized registry); deviations reported

## 7. Reproducibility readiness (JEEA Data Editor verifies before acceptance — DCAS)
- [ ] Raw data, code, documentation assembled; one master script regenerates all results
- [ ] Software versions pinned; seeds set and reported
- [ ] Restricted data: access instructions + exemption/limited-access request stated now
- [ ] Package readied for JEEA's online data submission platform and later Zenodo community deposit

## 8. Declarations & review awareness
- [ ] Data-availability, disclosure / conflict-of-interest, and funding statements prepared
- [ ] Confirmed not under review elsewhere; AI not listed as an author
- [ ] Single-blind aware: self-citation and tone professional (referees see the authors)
