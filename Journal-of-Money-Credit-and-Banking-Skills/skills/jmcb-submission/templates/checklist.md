# Journal of Money, Credit and Banking Submission Checklist

1. Fit: the paper clearly targets monetary economics, banking, credit markets, financial intermediation, and macro-finance.
2. Sibling boundary: the cover letter explains why not Journal of Monetary Economics, Review of Economic Dynamics, Journal of Finance, and Journal of Financial Intermediation.
3. Evidence: data, model, review protocol, or qualitative evidence is auditable.
4. Exhibits: tables and figures answer the main question directly.
5. Sources: current process facts are backed by `resources/official-source-map.md` or marked 待核实.
6. Reproducibility: code, data, or review coding materials are packaged where applicable.
7. Declarations: funding, conflicts, ethics, AI use, and data availability are ready.
8. Final pass: the next action is submission, not another uncontrolled rewrite.
