## Verified facts

- **Publisher / owner:** Springer Nature, Nature Portfolio (formerly Nature Publishing Group); Nature
  Geoscience is a monthly Nature-family flagship for the Earth sciences. (#1, #2)
- **Scope:** top-quality research across all geoscience disciplines — solid Earth (tectonics,
  volcanology, geochemistry, deep interior), surface processes, hydrology and the cryosphere,
  oceanography, atmospheric science and climate (past/present/future), biogeochemistry, and planetary/
  space science; theory, modelling, and field work. Manuscripts are edited to be accessible to
  non-specialists. (#1, #2)
- **ISSN:** 1752-0894. (#2)
- **Editorial model:** handled by a dedicated team of **professional in-house editors** (no external
  editorial board), based across the UK, China, and the USA (associate editors elsewhere). The **Chief
  Editor is Rebecca Neely** — *re-check on the official site, as editors change.* (#9)
- **Content types & limits (Article):** main text up to ~3,000 words; abstract up to ~200 words,
  unreferenced (using "Here we show"); title up to ~90 characters; 4–6 display items; references
  typically up to ~50; Results/Discussion divided by 3–4 topical subheadings; an online Methods section
  divided by topical subheadings. **Brief Communication:** ~1,000–1,500 words, ~70-word/3-sentence
  abstract, up to 2 display items, up to ~20 references. **Re-check current values on the official
  pages.** (#3, #6)
- **Presubmission enquiries:** **not accepted** — novelty/importance is judged from the full paper, so
  the cover letter must carry the case. (#8)
- **Cover letter:** should explain the importance of the work and why it suits the diverse readership;
  it is **not seen by peer reviewers.** (#4, #5)
- **Peer review:** **single-blind by default**, with an **opt-in double-blind** option (anonymise the
  manuscript and enter author details only in the system). Authors may suggest and exclude reviewers. (#4)
- **Reporting standards / availability:** a Nature Portfolio **Reporting Summary** is required; **data
  availability** via community repositories where available (PANGAEA and discipline archives), otherwise
  general repositories (Figshare, Dryad, Zenodo); **code availability** required for custom code. (#7)
- **Submission portal:** Nature Portfolio **Editorial Manager** manuscript-tracking system, reached from
  the submission-guidelines pages. (#4, #5)
- **Metrics (context, volatile):** Journal Impact Factor in the ~20 range (re-check the current year on
  the official/indexing pages). (external indexing; verify)
