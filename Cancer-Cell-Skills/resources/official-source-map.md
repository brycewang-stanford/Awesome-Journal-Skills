## Verified facts
- **Editor-in-Chief (verified 2026-06-23):** Steve Mao (Editor-in-Chief, Cancer Cell; also a Cell Press Publishing Director). EiC since 2020; in-house Cell Press editor model. (#1, #2)
- **Publisher / owner:** Cell Press, an imprint of Elsevier. (#4, #5)
- **APC / fees:** Hybrid journal — subscription route carries no author publication fee (color-figure charges: ~$1,000 first figure, $275 each additional, waived under open access). Optional gold open access APC is reported at approximately USD 10,400 (excluding taxes); GPOA discounts apply for eligible countries. Confirm the live figure on the publishing-options page before relying on it. (#3, #5)
- **ISSN / portal / review model:** ISSN 1535-6108 (print), 1878-3686 (online). Submission via Cell Press Editorial Manager; external peer review managed by in-house scientific editors. (#3, #4)
