# Worked Example: Reframing an Introduction for Journal of Consumer Psychology

This is a fictional example. It does not describe a real paper.

## Before

> This paper studies an important topic using a large dataset and several robustness checks. We contribute to the literature by adding new evidence and showing heterogeneous effects.

## Diagnosis

- The first sentence does not name the JCP audience or the journal's consumer psychology, judgment and decision-making, persuasion, emotion, identity, and consumption behavior scope.
- The contribution could fit several siblings: Journal of Consumer Research, Journal of Marketing Research, Marketing Science, and Psychological Science.
- The mechanism words that matter here are absent: consumer mechanism, experimental manipulation, psychological process, moderation logic.

## After

> This paper asks how **consumer mechanism** changes the decision margin that JCP readers care about. The design ties the estimate to **experimental manipulation**, separates the main mechanism from **psychological process**, and reports uncertainty in a way that makes the policy or theory implication portable beyond the study setting.
