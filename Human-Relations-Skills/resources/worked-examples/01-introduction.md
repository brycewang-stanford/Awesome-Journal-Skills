# Worked Example: Reframing an Introduction for Human Relations

This is a fictional example. It does not describe a real paper.

## Before

> This paper studies an important topic using a large dataset and several robustness checks. We contribute to the literature by adding new evidence and showing heterogeneous effects.

## Diagnosis

- The first sentence does not name the Human Relations audience or the journal's work, employment, organizations, social relations, power, identity, and critical management scope.
- The contribution could fit several siblings: Organization Studies, Journal of Management Studies, Administrative Science Quarterly, and Work Employment and Society.
- The mechanism words that matter here are absent: workplace relation, power and identity, employment institution, qualitative insight.

## After

> This paper asks how **workplace relation** changes the decision margin that Human Relations readers care about. The design ties the estimate to **power and identity**, separates the main mechanism from **employment institution**, and reports uncertainty in a way that makes the policy or theory implication portable beyond the study setting.
