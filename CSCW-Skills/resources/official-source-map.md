# Official Source Map - CSCW

Access date: 2026-07-08

This map records the official sources behind every CSCW-specific fact in this pack.
CSCW is mid-transition from fixed submission cycles to a rolling journal-style model,
so its mechanics are changing faster than at most venues in this collection. Reopen
the current cscw.acm.org pages, the rolling CFP, and the PACMHCI track page before
giving submission-ready advice.

**Access-method note (2026-07-08):** direct fetches of `cscw.acm.org` and
`dl.acm.org` were blocked (HTTP 403) in the verification environment, so
official-page contents were read through search-engine renderings of the exact URLs
and cross-checked against the ACM Digital Library PACMHCI track listings, dblp
records, the ACM CSCW Medium blog, and the SIGCHI events pages. Before relying on
any deadline, decision category, or platform name, open the primary URLs below

## Verified facts used in the skills (as of 2026-07-08)

- CSCW is the ACM SIGCHI Conference on Computer-Supported Cooperative Work and
  Social Computing. Accepted papers are not published in a standalone proceedings
  volume: they appear in **PACMHCI (Proceedings of the ACM on Human-Computer
  Interaction)**, in the journal's CSCW track, historically as paired issues
  (e.g., Vol. 8, CSCW1 April 2024 with 209 papers; Vol. 8, CSCW2 November 2024
  with 171 papers).
- **CSCW 2026** takes place October 10-14, 2026 in Salt Lake City, Utah, USA
  (Marriott City Creek Center). CSCW 2025 was held October 18-22, 2025 in Bergen,
  Norway.
- The **May 2025 cycle** was the final fixed-deadline cycle and feeds CSCW 2026:
  submissions due May 14, 2025, 12:00 EDT on PCS; Revise-for-External-Review
  resubmissions September 16, 2025 (AoE); first-round external decisions in
  November 2025 (33.8% final decision, 66.2% Revise and Resubmit); R&R
  resubmissions January 13, 2026; final decisions by late March 2026 (152
  accepted, 44 rejected).
- As of today there is **no live fixed deadline**: CSCW 2027 and beyond runs a
  **rolling, journal-style model** — submit whenever ready via Manuscript Central
  (https://mc.manuscriptcentral.com/pacmhci), with an editorial board (EiCs Amy
  Bruckman and Eric Gilbert), per-track handling editors, decisions in roughly
  3-4 months, publication in PACMHCI, and an invitation to present at the next
  CSCW conference.
- Formatting under the fixed cycles: **single-column ACM submission template**,
  no formal length cap; papers shorter than about 5,000 words or longer than
  about 12,000 words receive added scrutiny, and papers whose length is
  incommensurate with their contribution can be desk rejected.
- Review is anonymous ("double-blind" in ACM usage): author names, affiliations,
  institution/city references, and identifying grant details must be removed
  from submissions.
- The decision vocabulary of the 2026 cycle was journal-shaped: Revise for
  External Review (internal round), Revise and Resubmit (major revision,
  re-reviewed by the same reviewers), Conditional Accept with Minor Changes,
  Accept, and Reject.
- CSCW confers a **Lasting Impact Award** on a paper 10+ years old whose
  influence persists; the recipients listed in source 10 anchor the exemplar
  library.
