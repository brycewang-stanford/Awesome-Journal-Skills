## Verified 2026-cycle facts used in the skills

- ECCV 2026 takes place September 8–13, 2026 in Malmö, Sweden (Malmö Arena and
  Malmömässan), with workshops September 8–9. Some aggregators list the end date
  as September 12; the official schedule rendering shows the 13th — reconfirm the
  exact end date on the official program.
- The 2026 deadline chain: registration February 26; paper March 5, 11pm **CET**
  (not AoE); supplementary March 12; reviews May 2; rebuttal May 11 (delivered to
  reviewers May 12); decisions June 17; camera-ready June 30 AoE, described as
  extended.
- Submissions were 14 pages in Springer LNCS single-column style **including
  figures and tables**, with extra pages allowed only for cited references; the
  template changed after ECCV 2024.
- The rebuttal was optional: one page, two-column format, references counted
  inside the page.
- Submission and review ran on OpenReview under group
  `thecvf.com/ECCV/2026/Conference`.
- Proceedings are Springer LNCS; open-access copies appear via Springer and/or
  ECVA no earlier than about four weeks before the conference; ECVA hosts the
  free mirror at ecva.net.
- Publication required at least one author's registration plus a compliant
  camera-ready.
- Reviewer-duty enforcement and the LLM review ban operated as described in
  source 5.
- ECCV is managed by ECVA, is biennial (even years), has rotating per-edition
  chairs, no standing editor-in-chief, and no article-processing charge; costs to
  authors are registration and travel.

## Cycle-volatile by construction

Deadline chain and clocks (the CET convention especially), page limits and their
counting basis, rebuttal format, reviewer-duty enforcement wording, LLM policy,
supplement mechanics, and registration pricing are re-decided every two years by
new chairs. Nothing in this pack overrides the live pages at eccv.ecva.net.
