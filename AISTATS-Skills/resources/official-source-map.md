## Verified 2026 facts used in skills

- AISTATS 2026 is the 29th International Conference on Artificial Intelligence and
  Statistics, scheduled for May 2-4, 2026 in Tangier, Morocco.
- AISTATS 2026 used OpenReview for submissions.
- The 2026 timetable listed abstract deadline October 2, 2025; full paper deadline October 9,
  2025; supplementary-material deadline October 16, 2025; reviews January 23, 2026;
  author-reviewer discussion January 23-February 5, 2026; decisions February 27, 2026;
  camera-ready March 20, 2026; all deadlines 11:59 PM AoE.
- Submission papers were limited to 8 pages excluding references and appendices; final
  camera-ready papers were limited to 9 pages excluding references and supplementary
  material.
- Review submissions had to be double-blind, with anonymized supplementary material.
- The CFP required a reproducibility checklist.
- Author-reviewer discussion was text-only and did not allow links.
- At least one author of each accepted paper had to attend in person; remote presentation was
  not offered for AISTATS 2026.
- AISTATS proceedings are published through PMLR.
