## Official submission & author pages

- **https://academic.oup.com/esr/pages/general_instructions** — ESR General Instructions (Oxford
  Academic). Verifies: submissions should usually be **around 8,000 words, including endnotes and
  references, but excluding tables and figures** (longer articles are considered, with reviewers asked
  whether the contribution justifies the length); **abstract of not more than 200 words**; **double-
  blind** peer review requiring a **completely anonymous** manuscript; authors **may cite their own work
  but must use wording that does not identify them**; author-date citation style (e.g., "(Gans, 1962)");
  a **Data Availability Statement is required** (policy from **5 September 2022**); for submissions from
  **1 January 2025**, authors using **statistical or computational methods** must deposit a
  **replication package** as a condition of publication, with **qualitative-data work exempt**.
- **https://academic.oup.com/esr/pages/submission_online** — ESR online submission. Verifies the portal:
  **ScholarOne Manuscripts** at `mc.manuscriptcentral.com/esr`.
- **https://academic.oup.com/esr/pages/why-submit** — Why submit to ESR (scope/positioning).
- **https://academic.oup.com/esr/pages/Editorial_Board** — ESR Editorial Board (current masthead).
- **https://academic.oup.com/esr/pages/About** — About ESR (aims, scope, ownership).

## Data, replication & ethics

- **ESR replication / data policy** (via the General Instructions page): **Data Availability Statement**
  required for all manuscripts from 5 Sep 2022; **replication package** required for statistical/
  computational articles for submissions from 1 Jan 2025 (deposited as a condition of publication;
  typically required at conditional acceptance); qualitative work exempt; restricted data accommodated
  via shareable code + a documented access path. Submitting to another journal while under ESR review is
  not permitted; prior appearances must be disclosed; acceptable English is the author's responsibility.
