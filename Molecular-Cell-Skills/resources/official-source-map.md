## Verified facts

- **Publisher / owner:** Molecular Cell is published by **Cell Press** (an imprint of Elsevier); it is a companion journal to *Cell*, launched in **1997**, published semimonthly. (#1, #8)
- **ISSN:** print **1097-2765**, electronic **1097-4164**. (#8)
- **Scope:** mechanistic molecular biology — gene expression, chromatin/epigenetics, RNA biology, DNA replication/repair/recombination, signaling, proteostasis, and protein structure/function, with an emphasis on new **mechanistic** insight. (#1)
- **Editor-in-Chief (verified 2026-07-16):** **Lara Szewczak** (Editor-in-Chief since November 2024), supported by an in-house Cell Press editorial team of scientific editors (e.g., Krista Bledsoe, Bryan Harada, Heather Bisbee, Demis Menolfi). Molecular Cell uses a professional in-house editor model, not an external academic EiC. Re-check the masthead before relying on names. (#4)
- **Article types:** standard **Article** (main text ~**7,000 words** including main figure legends, excluding STAR Methods/supplemental legends/references; up to ~**7** display items); **Short Article** (~**4,000 words**, a single fully validated conceptual point); plus Resource, Matters Arising, Reviews/Minireviews (Minireview ~3,000 words, ≤35 references). Confirm live caps. (#2, #3)
- **Required components:** **STAR Methods** with a mandatory **Key Resources Table** and **Resource Availability**; **Highlights** (3–4 bullets, ≤85 characters), **eTOC/In Brief blurb** (~50 words), and a single-panel **Graphical Abstract**; an unstructured **Summary** (~150 words — confirm the exact cap on #2). (#2, #5)
- **References:** **Cell Press numbered style** — superscript numbers in order of first appearance, reference list numbered by appearance, full author lists, abbreviated journal names. This is current Cell Press policy across its journals (not author–date). (#6)
- **Data & code:** deposition in approved repositories with accessions/DOIs at submission (GEO/SRA, PDB/EMDB, PRIDE/ProteomeXchange, Mendeley Data as the Elsevier default); accessions mirrored into the Key Resources Table. (#2, #5)
- **Submission / review:** via Cell Press **Editorial Manager**; **single-blind** external review managed by in-house scientific editors. (#4, #7)
