# External Tools - ACM SIGCOMM

Access date: 2026-07-09

Use these surfaces after reopening the current SIGCOMM cycle pages. SIGCOMM restates its rules
every edition, and the single annual deadline means one stale date can cost a whole year.

## Official workflow

- SIGCOMM 2026 home: https://conferences.sigcomm.org/sigcomm/2026/
- SIGCOMM 2026 Call for Papers: https://conferences.sigcomm.org/sigcomm/2026/cfp/
- SIGCOMM 2026 submission instructions: https://conferences.sigcomm.org/sigcomm/2026/submission/
- SIGCOMM 2026 HotCRP site and clock: https://sigcomm26.hotcrp.com/ and https://sigcomm26.hotcrp.com/deadlines
- SIGCOMM 2026 camera-ready instructions: https://conferences.sigcomm.org/sigcomm/2026/cameraready/
- SIGCOMM Call for Artifacts (per edition): https://conferences.sigcomm.org/sigcomm/2025/cf-artifacts/
- ACM SIGCOMM award pages: https://www.sigcomm.org/awards/test-of-time-paper-award and https://www.sigcomm.org/awards/best-paper-award

## Author-side verification passes

Run these against the live pages before every SIGCOMM upload; each is a discrete check the generic
tooling cannot make for you.

- **Clock pass.** Confirm the abstract-registration and full-paper cutoffs and their exact time-zone
  rendering. SIGCOMM 2026 wrote deadlines in AoE on the CFP and as EST cutoffs on the HotCRP page —
  never reuse a previous edition's conversion.
- **Format pass.** Check the 12-single-spaced-page pre-reference limit including figures and tables,
  ACM `acmart.cls` (submission and final), and that references plus optional appendices sit outside
  the cap. Over-length pre-reference content is desk-filtered before review.
- **Blindness pass.** Verify the PDF, plots, dataset and repository links, acknowledgments, and
  metadata carry no author or institution identity; SIGCOMM double-blind covers self-citation phrasing
  and any linked artifact.
- **Track pass.** Decide research versus experience track honestly; an operational-deployment paper
  judged on research-novelty criteria, or the reverse, wastes a full year on the single deadline.
- **Status pass.** After decisions, classify the outcome (accept / one-shot revision / early or formal
  reject) and read the specific mechanism the CFP attaches to it before drafting any response.

## Camera-ready and artifacts

- ACM TAPS workflow for the final PDF plus source; ACM template compliance; shepherd sign-off recorded
  in HotCRP, including approval that any appendix is necessary.
- Artifact-evaluation committee submission for ACM badges (Artifacts Available, Evaluated, Results
  Reproduced); public archive with a DOI for permanent retrieval.

## Do not infer

- Do not infer SIGCOMM 2027 dates, deadline, or location from the 2026 cycle.
- Do not assume the same page budget, track list, rebuttal/revision mechanics, artifact badges, or
  camera-ready toolchain without reopening the current official pages.
- If official pages disagree, treat the newest CFP, HotCRP deadline page, or a direct chair
  communication as controlling, and record the conflict.
