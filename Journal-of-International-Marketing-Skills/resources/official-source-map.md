# Journal of International Marketing — Official Source Map

Single place where the facts used across the `jim-*` skills are tied to official pages. **Checked: 2026-07-16.** Editors, limits, fees, and portal details rotate — re-verify anything volatile the week you rely on it.

## Volatile / re-check on the official site

- **Editor masthead** — Özsomer's three-year term dates from 2024; a successor search may be under way at any time. Re-check the AMA EIC page.
- **Exact review timelines and acceptance rates** — not published as stable figures; treat any number as folklore unless the editorial office states it.
- **Open-access options and APC amounts** — follow SAGE's current terms; re-check on the SAGE page rather than assuming.
- **Reference-style details** — AMA marketing-journal author–date format; confirm against the current guidelines document, not a reference manager's "AMA" (medical) preset.
- **Insight-paper scope and word cap** — re-check before choosing the format.
