# JIM Submission Preflight Checklist

Print, check, submit. Facts checked 2026-07-16 — re-verify limits and portal details on ama.org and the SAGE author instructions before use.

## Fit and framing
1. The international dimension is the theoretical core — the relabel test passes (delete country names and the pitch breaks).
2. Cross-national hypotheses were stated a priori; culture/institutions are theorized, never post-hoc.
3. The cover letter argues JIM fit and the boundary vs. JIBS / Journal of Marketing / IMR in one sentence each.
4. Managerial implications name an actor, an action, a magnitude, and the market profile where it holds.

## Evidence
5. Measurement-invariance ladder (configural → metric → scalar / partial / alignment) is tabled with decision rules.
6. Translation and back-translation protocol documented; sampling equivalence across countries stated.
7. Every claimed country difference has a formal difference test, not two separate p-values.
8. Identification for export/entry-mode or panel claims is named and its key assumption defended.

## Files (double-anonymized, two-file split)
9. Title page file: authors, affiliations, ORCIDs, correspondence, acknowledgments, funding, ethics note.
10. Main document: no identity anywhere (text, metadata, links, Web Appendix); self-citations neutralized.
11. Format: 12-pt Times New Roman, double-spaced, 1-inch margins, no page/line numbers or headers.
12. Caps: manuscript ≤ 50 formatted pages all-inclusive; abstract ≤ 200 words (third person); keywords 3–8.
13. Web Appendix files anonymized, cited in text, and clearly labeled.

## Declarations and portal
14. Conflicts, funding, human-subjects/IRB, and AI-use disclosures ready per current AMA/SAGE policy.
15. Not under review elsewhere; prior conference/working-paper versions disclosed in the cover letter.
16. ScholarOne account with ORCID linked; submit at mc.manuscriptcentral.com/ama_jim; proof PDF read fully before approval.
17. Manuscript ID logged; anticipated-objections memo started (feeds jim-review-process / jim-rebuttal).
