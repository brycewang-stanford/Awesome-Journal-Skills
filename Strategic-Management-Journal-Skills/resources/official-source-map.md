## Representative / landmark SMJ papers (verified as SMJ publications)

| Paper | Locator | Note |
|---|---|---|
| Wernerfelt, B. (1984). A resource-based view of the firm. | *SMJ*, 5(2): 171–180 | Foundational resource-based view (RBV) statement; published in SMJ |
| Teece, D. J., Pisano, G., & Shuen, A. (1997). Dynamic capabilities and strategic management. | *SMJ*, 18(7): 509–533 (doi:10.1002/smj.4250160303 is the Wernerfelt 1995 retrospective) | Canonical dynamic-capabilities paper; published in SMJ |
| Wernerfelt, B. (1995). The resource-based view of the firm: Ten years after. | *SMJ*, 16(3): 171–174 (doi:10.1002/smj.4250160303) | RBV retrospective; published in SMJ |

> Note: Barney (1991) "Firm resources and sustained competitive advantage" is frequently
> associated with strategy but was published in **Journal of Management**, not SMJ — do not
> cite it as an SMJ landmark. Use Wernerfelt (1984) and Teece, Pisano & Shuen (1997) as the
> verified SMJ exemplars of RBV and dynamic capabilities.
