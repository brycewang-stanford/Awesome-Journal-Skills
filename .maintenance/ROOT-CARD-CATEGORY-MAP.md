# AJS Root Card Category Map

Created: 2026-06-01

Purpose: continue Wave 1 of `EXPANSION-PLAN.md` by mapping root `AJS-ROOT-JOURNAL-ENTRY`
navigation cards into the 10-category expansion frame. Root cards are browsing entries only; they
do not add installable skills.

## Rules Used

- Primary category follows `EXPANSION-PLAN.md`: one primary category per venue, optional tags later.
- Agricultural/rural economics remains category 1 (Econ & Management); field agriculture journals
  would go to category 9.
- Public administration, public policy, digital government, governance, and social security go to
  category 2 (Social Sciences).
- Engineering management / systems engineering root cards go to category 7 (Engineering & Tech)
  when the venue identity is engineering-first rather than management-school-first.
- Science-policy / academy-bulletin venues are marked category 0 when the venue is broad
  multidisciplinary science policy rather than a management journal.

## English Social-Science Root Cards

Current evidence: 100 root cards point into `English-SocialScience-Journal-Skills`. For Wave 1
planning they map primarily to category 1 (Econ & Management), because the bundle is the English
econ / finance / management / accounting / marketing / OM / IS roadmap. If the terminal 1000-pack
plan later separates law/political science/psychology English depth packs, those will come from the
new social-science master list rather than these 100 root cards.
