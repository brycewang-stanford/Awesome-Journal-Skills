# Worked Example: Reframing an Introduction for Annual Review of Economics

This is a fictional example. It does not describe a real paper.

## Before

> This paper studies an important topic using a large dataset and several robustness checks. We contribute to the literature by adding new evidence and showing heterogeneous effects.

## Diagnosis

- The first sentence does not name the AREcon audience or the journal's commissioned review articles synthesizing major areas of economics for specialists and adjacent economists scope.
- The contribution could fit several siblings: Journal of Economic Literature, Journal of Economic Perspectives, Handbook chapters, and Academy of Management Annals.
- The mechanism words that matter here are absent: field synthesis, research frontier, conceptual map, evidence stocktake.

## After

> This paper asks how **field synthesis** changes the decision margin that AREcon readers care about. The design ties the estimate to **research frontier**, separates the main mechanism from **conceptual map**, and reports uncertainty in a way that makes the policy or theory implication portable beyond the study setting.
