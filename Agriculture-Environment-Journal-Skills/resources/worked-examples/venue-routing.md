# Worked Routing Examples

These examples are fictional diagnostic scenarios for venue routing. They do not
describe real submissions or publication facts.

## Case 1: Food-Systems Significance Or Field Agronomy?

Manuscript: a multi-season trial reporting yield under a new management practice,
with a possible food-security framing.

Route:
- Test `nature-food` only when the advance changes how a broad food-systems
  audience understands production, nutrition, or sustainability, not merely
  because the trial is large.
- Choose `field-crops-research` when the contribution is a crop or agronomy field
  result judged by yield, management, and replication across sites and seasons.

## Case 2: Plant Ecology Or Molecular Mechanism?

Manuscript: a physiological response with an associated gene-expression analysis.

Route:
- Choose `new-phytologist` when the claim is plant ecology or whole-plant
  physiology and the ecological or physiological insight carries it.
- Choose `the-plant-journal` when the molecular plant mechanism, the pathway, or
  the gene function is the contribution.
- If the value is descriptive natural history without a tested mechanism, neither
  flagship is the fit.

## Case 3: Fundamental Water Science Or Broad Hydrology?

Manuscript: a catchment study with a process model and a regional dataset.

Route:
- Choose `water-resources-research` when the contribution is fundamental
  water-science: a process, a theory, or a quantitative mechanism.
- Choose `journal-of-hydrology` when the value is broad hydrological application
  across methods, basins, or operational settings.

## Case 4: Long Quantitative Letter Or Short Broad-Significance Letter?

Manuscript: an Earth-science result; or a dataset intended for reuse.

Route:
- Choose `earth-and-planetary-science-letters` for a long, quantitative,
  data-rich letter establishing a result.
- Choose `geology` for a short letter whose broad significance is immediate.
- Choose `earth-system-science-data` only when the dataset itself is the
  contribution and it is FAIR-deposited in a persistent repository.
- Review venues (`annual-review-of-plant-biology`, `trends-in-food-science-and-technology`)
  are invited; confirm before routing.
