# Official Source Map — Agriculture, Environment & Earth-Science journals

Use these as **first-stop** source families, then drill down to the specific
journal's current author-instructions page or submission system. Always prefer the
publisher's own domain or the submission system over any aggregator copy. Access
notes reflect checks made while building this bundle (2026-06); re-verify the live
page before any submission-ready advice.

## Publisher / society author centers

- **Nature Portfolio** — Nature Food, Nature Plants, and Communications Earth &
  Environment author pages on `https://www.nature.com/`; Springer Nature research-data
  policy at `https://www.springernature.com/gp/authors/research-data-policy/`. Re-check
  significance statement, reporting summary, and data-availability requirements.
- **Elsevier** — New Phytologist (note: New Phytologist is published by the New
  Phytologist Foundation / Wiley — check the journal site), The Plant Journal (Wiley),
  Field Crops Research, Agriculture Ecosystems & Environment, Soil Biology and
  Biochemistry, Food Chemistry, Trends in Food Science & Technology, Environment
  International, Journal of Cleaner Production, Resources Conservation & Recycling,
  Environmental Pollution, Journal of Hydrology, Earth and Planetary Science Letters,
  Agricultural and Forest Meteorology, and Forest Ecology and Management — each on
  `https://www.sciencedirect.com/journal/<journal>` with Guide for Authors; Editorial
  Manager submission.
- **Wiley** — The Plant Journal, New Phytologist, and Global Change Biology author
  guidelines on `https://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/`.
- **Annual Reviews** — Annual Review of Plant Biology and Annual Review of Environment
  and Resources are largely invited; see `https://www.annualreviews.org/`.
- **AGU (Wiley)** — Water Resources Research and Global Biogeochemical Cycles author
  guidance via `https://www.agu.org/publications` and the AGU data-and-software policy
  (FAIR deposition in a community repository).
- **AMS** — Journal of Climate and the Bulletin of the American Meteorological Society
  author instructions at `https://www.ametsoc.org/index.cfm/ams/publications/`.
- **GSA** — Geology author instructions at `https://www.geosociety.org/`.
- **ASLO (Wiley)** — Limnology and Oceanography author guidelines on
  `https://aslopubs.onlinelibrary.wiley.com/`.
- **Copernicus** — Earth System Science Data (ESSD) at `https://www.earth-system-science-data.net/`;
  ESSD is a data journal — the dataset must be publicly deposited with a DOI and the
  paper documents it.
- **EHP** — Environmental Health Perspectives at `https://ehp.niehs.nih.gov/`.
- **Springer** — Agronomy for Sustainable Development at `https://www.springer.com/journal/13593`.

## Cross-cutting reporting / policy anchors

- **Data and code availability**: earth-science and environmental venues increasingly
  mandate FAIR data deposition in community repositories; data journals (ESSD) require
  the dataset itself to be public with a DOI. Re-check the specific policy.
- **Field / site / sampling reporting**: report sites, sampling design, replication,
  and uncertainty; many venues expect a reproducible methods/data trail.
- **Permits and ethics**: field, soil, plant, and animal sampling may require permits
  or biosafety/ethics statements — re-check.
- Competing interests, funding, author contributions, and AI-use disclosure: follow the
  current publisher policy.

## Operational rule

1. Open the journal-specific official page above; do not infer rules from a sibling
   title in the same family.
2. Re-check article type, length/figure limits, data/code deposition, field/sampling
   reporting, permits/ethics, disclosures, preprint, and open-access terms.
3. If the live official instructions conflict with a skill, the official instructions
   win.
