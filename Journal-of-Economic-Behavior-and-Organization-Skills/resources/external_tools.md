## Method notes

- Keep a reproducibility manifest even when the journal page does not require a deposit at first submission.
- For review and management journals, document coding rules, construct definitions, and inclusion/exclusion criteria with the same care as datasets.
- For empirical economics and finance journals, preserve raw-to-analysis data lineage and table-building scripts.
