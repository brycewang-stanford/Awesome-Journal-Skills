# External Tools - ASE (IEEE/ACM Automated Software Engineering)

Access date: 2026-07-09

Use these surfaces after reopening the current ASE cycle pages. ASE mechanics — deadline count,
required template (IEEE two-column vs. ACM `acmart`), page budget, revision-round rules, artifact
badges — are decided per edition, and the `conf.researchr.org` gateway may block direct fetches
(read via search renderings and cross-check dblp, ACM DL, and IEEE Xplore). Do not confuse this
venue with the *Association for Science Education* or *Association for Surgical Education* (both
"ASE"), nor with the Springer journal *Automated Software Engineering* (AUSE).

## Author-side checks

- HotCRP profile, conflicts, coauthor emails, topic/area tags, and any abstract-registration step
  that precedes the full-paper deadline.
- Template compliance: for ASE 2026, `\documentclass[sigconf,review,anonymous]{acmart}` — confirm
  the current edition's template (IEEE two-column vs. ACM `acmart`) before formatting.
- Page budget: 10 pages of content (incl. figures/tables/appendices) + 2 pages of references only;
  the mandatory Data Availability Statement after Conclusions counts inside the 10 pages.
- Double-anonymous sweep: anonymize the PDF, tool names, repository owners, and data-availability
  links; keep any rebuttal and revision summary-of-changes anonymous too.
- Artifact-evaluation intent (Available / Reusable) and its separate deadline; place a public,
  archival DOI (Zenodo / figshare / Software Heritage) post-acceptance.

## Do not infer

- Do not infer the required template: ASE's dual IEEE/ACM sponsorship means the mandated format has
  differed across editions (IEEE two-column in some years, ACM `acmart` sigconf for 2026). Confirm
  on the current research-track call.
- Do not assume the deadline count, rebuttal window, revision-round rules, artifact badges, or any
  AI-disclosure wording carry over between editions.
- Do not carry the ASE 2027 (42nd edition) host, dates, or deadlines from a same-acronym
  conference; they are **待核实** until the official ASE page posts them.
- If pages disagree, treat the newest research-track call, the HotCRP deadline page, or a direct
  chair announcement as controlling, and record the conflict.
