# Quantitative Economics Skills

Twelve agent skills for manuscripts targeted at **Quantitative Economics (QE)**, the
Econometric Society journal for quantitative theoretical and empirical economics. The pack
focuses on QE's Society-run submission process, membership and fee rules, 150-word abstract,
25-page Supplemental Appendix cap, open-access publication, disclosure rules, experimental
pre-registration, and centralized Econometric Society data/code checks.

Official basis checked on 2026-06-20: Econometric Society QE submission instructions,
editorial procedures and policies, publication-preparation instructions, data/code policy,
and Wiley journal metadata. See `resources/official-source-map.md`.

## Skills

- `qe-workflow` - route the manuscript from fit through submission and acceptance.
- `qe-topic-selection` - decide whether the paper fits QE rather than Econometrica, TE, or a field journal.
- `qe-literature-positioning` - place the paper in economics and econometrics literature.
- `qe-identification-strategy` - audit theory, assumptions, identification, and empirical strategy.
- `qe-data-analysis` - prepare quantitative evidence, simulations, experiments, and empirical work.
- `qe-tables-figures` - build exhibits that respect QE formatting norms.
- `qe-contribution-framing` - make the quantitative-economics contribution explicit.
- `qe-writing-style` - revise for QE abstract, spacing, appendix, and significance-star conventions.
- `qe-replication-and-data-policy` - prepare ES data/code materials and README.
- `qe-review-process` - plan around editorial review, desk rejection, and data-editor checks.
- `qe-submission` - preflight membership, fee, PDF, format, and disclosures.
- `qe-rebuttal` - draft response letters and revision plans.
