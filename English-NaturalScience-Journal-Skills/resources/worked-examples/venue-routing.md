# Worked Routing Examples

These examples are fictional diagnostic scenarios for venue routing. They do not
describe real submissions or publication facts.

## Case 1: Broad Discovery Or Specialist Biology?

Manuscript: a molecular mechanism with strong in-vivo validation and a possible
general disease implication.

Route:
- Test Nature/Science/Cell only if the advance changes a broad field's model,
  not merely because the data are extensive.
- Use specialist Cell Press or Nature Portfolio journals when the contribution
  is deep but field-specific.
- If the main value is a method, route to Nature Methods or a methods venue
  rather than a discovery journal.

## Case 2: Clinical Trial Versus Translational Mechanism

Manuscript: a randomized clinical trial with a clinically meaningful endpoint
and a mechanistic biomarker appendix.

Route:
- Start with NEJM/Lancet/JAMA/BMJ only if the clinical question, population,
  endpoint, and practice implications are central.
- Use specialty clinical venues when the disease-area audience is the real
  reviewer community.
- Use translational or cancer venues when the mechanism and biology, not the
  clinical-practice change, carry the claim.

## Case 3: Materials Or Chemistry Flagship?

Manuscript: a new catalyst improves performance in a benchmark reaction and
includes mechanistic spectroscopy.

Route:
- Choose JACS/Angewandte/Chem when the chemical mechanism and conceptual advance
  are central.
- Choose ACS Catalysis or related specialist venues when the contribution is
  catalyst design, reaction scope, durability, or mechanistic catalysis.
- Choose materials/energy venues when device performance, morphology, or energy
  application is the primary claim.

## Case 4: Physics, Mathematics, Or Computer Science?

Manuscript: a theorem plus numerical experiments for a computational physics
problem.

Route:
- If the theorem is the contribution, test mathematics or theoretical physics
  venues.
- If the physics result is the contribution, test physics venues and require a
  physical interpretation.
- If the algorithm and benchmarks are the contribution, route to CS/AI or
  numerical-analysis venues instead of physics prestige journals.
