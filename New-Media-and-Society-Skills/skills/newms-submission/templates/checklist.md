# New Media & Society — Submission Preflight Checklist

Copy-paste this and tick each item before uploading to **Sage Track** (ScholarOne Manuscript Central,
`mc.manuscriptcentral.com/nms`). Volatile specifics are marked **(检索于 2026-06；以官网为准)** — confirm
on the live SAGE author-instructions page, which may block automated tools; mark anything you cannot
verify **待核实**. See [`../../../resources/official-source-map.md`](../../../resources/official-source-map.md).

## Scope & fit
- [ ] The paper says something about **new media and society broadly**, not just one platform
- [ ] A **portable concept or mechanism** is named, not only a finding
- [ ] Interdisciplinary reach is explicit (a reader two fields away sees why it matters)
- [ ] Correct venue confirmed vs. siblings (Journal of Communication / Communication Research,
      Information Communication & Society, Social Media + Society)

## Length & format
- [ ] **~8,000-word target** met — *all text incl. notes, references, tables, and charts*; not more than
      a few hundred words over (检索于 2026-06；以官网为准). Word count: ______
- [ ] **Unstructured abstract ~150 words** — purpose, approach, findings (检索于 2026-06；以官网为准)
- [ ] **At least 4 keywords** after the abstract (检索于 2026-06；以官网为准)
- [ ] **SAGE Harvard referencing** — author-date in text, alphabetical list, consistent
- [ ] Figures/tables legible, self-contained, anonymized, and counted toward the word target

## Anonymous (double-anonymized) review
- [ ] Manuscript **masked** — no author names, affiliations, acknowledgments, or self-identifying phrasing
- [ ] Self-citations phrased without naming the authors
- [ ] **Separate title page** with authors, affiliations, acknowledgments, funding, corresponding-author
      contact, and word count
- [ ] Confirmed current masking format on the live page (待核实 if not)

## Research ethics & data (online/platform data)
- [ ] **Research-ethics statement** — IRB/ethics approval, consent, public/private rationale
- [ ] **Anonymization** — handles removed, verbatim quotes paraphrased to resist reverse-search, faces
      and identifying detail blurred in screenshots
- [ ] **Data collection disclosed** — API vs. scraping, time window, and platform ToS/legal constraints
- [ ] **Data-availability statement** — what is/ isn't shared and why (consistent with consent/ToS)
- [ ] Vulnerable users (minors, marginalized groups, sensitive topics) handled with extra care

## Account & portal
- [ ] **ORCID** for corresponding author, if required (待核实)
- [ ] Not under review elsewhere; not previously published (prior overlap clearly identified if any)
- [ ] Open-access / APC option understood, if relevant (待核实)
- [ ] File set assembled: anonymized manuscript, separate title page, figures, supplementary (if any)
