## VERIFIED facts injected into the skills (with source #)

- **Publisher / identity:** Oxford University Press; JCR is a multi-disciplinary journal founded 1974 whose overriding criterion is advancing understanding of consumer behavior or the conduct of consumer research, drawing on psychology, anthropology, sociology, economics, marketing, statistics, and communication. (#1)
- **Sponsoring structure:** owned by Journal of Consumer Research, Inc., a nonprofit governed by a Policy Board of representatives from ~11 sponsoring professional organizations, institutionalizing the multi-disciplinary identity. (#1)
- **Submission system:** ScholarOne Manuscripts (ManuscriptCentral) at **mc.manuscriptcentral.com/jconres**. (#2)
- **Fees:** no submission or publication fee; the only charges are optional (open-access licensing and print reproduction of color figures). (#2)
- **Length:** manuscript may not exceed **60 double-spaced pages**, inclusive of title, abstract, main text (with tables and figures embedded), appendices, and references. A web appendix (supplementary materials, max **40 MB**) is distinct from a manuscript appendix and does **not** count toward the 60-page cap. (#2, #3)
- **Abstract:** ≤ **200 words**. (#3)
- **Consumer Relevance and Contribution Statement:** ≤ **300 words**, required for new submissions. (#2, #3)
- **Review model:** **double-anonymized** (double-blind / masked); authors must fully anonymize (remove affiliations, author notes, acknowledgments, identifying URLs; use generic phrasing such as "large public university"); self-citations permitted if the cited work is publicly available. (#3)
- **Style:** Microsoft Word manuscript; follows the JCR style guide, and for matters not covered, the **Chicago Manual of Style** (e.g., title capitalization, author-date referencing). Final accepted version supplied in Word. (#2, #3)
- **Research transparency:** required **Step-6 Data Collection Statement** on all submissions (hidden from reviewers, published if accepted); posting of data/materials encouraged at initial submission and required (accessible to editors) at invited revision unless an exemption is granted; approved repositories **OSF, Harvard Dataverse, Qualitative Data Repository, ResearchBox**; statistical/programming replication code required (or a written description for proprietary code); exemptions for non-de-identifiable / restricted / proprietary / non-archivable data; data retained **≥ 7 years** after publication. (#5)
- **Editor in Chief:** Oleg Urminsky (University of Chicago Booth), term **Jan 1, 2025 – Dec 31, 2028**. (#4)
- **Expanded formats (Jan 1, 2025):** Registered Reports and Brief Commentaries in addition to the standard empirical paper. (#6)

## Items flagged approximate or 需复核 (confirm via sources above)

- **Co-editor / area-editor roster** — refreshed 2026-06-22 (time-sensitive). The editorial-leadership page and the JCR editor-announcement posts (consumerresearcher.com; corroborated by O. Verify the live co-editor list before relying on specific names.
- **Manuscript routing / desk-assignment mechanism** — 需复核. JCR uses Co-Editors who handle full manuscript portfolios plus Associate Editors and an Editorial Review Board, but the official pages reviewed do not describe a formal fixed departmental "area-editor routing" structure; the precise routing/desk-assignment mechanism was not explicitly documented in the sources accessed.
- **Dominant methodology characterization** — characterization, not quoted policy. The pairing of multi-study behavioral experiments with qualitative/CCT interpretive work is an inference from JCR's stated multi-disciplinary scope and well-known publishing patterns; JCR's official guidelines deliberately state **no single preferred method**.
- **Standalone preregistration mandate** — 需复核. No explicit standalone preregistration requirement for standard submissions was found in the research-ethics document reviewed; preregistration is associated with the optional Registered Reports format. A dedicated preregistration policy, if any, was not located on the pages accessed.
- **Impact factor / acceptance rate** — 需复核; not confirmed on an official page on 2026-06-01. Treat any single number as approximate.
