# External Tools - ATC (ACM SIGOPS Annual Technical Conference)

Access date: 2026-07-09

Use these surfaces after reopening the current ATC cycle pages. ATC 2026 is a **transition edition**
(USENIX ATC → ACM SIGOPS ATC), so its sponsor, calendar, review structure, and publisher all moved
at once; the `sigops.org`/`usenix.org` gateway may block direct fetches (read via search renderings
and cross-check the USENIX ATC '25 pages, the Wikipedia history article, ACM DL, and dblp). Do not
confuse this ATC with the *American Transplant Congress* or an *Academic Tax Conference*, and do not
confuse ATC 2026 with OSDI '26.

## Author-side checks

- HotCRP profile, conflicts, coauthor emails, and topic tags on `atc26.hotcrp.com`.
- The **paired upload**: a two-page extended abstract *and* the full paper in one submission — the
  extended abstract is review-only and must stand alone.
- Two-column format (178×229 mm text block, 10pt Times Roman, A4/US letter); page budget **≤12**
  full / **≤6** short excluding references and appendices. The exact template file (USENIX vs. ACM)
  is **待核实**.
- Double-blind sweep: anonymize the PDF, any linked repository, and system/tool names; cite your own
  prior work in the third person; no "reference removed for blind review."
- Artifact-evaluation intent (Available / Functional / Reproduced) and its separate post-acceptance
  AEC schedule.

## Do not infer

- Do not infer the exact deadline day: renderings give **1 June 2026** and **10 June 2026**;
  confirm on the SIGOPS CFP.
- Do not assume the two-round extended-abstract model, the Deployed Systems Track, the template
  file, or the APC terms carry over between editions — all moved in the transition.
- Do not assume USENIX's free-video / no-APC model persists; the 2026 edition uses ACM Open Access
  with a subsidized APC.
- If pages disagree, treat the newest SIGOPS call, the ATC HotCRP deadline page, or a direct chair
  announcement as controlling, and record the conflict.
