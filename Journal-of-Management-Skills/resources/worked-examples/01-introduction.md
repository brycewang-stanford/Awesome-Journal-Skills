# Worked Example: Reframing an Introduction for Journal of Management

This is a fictional example. It does not describe a real paper.

## Before

> This paper studies an important topic using a large dataset and several robustness checks. We contribute to the literature by adding new evidence and showing heterogeneous effects.

## Diagnosis

- The first sentence does not name the JOMgmt audience or the journal's management theory and empirical work across organizational behavior, strategy, HR, entrepreneurship, and research methods scope.
- The contribution could fit several siblings: Academy of Management Journal, Strategic Management Journal, Organization Science, and Journal of Management Studies.
- The mechanism words that matter here are absent: management theory contribution, multi-study design, construct validity, organizational mechanism.

## After

> This paper asks how **management theory contribution** changes the decision margin that JOMgmt readers care about. The design ties the estimate to **multi-study design**, separates the main mechanism from **construct validity**, and reports uncertainty in a way that makes the policy or theory implication portable beyond the study setting.
