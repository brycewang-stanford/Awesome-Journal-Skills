# External Tools - EDBT

Access date: 2026-07-09

Use these surfaces after reopening the current EDBT cycle pages. EDBT mechanics — the number of
cycles, per-cycle dates, page budgets per paper shape, and reproducibility handling — are decided
per edition, and the `edbticdt*.github.io`/`edbt.org`/`openproceedings.org` gateway may block direct
fetches (read via search renderings and cross-check OpenProceedings, dblp, and WikiCFP). EDBT is a
databases conference; do not confuse it with similarly abbreviated non-database venues.

## Official workflow

- EDBT Association / series hub: https://edbt.org/
- EDBT/ICDT 2026 Joint Conference (Tampere, 24-27 Mar 2026): https://edbticdt2026.github.io/
- EDBT 2026 research-track call: https://edbticdt2026.github.io/?contents=EDBT_CFP.html
- EDBT 2026 industrial & application call: https://edbticdt2026.github.io/?contents=call_for_IAP.html
- OpenProceedings.org home (open-access platform founded by ICDT and EDBT): https://openproceedings.org/
- OpenProceedings EDBT 2026 volume (29th edition): https://openproceedings.org/html/pages/2026_edbt.html
- Microsoft CMT (Conference Management Toolkit — the review portal): https://cmt3.research.microsoft.com/
- Co-located ICDT call (the theory boundary): https://databasetheory.org/ and https://easychair.org/cfp/ICDT2026

## Cross-check surfaces (when the gateway blocks the primary page)

- OpenProceedings EDBT volume tables of contents (per year) for accepted-paper verification and the
  ISBN/CC-BY-NC-ND license of the volume.
- dblp EDBT stream: https://dblp.org/db/conf/edbt/index.html (edition numbers, proceedings, authors).
- WikiCFP EDBT program: http://www.wikicfp.com/cfp/program?id=830 (cycle deadlines across editions).
- The EDBT Association page for the standing description of the three-cycle model, the CMT review
  process, and the 12-month resubmission ban.
- CEUR-WS for the co-located EDBT/ICDT workshop proceedings (distinct from the OpenProceedings main
  proceedings).

## Author-side checks

- Microsoft CMT profile, conflicts, coauthor emails, subject-area tags, and the chosen **cycle**
  and **paper shape** (Regular / Experiments & Analysis / Vision) for the current call.
- Host/OpenProceedings LaTeX template compliance and the current page budget per shape (EDBT 2022:
  Regular & Experiments-&-Analysis ≤12 pages + unlimited references; Vision ≤6 pages + unlimited
  references — reverify per cycle).
- Resubmission-ban check: has this work, in this paper format, been rejected or withdrawn from an
  EDBT track within the last 12 months?
- A runnable artifact and a data/availability story consistent with the paper; pin workloads,
  dataset versions, and hardware now.
- Whether reviewing this cycle is single- or double-blind, and anonymize accordingly if required
  (**待核实**; confirm on the current call).

## Do not infer

- Do not infer the number of cycles or any per-cycle date: EDBT runs three cycles since 2022, but
  the exact author-feedback and revision windows are set per edition. Confirm on the current host
  site.
- Do not assume the page budget, template revision, blind policy, paper-shape set, or
  reproducibility/badge handling carry over between editions.
- Do not assume the publication venue is a paywalled DL: EDBT publishes open access on
  OpenProceedings.org under CC-BY-NC-ND, with copyright retained by authors.
- If pages disagree, treat the newest host-site research call, the EDBT Association page, or a
  direct chair announcement as controlling, and record the conflict.
