# Official Source Map — Engineering & Technology journals

Use these as **first-stop** source families, then drill down to the specific
journal's current author-instructions page or submission system. Always prefer the
publisher's own domain or the submission system over any aggregator copy. Access
notes reflect checks made while building this bundle (2026-06); re-verify the live
page before any submission-ready advice.

## Publisher / society author centers

- **IEEE** — IEEE Author Center (journals): `https://journals.ieeeauthorcenter.ieee.org/`
  and per-journal pages under `https://www.ieee.org/` and IEEE Xplore. Most IEEE
  Transactions use ScholarOne Manuscripts. Re-check article types (Regular Paper /
  Communication / Letter / Survey), the double-column template, graphical/overview
  requirements, and IEEE's open-access and reproducibility/code policies.
- **IFAC / Automatica (Elsevier)** — `https://www.journals.elsevier.com/automatica`
  and the IFAC author guidance; Editorial Manager submission.
- **Optica Publishing Group** — author resources at
  `https://opg.optica.org/submit/` for Optica; re-check length, supplementary, and
  data-availability rules.
- **Nature Portfolio** — Nature Electronics and Nature Biomedical Engineering author
  pages on `https://www.nature.com/`; Springer Nature research-data policy at
  `https://www.springernature.com/gp/authors/research-data-policy/`.
- **Wiley** — Light: Science & Applications and Laser & Photonics Reviews author
  guidelines on `https://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/`; Light: Science & Applications is
  Nature Portfolio / CIOMP — check the nature.com page for that title.
- **Elsevier** — JMPS, International Journal of Plasticity, International Journal of
  Machine Tools and Manufacture, Additive Manufacturing, Composites Part B,
  Progress in Aerospace Sciences, Chemical Engineering Journal, Progress in Energy
  and Combustion Science, Journal of Membrane Science, Applied Energy, Journal of
  Power Sources, Energy Storage Materials, Acta Materialia, Progress in Materials
  Science, Transportation Research Part B, Cement and Concrete Research — each on
  `https://www.sciencedirect.com/journal/<journal>` with Guide for Authors;
  Editorial Manager submission.
- **Cambridge University Press** — Journal of Fluid Mechanics author instructions at
  `https://www.cambridge.org/core/journals/journal-of-fluid-mechanics`.
- **AIChE / Wiley** — AIChE Journal author guidelines on
  `https://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/journal/15475905`.
- **SAGE** — The International Journal of Robotics Research author guidelines on
  `https://journals.sagepub.com/home/ijr`.

## Cross-cutting reporting / policy anchors

- Data and code availability: most engineering venues now expect a data-availability
  statement and, increasingly, code/artifact sharing — re-check the journal page.
- Human-subjects / clinical engineering work (medical imaging, biomedical
  engineering, biomaterials): re-check ethics/IRB, consent, and any applicable
  reporting guideline.
- Competing interests, funding, author contributions, and AI-use disclosure: follow
  the current publisher policy.

## Operational rule

1. Open the journal-specific official page above; do not infer rules from a sibling
   title in the same family.
2. Re-check article type, length/figure limits, template, reporting/data/code
   requirements, ethics, disclosures, preprint, and open-access terms.
3. If the live official instructions conflict with a skill, the official
   instructions win.
