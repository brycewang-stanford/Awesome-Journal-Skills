# Venue-Selection Patterns

These patterns guide routing across engineering and technology venue families.
They are not paper exemplars.

## Theory Versus Deployed System

- Theory venues (`ieee-transactions-on-automatic-control`, `automatica`) ask for a
  theorem, bound, or proof with stated assumptions, not a demonstration alone.
- Systems and applied venues ask whether a deployed artifact measurably works,
  with baselines, ablations, and reproducible evaluation.
- A hardware demo supports a theory claim; it does not replace the proof.

## Specialist Transactions Versus Broad-Significance Flagship

- IEEE Transactions reward depth, rigor, and reproducibility for a defined
  engineering community.
- `nature-electronics` and `nature-biomedical-engineering` need broad conceptual
  reach; a better prototype is not by itself broad reach.

## Materials And Energy

- Structural-materials venues (`acta-materialia`, `international-journal-of-plasticity`)
  prioritize microstructure, deformation, and structure-property relationships.
- Functional-materials and device venues prioritize the working property and its
  application context.
- Systems-energy journals (`applied-energy`) need integration and whole-system
  benchmarks; device-energy journals (`journal-of-power-sources`,
  `energy-storage-materials`) need cell-level controls, durability, and stability.

## Review And Reproducibility Discipline

- Review and tutorial venues (`proceedings-of-the-ieee`, `progress-in-materials-science`)
  are invited or scoped; confirm article type and invitation status first.
- Every empirical route requires baselines, ablations, released artifacts where
  feasible, and a reproducibility statement re-checked on the official venue page.
