# Worked Routing Examples

These examples are fictional diagnostic scenarios for venue routing. They do not
describe real submissions or publication facts.

## Case 1: Control Theorem Or Methodological Breadth?

Manuscript: a new stability result for a class of nonlinear systems, with
simulations and a hardware demonstration.

Route:
- Choose `ieee-transactions-on-automatic-control` when the contribution is a
  theorem, bound, or proof that advances control theory itself.
- Choose `automatica` when the value is methodological breadth across estimation,
  identification, or optimization, not a single deep theorem.
- If the deployed system and its measured behavior carry the claim, route to an
  applied systems venue and lead with the engineering result, not the proof.

## Case 2: IEEE Transactions Or Broad-Significance Flagship?

Manuscript: a device-level advance with a working prototype and possible
cross-field impact.

Route:
- Use the relevant IEEE Transactions (e.g. `ieee-transactions-on-power-electronics`,
  `ieee-transactions-on-signal-processing`) when the audience is a specific
  engineering community judging rigor and reproducibility.
- Test `nature-electronics` or `nature-biomedical-engineering` only when the
  advance changes what a broad field considers possible, not merely because the
  prototype works well.

## Case 3: Structural Or Functional Materials?

Manuscript: a processing route changes microstructure and a measured property.

Route:
- Choose `acta-materialia` or `international-journal-of-plasticity` when the claim
  is structural metallurgy: microstructure, deformation, structure-property law.
- Choose a functional-materials or device venue when the property is functional
  (optical, electrochemical, transport) and the application carries the claim.

## Case 4: Systems-Level Energy Or Device-Level Storage?

Manuscript: an energy result spanning a cell, a control scheme, and a system model.

Route:
- Choose `applied-energy` when the contribution is systems-level: integration,
  dispatch, lifecycle, or whole-system benchmarks.
- Choose `journal-of-power-sources` or `energy-storage-materials` when the claim
  is device-level electrochemistry, electrode design, or cell durability.
- Review and tutorial venues (`proceedings-of-the-ieee`, `progress-in-materials-science`)
  are invited or scoped; confirm the article type before routing there.
