# Worked Example: Reframing an Introduction for Academy of Management Annals

This is a fictional example. It does not describe a real paper.

## Before

> This paper studies an important topic using a large dataset and several robustness checks. We contribute to the literature by adding new evidence and showing heterogeneous effects.

## Diagnosis

- The first sentence does not name the Annals audience or the journal's commissioned and high-level reviews that synthesize management and organization research scope.
- The contribution could fit several siblings: Academy of Management Review, Journal of Management, Journal of Management Studies, and Annual Review outlets.
- The mechanism words that matter here are absent: integrative review, theory synthesis, agenda setting, management field map.

## After

> This paper asks how **integrative review** changes the decision margin that Annals readers care about. The design ties the estimate to **theory synthesis**, separates the main mechanism from **agenda setting**, and reports uncertainty in a way that makes the policy or theory implication portable beyond the study setting.
