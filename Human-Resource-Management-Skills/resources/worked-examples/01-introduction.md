# Worked Example: Reframing an Introduction for Human Resource Management

This is a fictional example. It does not describe a real paper.

## Before

> This paper studies an important topic using a large dataset and several robustness checks. We contribute to the literature by adding new evidence and showing heterogeneous effects.

## Diagnosis

- The first sentence does not name the HRM audience or the journal's human resource management, talent, compensation, employment systems, workforce analytics, and HR strategy scope.
- The contribution could fit several siblings: Personnel Psychology, Journal of Management, Human Relations, and Academy of Management Journal.
- The mechanism words that matter here are absent: HR system, talent architecture, employment relation, workforce analytics.

## After

> This paper asks how **HR system** changes the decision margin that HRM readers care about. The design ties the estimate to **talent architecture**, separates the main mechanism from **employment relation**, and reports uncertainty in a way that makes the policy or theory implication portable beyond the study setting.
