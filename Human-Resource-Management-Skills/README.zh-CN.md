# Human Resource Management Skills

<p align="center"><img src="assets/cover.svg" width="200" alt="Human Resource Management Skills 封面"></p>

[English](README.md) | 简体中文

面向 **Human Resource Management（HRM）** 投稿的 12 个 agent skills。本包围绕 human resource management, talent, compensation, employment systems, workforce analytics, and HR strategy 设计，帮助稿件区别于 Personnel Psychology, Journal of Management, Human Relations, and Academy of Management Journal，并强调 HR scholarship that connects people practices to organizational mechanisms and outcomes。

**官方依据核验日期：2026-06**（投稿前需复核易变细节）：见 [`resources/official-source-map.md`](resources/official-source-map.md)。

## 为什么需要单独的技能栈？

| HRM 约束 | 对稿件的要求 |
|-------------------|--------------|
| 范围 | 主张必须服务于 human resource management, talent, compensation, employment systems, workforce analytics, and HR strategy |
| 同门边界 | 说明为什么不是 Personnel Psychology, Journal of Management, Human Relations, and Academy of Management Journal |
| 证据标准 | 设计、模型、综述或质性证据必须匹配 HR scholarship that connects people practices to organizational mechanisms and outcomes |
| 来源纪律 | 当前流程事实必须有来源，或明确标记 待核实 |

## 快速开始

```text
/plugin marketplace add ./Human-Resource-Management-Skills
/plugin install human-resource-management-skills
```

手动使用：先打开 [`skills/hrm-workflow/SKILL.md`](skills/hrm-workflow/SKILL.md)。

## 默认工作流

```text
hrm-workflow → hrm-topic-selection → hrm-theory-development → hrm-literature-positioning → hrm-methods → hrm-data-analysis → hrm-contribution-framing → hrm-tables-figures → hrm-writing-style → hrm-submission → hrm-review-process → hrm-rebuttal
```
