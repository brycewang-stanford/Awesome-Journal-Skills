# Annals of the AAG — Submission Preflight Checklist

Copy this into your submission notes and tick every box before uploading to **ScholarOne Manuscripts**.
Verify volatile specifics (caps, fee/APC, ORCID requirement, policy wording) on the official Taylor &
Francis / AAG pages first (检索于 2026-06；以官网为准).

## 1. Area & article type
- [ ] Area declared: Geographic Methods / Human Geography / Nature and Society / Physical Geography,
      Earth & Environmental Sciences / General Geography (cross-disciplinary)
- [ ] Article type chosen: Article / Forum / Commentary
- [ ] One-line statement of the geographic contribution for the cover letter

## 2. Length (caps are INCLUSIVE)
- [ ] Article ≤ 11,000 words **including abstract, references, notes, tables, and figure captions**
- [ ] Forum: intro ≤ 2,500 + individual papers ≤ 5,000 each, ≤ 25,000 total (if applicable)
- [ ] Commentary < 2,000 words including references (if applicable)
- [ ] Total word count computed over the whole document, not just the body

## 3. Abstract & keywords
- [ ] Abstract present (verify length on the Style Sheet)
- [ ] 3-5 keywords, **italicized and alphabetized**, at the end of the abstract

## 4. Anonymity (double-anonymous)
- [ ] No author names, affiliations, funding, or acknowledgments in the manuscript
- [ ] Self-citations neutralized; no "as we showed in …"
- [ ] File metadata stripped (document properties, comments)
- [ ] Study-site / fieldwork detail does not de-anonymize the authors
- [ ] Separate, non-anonymous title page prepared as a distinct file

## 5. Style & references
- [ ] Chicago Manual of Style, author-date, throughout
- [ ] Reference list consistent and complete

## 6. Maps, figures & tables
- [ ] Projection named and appropriate; classification method + class count justified
- [ ] Scale bar / legend / data source / date present; areal data normalized (rates, not raw counts)
- [ ] Colorblind-safe and grayscale-legible
- [ ] Remote-sensing figures: band/stretch + accuracy/error panel + extent/resolution
- [ ] Graphics meet the journal's resolution/format specs (Graphics Guidelines)
- [ ] Captions/tables/figures counted toward the inclusive word cap

## 7. Data, ethics & transparency
- [ ] Data-availability statement drafted (shared / restricted-why / access path / synthetic)
- [ ] Spatial-data provenance documented: sources, licenses, access dates, CRS, processing log
- [ ] Geoprivacy handled — masking/aggregation for identifiable locations or sensitive sites
- [ ] IRB / consent documented for human-subjects, field, and participatory work
- [ ] Reproducibility: master script + README + seeds + pinned versions (quantitative)

## 8. Metadata & policy
- [ ] ORCID ready for authors (verify requirement)
- [ ] Preprint status checked against Taylor & Francis policy
- [ ] AI-use policy reviewed and any use disclosed per journal policy
- [ ] No submission fee assumed beyond what the official page states; OA APC noted if applicable

## 9. Final
- [ ] Cover letter states area, contribution, and fit with the Annals
- [ ] Files match ScholarOne's requested set
- [ ] Re-read `../SKILL.md` (aaag-submission) and `../../aaag-review-process/SKILL.md` once more
