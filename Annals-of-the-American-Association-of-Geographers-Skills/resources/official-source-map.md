## Official journal, submission & author pages

- **https://www.aag.org/journal/annals-of-the-aag/** — AAG's Annals home. Verifies: the journal is
  organized around **four areas** — **Geographic Methods; Human Geography; Nature and Society; Physical
  Geography, Earth, and Environmental Sciences** — plus a **General Geography / cross-disciplinary**
  track; a **subject editor leads each area**, plus a **Cartography Editor**; **editors serve four-year
  terms**; submission via **ScholarOne Manuscripts**; author resources include **Information for
  Authors, Style Sheet, Graphics Guidelines, Commentary Submission Guidelines, and an AI Policy**.
  Word limits: **regular manuscripts ≤ 11,000 words including abstract, references, notes, tables, and
  figure captions**; **Forums** = an introduction (**2,500 words**) plus individual papers (**5,000
  words each**), **≤ 25,000 total**; **Commentaries < 2,000 words including references**; Special-Issue
  papers around **5,000 words**.
- **https://www.tandfonline.com/journals/raag21** — Annals on Taylor & Francis Online (publisher home,
  current issue, Instructions for Authors). Verifies: publisher **Taylor & Francis (Routledge)** for the
  **American Association of Geographers**; bimonthly; Instructions for Authors / Editorial Policies.
- **https://www.tandfonline.com/journals/raag21/about-this-journal** — "Learn about" page. Verifies:
  **double-anonymous** peer review for Articles, Special Issues, Forums, and Commentaries by **one to
  three referees, one of which is the editor or a designate**; **Commentary Responses** undergo
  **single-anonymous** review.
- **https://www.aag.org/annals-of-the-aag-style-sheet/** and the PDF Style Sheet
  (`cas.umw.edu/gis/files/2020/01/Annals_of_the_AAG_Style_Sheet.pdf`, an instructor-hosted copy of an
  older version — confirm the current Style Sheet via the AAG page). Verifies: **Chicago Manual of
  Style (author-date)** house style; authors supply **3-5 keywords**, **alphabetized and italicized at
  the end of the abstract**.
- **https://www.aag.org/annals-guidelines-for-final-submission-of-accepted-manuscripts/** — final-
  submission guidelines for accepted manuscripts (production-stage requirements).

## Owner, publisher, history

- **https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Annals_of_the_American_Association_of_Geographers** — Verifies: founded
  **1911** as the **Annals of the Association of American Geographers**; renamed **Annals of the American
  Association of Geographers** after the AAG renamed itself (the "American Association of Geographers"
  name change); published by **Taylor & Francis** for the AAG; **bimonthly**; **ISSN 2469-4452 (print) /
  2469-4460 (online)**.

## Sibling-journal guardrail (do not misattribute)

- **Progress in Human Geography** (SAGE) — review/agenda essays, not original empirical studies.
- **Geographical Analysis** (Wiley) — formal spatial-analytic methods; narrower than the Annals' Methods area.
- **The Professional Geographer** (also AAG / Taylor & Francis) — shorter articles and reports; sibling AAG journal.
- **Transactions of the Institute of British Geographers** (RGS-IBG / Wiley) — UK flagship; not the AAG flagship.
