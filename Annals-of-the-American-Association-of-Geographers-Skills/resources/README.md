# Annals of the American Association of Geographers — Resources

Capability layer for the **Annals of the AAG** skill pack. This pack is **method-plural** across the
journal's four areas, so it does not vendor a single causal-inference code kit; instead it points to
external geographic tooling and to the cross-cutting method guidance in the shared empirical-methods hub.

## Contents

Illustrative fictional paper. |

## How to use

1. **Before drafting tables/maps** — stress-test the design against the area-specific skills
   (`aaag-research-design`, `aaag-data-analysis`, `aaag-tables-figures`); for quantitative-spatial work,
   cross-read the shared reviewer-objection checklist as background.
2. **When building results** — adapt the external geographic tooling in `external_tools.md` to your
   area; keep space honest (spatial dependence, MAUP/scale, validation, geoprivacy).
3. **Before submission** — walk this pack's `official-source-map.md` for venue limits and the
   reporting-standards background for the inference subset relevant to your method.

> The shared hub guidance is **venue-neutral and code-quant-oriented**; always defer to this pack's
> skills and `official-source-map.md` for what the *Annals* specifically requires across its four areas.
