# JFI Official Source Map

Primary and authoritative sources behind the **Journal of Financial Intermediation (JFI)**-specific
facts injected into this skill pack. Access date for all entries: **2026-06-01**.

Re-verify volatile items (editors, fee amount, policy wording) before relying on them — journal pages
change, and the primary ScienceDirect pages block automated fetching (HTTP 403). Where a fact could not
be confirmed against a live, dated official source it is marked **待核实 (to be verified)**.

## Official journal pages (Elsevier / ScienceDirect)

- **https://www.sciencedirect.com/journal/journal-of-financial-intermediation/publish/guide-for-authors**
  — Guide for Authors (Elsevier). Verifies: US$500 submission fee for all new submissions, payable in
  Editorial Manager before consideration, non-refundable for desk-rejected papers since 1 Jan 2018;
  single-anonymized (single-blind) review, editor-first assessment then a minimum of one independent
  expert reviewer, final editorial decision; submission via Editorial Manager (files converted to a
  single PDF for review, editable files required for typesetting); free SSRN preprint posting offered at
  submission; research-data sharing required/enabled with a published Data Statement and the `[dataset]`
  reference tag; no strict reference style at submission (consistent style accepted) with the author–date
  Elsevier reference style applied at proof; up to 6 JEL codes and a maximum of 6 keywords; mandatory
  "Declaration of Generative AI and AI-assisted technologies in the writing process" before the
  References; active desk-rejection policy; option to choose the Managing Editor in charge.
  *Accessed 2026-06-01. Live page returned HTTP 403 to automated fetch; facts confirmed via a Wayback
  Machine archived copy (snapshot 2024-03-31) plus corroborating live-page search-engine snippets.*

- **https://www.sciencedirect.com/journal/journal-of-financial-intermediation**
  — Journal homepage. Verifies: scope = banking, financial intermediation, and the economics of
  financial institutions and markets (theory and empirics); publisher Elsevier B.V.; ISSN 1042-9573.
  *Accessed 2026-06-01.*

- **https://www.sciencedirect.com/journal/journal-of-financial-intermediation/about/editorial-board**
  — Editorial Board. Intended to verify the current Editor-in-Chief / Managing Editors. **待核实**:
  live page returned HTTP 403 with no Wayback snapshot; could not be confirmed from a dated official
  source. *Accessed 2026-06-01.*

  official Guide; treated as none. **待核实** against the live page.
- **Open-access APC**: the US$3,760 figure in secondary sources is the open-access Article Publishing
  Charge, distinct from the submission fee; not independently confirmed against an official Elsevier APC
  page. **待核实**.
- **Drift since the 2024 snapshot**: the desk-rejection / single-blind / choose-Managing-Editor language
  appears in current live-page snippets but not verbatim in the 2024 Wayback snapshot; the live page may
  have been revised. Re-confirm wording before quoting.
