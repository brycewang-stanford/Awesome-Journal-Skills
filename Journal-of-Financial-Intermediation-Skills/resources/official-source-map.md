## Official Elsevier / ScienceDirect pages

- **https://www.sciencedirect.com/journal/journal-of-financial-intermediation**
  — JFI homepage. Verifies: submission link points to **Editorial Manager**;
  open-access APC is **USD 3,760 excluding taxes**; journal scope covers
  financial intermediation, investment banking, corporate finance, financial
  contracting, financial regulation, and credit markets; editorial philosophy
  includes active desk rejection, single-blind submission, and author choice of
  Managing Editor within constraints.
- **https://www.sciencedirect.com/journal/journal-of-financial-intermediation/publish/guide-for-authors**
  — Guide for Authors. Verifies: **USD 500** submission fee for all new
  submissions; fee must be paid during submission before a paper is considered;
  desk-rejected papers are not refunded; transferred manuscripts do not require
  another submission fee; **single anonymized** review; initial editor
  suitability assessment; suitable manuscripts are typically sent to at least
  **one** independent reviewer; editors make the decision; appeals are possible
  under Elsevier policy and only one appeal per submission is considered.
- The same Guide verifies submission and formatting facts: editable Word/LaTeX
  source files are required and PDF is not an acceptable source file for
  production; the system converts article files to one peer-review PDF; abstract
  must be concise, factual, and **250 words or fewer**; **1-7 English keywords**
  are required; Highlights are encouraged as a separate editable file with **3-5
  bullets**, each **85 characters or fewer**; tables must be editable text and
  figures supplied as separate files.
- The same Guide verifies ethics and disclosure facts: authors must disclose
  competing interests and funding; generative-AI use in manuscript preparation
  must be declared at first submission if used, and reviewers/editors may not use
  generative-AI tools in evaluation; SSRN posting is offered during submission
  once the paper passes initial desk review.
- The same Guide verifies research-data and reference facts: Elsevier
  **Option C** applies, meaning authors must deposit research data in a relevant
  repository and cite/link it, or explain why data cannot be shared; data
  statements are supported in the submission flow; no strict reference format is
  required at submission if references are complete and consistent; proof-stage
  style is **author-year**, with an alphabetically and chronologically arranged
  reference list; dataset citations use the `[dataset]` tag.
- **https://www.sciencedirect.com/journal/journal-of-financial-intermediation/about/editorial-board**
  — official editorial board. Verifies: **Murillo Campello** as Managing Editor;
  Co-Editors **Elena Loutskina** and **Joao Santos**; 50 editors/editorial-board
  members in 11 countries/regions.

## Operational notes

- Treat **USD 500** as the current official new-submission fee and the **USD
  3,760** figure as the optional OA APC, not the submission fee.
- Treat **Murillo Campello** as current Managing Editor and **Elena Loutskina /
  Joao Santos** as Co-Editors until the official editorial-board page changes.
- Treat JFI as **single anonymized / single-blind**; do not anonymize the
  manuscript unless the live instructions change.
- Treat data policy as **Option C deposit/cite/link-or-explain** when research
  data exist. Restricted bank or supervisory data should still have a clear data
  statement and shareable code/access path.
