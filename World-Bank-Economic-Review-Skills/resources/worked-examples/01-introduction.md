# Worked Example: Reframing an Introduction for The World Bank Economic Review

This is a fictional example. It does not describe a real paper.

## Before

> This paper studies an important topic using a large dataset and several robustness checks. We contribute to the literature by adding new evidence and showing heterogeneous effects.

## Diagnosis

- The first sentence does not name the WBER audience or the journal's policy-relevant development economics, impact evaluation, institutions, trade, labor, agriculture, and poverty scope.
- The contribution could fit several siblings: Journal of Development Economics, World Development, Economic Development and Cultural Change, and AEJ Applied.
- The mechanism words that matter here are absent: policy counterfactual, program evaluation, World Bank audience, developing-country data.

## After

> This paper asks how **policy counterfactual** changes the decision margin that WBER readers care about. The design ties the estimate to **program evaluation**, separates the main mechanism from **World Bank audience**, and reports uncertainty in a way that makes the policy or theory implication portable beyond the study setting.
