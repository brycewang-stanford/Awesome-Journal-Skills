# Review of Finance — Cover Letter Template

> Replace bracketed fields. Keep it concise. The cover letter is where RoF requires
> certain disclosures — omissions can trigger desk rejection and a two-year ban.
> Verify current requirements at revfin.org and the Editorial Express portal.

---

Dear Editors of the *Review of Finance*,

Please find enclosed our manuscript, **"[TITLE]"**, which we submit for consideration
at the *Review of Finance*.

**Summary.** [1–3 sentences: the first-order finance question, the approach
(empirical natural experiment / model), and the headline result with a number or the
key proposition. State why this is of general interest to finance researchers, at the
standard of a top-three finance journal.]

**Track.** We are submitting under the **[regular / Fast-Track]** track.
[If Fast-Track: we understand this carries the higher fee and a 14-day editorial
decision target.]

**EFA membership.** [State whether a corresponding author is a current EFA member,
which affects the submission fee.]

**Required disclosures.**
- This manuscript is **not under review at any other journal** and has not been
  published elsewhere.
- [If applicable] This paper was **previously rejected by the Review of Finance**
  [or by another journal]; we disclose this here. [Briefly note how it has changed.]
- [If applicable] **Code-sharing exception request:** we request an exception to the
  Code Sharing and Data Availability Policy because [reason]. [This request is made
  here, at initial submission, as the policy requires.]

**Data and code.** [State data sources. If proprietary (e.g., CRSP/Compustat/TRACE/
vendor), note that a pseudo-dataset and log files will be provided per the policy.]

**Anonymization.** The manuscript is prepared for **double-blind** review: it contains
no author-identifying information, and self-citations are phrased neutrally.

**Suggested / excluded referees.** [Optional: list, with brief justification.]

Thank you for considering our work.

Sincerely,
[Corresponding author, on behalf of all authors]
[Contact information]
