# Review of Finance — Pre-Submission Checklist

> Self-check before uploading to Editorial Express. Verify every volatile figure on the
> official pages first (see `resources/official-source-map.md`). Items marked 待核实 should
> be re-confirmed live.

## 1. Fit & contribution
- [ ] First-order, general-interest finance question (not subfield-only)
- [ ] Marginal contribution stated in one sentence; would survive a top-three-finance referee
- [ ] Empirical vs. theoretical register is clear and consistent

## 2. Length & format
- [ ] Whole manuscript ≤ **60 pages**, including appendices, bibliography, figures, and tables
- [ ] Abstract ≤ **150 words** (counted)
- [ ] **≥ 12-point** font, **≥ 1.5** line spacing
- [ ] **1-inch** side margins, **1.5-inch** top/bottom margins
- [ ] Submitted as a **PDF**, first page numbered **page 1**
- [ ] References in **Chicago** style, consistent throughout

## 3. Anonymization (double-blind)
- [ ] No author names, affiliations, acknowledgments, or grant numbers in the PDF
- [ ] Self-citations phrased neutrally
- [ ] PDF metadata scrubbed of author identity

## 4. Fee & track
- [ ] Track chosen: **regular (EUR 350 / EUR 300 EFA members)** or **Fast-Track (EUR 900, 14-day decision)**
- [ ] EFA membership status confirmed (affects fee)
- [ ] Tiered **refund** schedule for the chosen track understood (Fast-Track refund amounts 待核实)

## 5. Cover-letter disclosures (omission → desk reject + 2-year ban)
- [ ] Confirmed **not under review elsewhere** (no simultaneous submission)
- [ ] Any **prior RoF rejection disclosed**
- [ ] Any **code-sharing exception requested here**, at initial submission
- [ ] Relevant work searched and cited (lack of awareness is not a defense)

## 6. Replication readiness (Code Sharing and Data Availability Policy)
- [ ] Replication programs with software details and comments
- [ ] Non-proprietary data included, or **pseudo-dataset + log files** for proprietary data
- [ ] **Data Availability Statement** drafted
- [ ] **Formatted dataset citations** included
- [ ] Seeds and software versions pinned

## 7. Final sanity
- [ ] Abstract states the finding (number for empirical; proposition for theory)
- [ ] Headline result/proposition appears early in the introduction
- [ ] Claims never exceed what the design or model supports
