# Venue-Selection Patterns

These are routing archetypes for the breadth bundle. They are not publication
examples and do not replace the current official source map.

## Prestige Is Not Fit

Chinese economics and management journals often reject papers that are
technically competent but written for the wrong reader. Route by the paper's
claim:

- Theory-level national economic mechanism: test `economic-research` or
  `china-economic-quarterly`.
- Industrial policy / enterprise innovation / digital economy: test
  `china-industrial-economics`.
- International trade / openness / global value chains: test
  `journal-of-world-economy`, `journal-of-international-trade`, or
  `international-economics-and-trade-research`.
- Fiscal policy / tax / budget / local debt: test `public-finance-research`,
  `tax-research`, and `finance-and-trade-economics`.
- Management constructs / organization / strategy: test `nankai-business-review`,
  `management-review`, and `chinese-journal-of-management`.

## Sibling-Journal Traps

- Finance as a control variable is not enough for a finance journal.
- A government-policy setting is not enough for a public-administration journal;
  the public-governance mechanism must be the claim.
- An algorithm in an appendix is not enough for management-science journals; the
  model or optimization insight must be central.
- A clean causal design is not enough for a top general journal; the mechanism
  and theoretical contribution must travel beyond the policy episode.

## Output Discipline

When routing, return one top skill, two alternatives, one venue to avoid, and
the single biggest revision needed before invoking the per-journal skill.
