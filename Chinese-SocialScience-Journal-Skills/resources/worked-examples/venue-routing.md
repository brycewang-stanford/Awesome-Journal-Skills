# Worked Routing Examples

These examples teach the router how to choose among similar Chinese
social-science journals. They are fictional diagnostic scenarios and must not be
presented as real submissions or publication facts.

## Case 1: National Policy Shock With Strong Identification

Manuscript: a province-by-year policy shock with a clean staggered rollout,
mechanism tests, and national welfare implications.

Route:
- First check whether the question is a general economic mechanism or a sector
  implementation detail.
- If the contribution is theory-facing and the identification is central, test
  `economic-research`, `china-economic-quarterly`, and
  `china-industrial-economics`.
- If the paper is mainly industrial policy, innovation, enterprise behavior, or
  digital transformation, prefer `china-industrial-economics`.
- If it is mostly a policy-effect estimate without a bigger mechanism, reroute
  to field journals such as `public-finance-research`,
  `finance-and-trade-economics`, or `journal-of-public-management`.

Do not send a clean DID paper to the most prestigious venue by default. The
problem level and mechanism depth decide the target.

## Case 2: Finance Channel Is Mentioned But Not Central

Manuscript: a regional development paper includes bank credit as one mechanism,
but the main contribution is industrial upgrading.

Route:
- Do not choose `journal-of-financial-research` merely because financing
  variables appear in the regression.
- Use `china-industrial-economics` if the industrial mechanism is the claim.
- Use `journal-of-world-economy` or `journal-of-international-trade` only if
  openness, trade, FDI, exchange rate, or global value-chain exposure is the
  organizing mechanism.
- Use `journal-of-financial-research` only when the theory, data, and reviewer
  conversation are genuinely finance-first.

## Case 3: Survey-Based Management Theory

Manuscript: a questionnaire/SEM study on leadership, innovation climate, and
firm performance.

Route:
- Start from `nankai-business-review`, `management-review`,
  `chinese-journal-of-management`, and `business-management-journal`.
- If the paper claims optimization, algorithmic decision support, or management
  science modeling, test `journal-of-management-sciences-china` instead.
- If it is mostly policy commentary, do not frame it as a management-theory
  contribution without constructs, boundary conditions, and method discipline.

## Case 4: Digital Government and Public Administration

Manuscript: platform governance, government data sharing, or digital public
service reform.

Route:
- Use `chinese-public-administration` for administrative reform and governance
  theory.
- Use `e-government` when digital-government practice, platform architecture,
  or digital-service implementation is central.
- Use `journal-of-public-management` for public-management theory and empirical
  designs aimed at the public-administration research community.
- If the paper is a national strategy commentary without empirical or
  theoretical leverage, route away from narrow empirical journals.
