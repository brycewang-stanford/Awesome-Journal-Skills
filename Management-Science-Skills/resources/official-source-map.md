## VERIFIED facts injected into the skills (with source #)

- **Publisher / lineage:** published by INFORMS (Institute for Operations Research and the Management Sciences); the journal was established in **1954** by the precursor Institute of Management Sciences (TIMS). (#1)
- **Editor-in-Chief:** Christoph H. Loch, term **January 1, 2024 – December 31, 2026**. (#1, #2)
- **Scope / dual mission:** a deliberately bimethodological flagship publishing rigorous **analytical/quantitative** work (operations research, optimization, stochastic processes, game theory, economic theory) **and empirical** work (econometrics, lab/field experiments, behavioral studies, data science) across all functional business areas, routed through a **department / area-editor structure**. (#1, #5)
- **Review model:** **double-anonymized** (double-blind) peer review; a Department Editor in the relevant area runs the initial desk screen; papers passing screen go to roughly 2–3 reviewers, often via an Associate Editor; the Editor-in-Chief provides final oversight and cross-department arbitration. (#5; desk-screen ownership #5)
- **Submission system:** ScholarOne Manuscripts ("Manuscript Central") at **mc.manuscriptcentral.com/mnsc**, accessed via INFORMS PubsOnline; seven-step upload workflow. (#3, #8)
- **Submission fee:** **USD $79** per original submission, effective **August 1, 2025**; waived if the submitting author is a current INFORMS member at submission, or whose primary affiliation is in a low-/lower-middle-income economy; an **honor-based, no-justification waiver** is also available; ability to pay is not visible to editors/reviewers and has no bearing on editorial decisions. (#2, #7)
- **Length:** **no formal page/word limit at initial submission**, but the journal explicitly prefers **short, focused** papers; **invited revisions** must fit within **47 pages double-spaced (25 lines/page)** or **32 pages at 1.5 spacing (33 lines/page)**, 11-pt font, 1-inch margins; the **online appendix does not count** toward this limit. (#3)
- **Abstract / keywords:** abstract **250 words or less**; **3–5 keywords**. (#3)
- **Citation style:** **author-year (name–date)** in text, e.g., (Norman 1977) or Norman (1977); references alphabetical by author in INFORMS/journal style; manuscripts double- or 1.5-spaced, 11-pt, 1-inch margins; PDF preferred. (#3)
- **Data and Code Disclosure Policy:** introduced **2019** (articles submitted on/after **July 1, 2019**); a dedicated **Data Editor (Ben Greiner**, team incl. Milos Fisar and Ali Ozkes) **verifies** that accepted papers' data and code replicate **all main-manuscript results before publication**; appendix replication welcomed, not compulsory; verification adds **~17 days** on average; reinforced in 2025 alongside the submission-fee/transparency initiative. (#2, #4, #6)
- **Conference-proceedings disclosure:** authors whose shortened versions appeared in proceedings must disclose all related publications in the cover letter and **cite the proceedings paper anonymously** to preserve double-anonymized review. (#3)

## Verify before you rely

Editorial team and EiC term, the submission fee and its waivers, the department/area-editor
roster, desk-reject and turnaround figures, length limits for invited revisions, and the
Data and Code Disclosure verification workflow all change over time. Always confirm current
requirements on the official Management Science submission guidelines, editorial board, and
Code and Data Disclosure Policy pages on INFORMS PubsOnline before submitting.
