## Verified facts injected into the skills

- **Publisher / lineage:** Management Science is an INFORMS PubsOnline journal; the online archive lists issues back to **1954**. (#1)
- **Editor-in-Chief:** **Christoph H. Loch**, term **January 1, 2024 - December 31, 2026**. (#1, #3)
- **Scope / mission:** publishes scientific research on the practice of management across strategy, entrepreneurship, innovation, information technology, organizations, and functional business areas such as accounting, finance, marketing, and operations; includes organizational, managerial, and individual decision making from normative and descriptive perspectives. (#2)
- **Department routing:** submission "Type" is the Management Science department; authors designate a preferred Department Editor; authors also provide five reviewer names and three Associate Editor names. (#2, #3)
- **Review model and timeline:** **double-anonymous** review; a Department Editor and possibly an Associate Editor first evaluate fit/merit; if the manuscript passes initial screening, an Associate Editor recruits reviewers; the journal's policy is a clear editorial decision by the end of the second review round; Management Science strives to provide high-quality feedback to **90%** of authors within **90 days**, and if sent to reviewers, a Department Editor decision within **65 days on average**. (#2)
- **Submission system:** ScholarOne Manuscripts at **mc.manuscriptcentral.com/mnsc**; seven-step upload flow. (#2, #7)
- **Submission fee:** **USD $79** per original submission, effective **August 1, 2025**; waived for current INFORMS members, authors with a primary affiliation in a low-/lower-middle-income country, and authors using the honor-based no-justification waiver; fee is not visible to the editorial decision hierarchy; no fee on invited revisions. (#5, #6)
- **Length / format:** no first-round page limit, but papers must be succinct and Department Editors may reject excessive length; invited revisions must fit **47 pages double-spaced (25 lines/page)** or **32 pages at 1.5 spacing (33 lines/page)**, with online appendix excluded; submissions use at least 1.5 spacing, 11-point font, and 1-inch margins. (#2)
- **Abstract / keywords / ORCID:** abstract **250 words or less**; **3-5 keywords**; submitting author provides ORCID iD. (#2)
- **Citation style:** **author-year** in-text citations, with references listed alphabetically in journal style. (#2)
- **Data and Code Disclosure:** policy effective **June 1, 2019** and revised **April 20, 2026**; authors must create an **AsCollected** project page and provide its URL during submission; accepted numerical/computational papers must provide data, programs, and details sufficient to permit replication before production, or an approved alternative disclosure plan for proprietary/sensitive data. (#2, #4)
- **Conference disclosure / dual submission:** proceedings versions must be disclosed and cited anonymously; Management Science also collaborates with pre-approved conferences through a recommendation/invitation dual-submission path, where invited authors submit through the online portal within six months. (#2)

## Verify before you rely

Editorial team and EiC term, the submission fee and its waivers, the department/area-editor
roster, review timelines, length limits for invited revisions, and the Code and Data
Disclosure workflow all change over time. Always confirm current requirements on the
official Management Science submission guidelines, editorial board, submission-fee FAQ, and
Code and Data Disclosure Policy pages on INFORMS PubsOnline before submitting.
