(the ACM-SIAM Symposium on Discrete Algorithms) re-decides policy each cycle
through a freshly appointed program committee, so reopen the live SIAM pages
before any deadline-sensitive advice.

## Verified facts used across the skills (2027-cycle anchors)

- SODA is jointly sponsored by the SIAM Activity Group on Discrete Mathematics
  and the ACM Special Interest Group on Algorithms and Computation Theory
  (SIGACT); proceedings are produced through SIAM and appear on epubs.siam.org.
- SODA 2027: Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, January 24-27, 2027; submissions due
  July 9, 2026 (AoE) via soda27.hotcrp.com; reviews to authors by September 1,
  2026; author responses due September 4, 2026; decisions October 2026.
- Format: no page limit, full version encouraged; title page = title + 1-2
  paragraph abstract; 11-point+ font, single column, single-spaced, 1-inch
  margins, letter paper; deviation risks summary rejection.
- Review model: "fairly lightweight double-blind"; arXiv posting and talks
  explicitly permitted; important references must not be omitted or anonymized.
- SODA has a rebuttal phase: institutionalized by 2026 (the 2026 rebuttal opened
  September 8, 2025) and scheduled for 2027 as above. The SODA 2026 PC chair
  publicly reported a record submission pool, ~25% above the prior year
  (reported via the chair's announcement — secondary source).
- Co-located satellites for 2027: ALENEX (experimental algorithmics, with
  artifact evaluation) and SOSA (simplicity in algorithms). ANALCO, the former
  analytic-combinatorics satellite, last appears in dblp for 2019.
- Recent edition anchors: SODA 2024 = Alexandria, VA, January 7-10, 2024;
  SODA 2025 = 36th, New Orleans, January 12-15, 2025; SODA 2026 = 37th,
  Vancouver, January 11-14, 2026; hence SODA 2027 = 38th (derived count).
