## Verified facts
- **Lead Editor (verified 2026-06-23):** Rafael M. Fernandes, condensed-matter theorist, University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign; three-year term began **1 September 2025** (renewable). He serves as lead scientific advisor and chairs the PRL editorial board. Day-to-day handling is by APS's professional editorial staff and divisional associate editors. (#3, #4)
- **Publisher / owner:** American Physical Society (APS); PRL is the APS flagship physics letters journal. (#1)
- **APC / fees:** Subscription/hybrid model — **no required author charge** under the traditional subscription route. Optional **CC-BY open access** APC approximately **$2,700** per Letter. An **Optional Page Charge** of approximately **$830** (≈$310 for a Comment/Reply) is voluntary and not required to publish. PRL is a **SCOAP3** participating journal (high-energy-physics OA costs covered by the consortium). **Confirm live figures on the author page.** (#2)
- **ISSN:** 0031-9007 (print) / 1079-7114 (online). (#1)
- **Submission portal / review model:** Submit via the APS Editorial system; single-blind peer review with subject-area / divisional editor tracks. (#1, #2)
