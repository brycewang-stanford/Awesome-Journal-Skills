# YLJ Official Source Map

Primary and authoritative sources behind the YLJ-specific facts injected into this skill pack.
Access date for all entries: **2026-06**. Re-verify volatile items (exact word/length caps, fee,
portal mechanics, expedite wording, editorial process) on the official page before relying on them —
journal pages change each volume.

## Official journal, submission & author pages

- **https://yalelawjournal.org/submissions** — YLJ general submissions page. Verifies: YLJ uses its
  **own online submission system** and **does not accept pieces through external services like
  Scholastica**; authors may submit a piece as either an **Essay** or an **Article**; submissions are
  reviewed in **fully anonymized** form (the Articles & Essays Committee reviews without the author's
  name, institutional affiliation, or prior publications); quotations require quotation marks and
  citations conforming to the **Bluebook** and the Journal's citation requirements. (检索于 2026-06；以官网为准)
- **Volume-specific General Submission Guidelines PDFs** (linked from the submissions page, e.g.
  `yalelawjournal.org/files/Volume134GeneralSubmissionGuidelines_...pdf` and the Volume 135 equivalent)
  — Verify: **Articles** strongly encouraged **< 25,000 words including footnotes** (~50 Journal pages);
  **Essays** strongly encouraged **< 15,000 words including footnotes** (~30 Journal pages); over-length
  weighs significantly against acceptance. (检索于 2026-06；以官网为准)
- **Volume-specific Forum Submission Guidelines PDFs** (e.g. Volume 135 Forum guidelines) — Verify:
  **Forum** pieces strongly encouraged **< 10,000 words including footnotes** (~20 Journal pages);
  Forum submissions go through the Journal's online submission process. (检索于 2026-06；以官网为准)
- **Volume-specific Notes & Comments Submission Guidelines PDFs** — Verify: **Notes & Comments are
  authored by Yale Law students**; first-time **Note** word limit **~20,000 words including
  footnotes**; first-time **Comment** word limit **~7,000 words including footnotes**; reviewed
  **anonymously**, with identity revealed to the Committee only after acceptance; **Notes Development
  Editors** assist student authors. (检索于 2026-06；以官网为准)

## Expedited review & placement

- **https://yalelawjournal.org/submissions** (expedite section) — Verifies: expedited review provides
  **no competitive advantage**; the Journal **will not depart from its rigorous review process** for
  expedited pieces; authors with an outside offer may request an expedite **via their author
  submissions account**, and if facing an imminent deadline may also **email the submissions office**.
  (检索于 2026-06；以官网为准)

## Owner, model, history, and the Forum

- **https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Yale_Law_Journal** — Verifies: published by **The Yale Law Journal
  Company, Inc.**; a **student-run law review affiliated with Yale Law School**, **edited entirely by
  students**; publishes **articles, essays, features, and book reviews by professional legal scholars as
  well as student-written notes and comments**; maintains an online companion, the **Yale Law Journal
  Forum**; among the most-cited U.S. legal publications. Wikipedia gives "published continuously since
  1891." (检索于 2026-06)
- **https://yalelawjournal.org/about-the-yale-law-journal** — About page. Verifies: in **2005** YLJ
  became the **first leading law review to publish an online companion**, and in **2014** relaunched its
  online component as the **YLJ Forum**, a hub for short-form, timely discussion of law and legal
  scholarship. (检索于 2026-06；以官网为准)

## Sibling-confusion guardrail (do NOT conflate)

YLJ is **not** the *Harvard Law Review*, *Stanford Law Review*, *Columbia Law Review*, the *University
of Chicago Law Review*, or any of Yale's **other** student journals (Yale Law & Policy Review, Yale
Journal on Regulation, Yale Journal of Law & Technology, Yale Journal of International Law, Yale Journal
of Law & the Humanities). Several of those have **different** submission systems (some *do* use
Scholastica) and **different** word limits — verify each venue separately and do not import YLJ's
own-portal/no-expedite-advantage rules to them.
