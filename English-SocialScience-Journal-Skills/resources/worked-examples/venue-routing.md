# Worked Routing Examples

These are fictional diagnostic scenarios. They are designed to improve routing
judgment and must not be described as real submissions or published papers.

## Case 1: Credible Labor-Market Shock

Manuscript: a quasi-experimental labor-market paper with strong administrative
data, a policy shock, and clear worker-level welfare implications.

Route:
- If the result changes a broad economics conversation, compare AER/QJE/JPE and
  field depth packs.
- If the audience is labor specialists and the contribution is empirical labor
  economics, start with `journal-of-labor-economics`.
- If the main policy margin is taxation, social insurance, or public spending,
  compare `journal-of-public-economics`.
- If the value is data quality plus careful applied econometrics but the
  general-interest contribution is thinner, test REStat, AEJ: Applied, or a
  strong field journal.

## Case 2: Asset Pricing Versus Corporate Finance

Manuscript: firm-level disclosure predicts expected returns, but the mechanism
is investor information processing and market pricing.

Route:
- Use `journal-of-finance`, `journal-of-financial-economics`, or
  `review-of-financial-studies` only after deciding whether the paper is
  asset-pricing, corporate finance, market microstructure, or intermediation.
- If the paper is mainly banking balance sheets or lending frictions, test
  `journal-of-financial-intermediation` or `journal-of-banking-and-finance`.
- If the contribution is empirical finance with a narrower scope, consider
  `journal-of-financial-and-quantitative-analysis` before overclaiming a top-3
  finance fit.

## Case 3: Management Theory Without New Constructs

Manuscript: an organizational survey with good data but mostly confirms known
relationships.

Route:
- AMJ needs theory-building and empirical leverage; method alone is not enough.
- AMR is theory-only and should not be used for empirical testing papers.
- ASQ expects organization-theory depth and often a sociological mechanism.
- If the paper is primarily practical management insight, test Journal of
  Management, Human Relations, or field-specific management outlets before
  forcing an elite general-management frame.

## Case 4: Marketing, IS, Or Operations?

Manuscript: a platform experiment changes recommendations, prices, and user
engagement.

Route:
- If the core is consumer response or marketing strategy, compare JM/JMR/MS.
- If the core is recommender-system implementation and platform information
  systems, compare MISQ/ISR/JMIS.
- If the core is inventory, service design, or operational decision-making,
  compare Management Science, M&SOM, POM, or Operations Research.
- If the contribution is purely algorithmic, route to CS/AI conference skills
  rather than a business journal.
