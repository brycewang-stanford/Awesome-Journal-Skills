# Worked Example: Reframing an Introduction for INFORMS Journal on Computing

This is a fictional example. It does not describe a real paper.

## Before

> This paper studies an important topic using a large dataset and several robustness checks. We contribute to the literature by adding new evidence and showing heterogeneous effects.

## Diagnosis

- The first sentence does not name the IJOC audience or the journal's operations research and computing, algorithms, optimization, machine learning, simulation, and computational decision systems scope.
- The contribution could fit several siblings: Operations Research, Management Science, Manufacturing & Service Operations Management, and ACM/IEEE computing venues.
- The mechanism words that matter here are absent: algorithmic contribution, computational experiment, optimization benchmark, reproducible code.

## After

> This paper asks how **algorithmic contribution** changes the decision margin that IJOC readers care about. The design ties the estimate to **computational experiment**, separates the main mechanism from **optimization benchmark**, and reports uncertainty in a way that makes the policy or theory implication portable beyond the study setting.
