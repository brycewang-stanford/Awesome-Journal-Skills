## Verified on official pages (2026-06)

- Published by **INFORMS** (PubsOnLine); area editors are listed on the IJOC editorial-board page. **Editor-in-Chief: David L. Woodruff** (University of California, Davis), succeeding Alice E. Smith (whose term ran through 2025-12-31). Confirmed via the INFORMS EiC video and editorial-board page (verified 2026-06-22). Re-verify the masthead before submission.
- **Open access:** optional **INFORMS Open Option** one-time fee of **US$3,000**, invoiced only after acceptance; no submission fee and no APC for standard (subscription) publication. Confirmed on the INFORMS Open Option author-portal page (verified 2026-06-22).
