## Verified facts
- **Editor-in-Chief (verified 2026-06-23):** H. Holden Thorp, EiC of the *Science* family of journals (in this role since 28 Oct 2019); chemist, on leave from George Washington University. (#2, #3)
- **Publisher / owner:** American Association for the Advancement of Science (AAAS), Washington, D.C. (UK office in Cambridge). General multidisciplinary weekly. (#1, #6)
- **APC / fees:** *Science* is subscription/hybrid — there is **no required author APC to publish** a Research Article under the standard model. AAAS offers paid open-access/CC-BY options on selected article types and supports transformative agreements; **figures vary by article type and agreement — confirm the live charge on the AAAS author/open-access page before relying on a number.** (#1)
- **ISSN:** 0036-8075 (print) / 1095-9203 (online). (#4, #5)
- **Submission portal / review model:** Submit via the *Science* online submission system (cts.sciencemag.org / science.org author center). Editor-triaged: most submissions are screened in-house before external single-blind peer review. (#1)

## Live-check rules
- Treat the Editor-in-Chief and any open-access/APC figure as volatile; re-confirm on the masthead and the AAAS author/charges page before submission.
- *Science* desk-rejects a large share of submissions before review — verify current scope and format requirements on the official author pages each cycle.
