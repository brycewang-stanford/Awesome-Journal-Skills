## Access-method note

in the verification environment on 2026-07-08. Official pages were therefore read
via search-engine renderings of the exact URLs listed below, and cross-checked
against the OpenReview group, PMLR volume pages, and public organizer statements.
Facts that could not be pinned down through at least one such rendering are marked
待核实 rather than asserted.

## Verified 2026-cycle facts used in the skills

- CoRL 2026 is the 10th edition (the 9th was Seoul 2025 per PMLR v305), held in
  Austin, Texas, November 9–12, 2026, with workshops on November 9.
- Submission ran on OpenReview under group `robot-learning.org/CoRL/2026/Conference`,
  double-anonymous, with abstract registration May 26, 2026 and paper deadline
  May 29, 2026 — both **already passed** as of the 2026-07-08 access date.
- Submissions: 8 pages of main text including a mandatory Limitations section
  (counted); acknowledgments, references, and an optional appendix uncounted, in
  the same PDF.
- Supplementary material encouraged; overview video capped at 250 MB (strict),
  ~3 minutes suggested length.
- Rebuttal: a single-page PDF due August 11, 2026, 11:59 PM AoE; reviewer–AC
  discussion August 12–19, 2026; first-round rejection without rebuttal possible
  when no reviewer or AC score reaches weak accept.
- Decisions come out of SAC–AC meetings and a final SACs–PCs meeting; reviews and
  rebuttals of accepted papers are made public.
- Camera-ready: 9-page main text under `corl_2026` final mode, appendix merged at
  the end of the PDF, due October 12, 2026, 23:59 AoE; videos hosted externally
  because PMLR takes no video supplementary.
- Proceedings publish in PMLR. Verified volume anchors: v78 = 2017 (Mountain
  View), v164 = 2021 (5th edition; pages dated 2022), v205 = 2022 (Auckland),
  v229 = 2023 (Atlanta), v270 = 2024 (Munich), v305 = 2025 (Seoul).

## Still cycle-volatile

Page accounting (8 vs 9 and what counts), the Limitations mandate, video caps,
the rebuttal format and gate rule, discussion-window mechanics, GenAI policy,
camera-ready forms, and every date above are set annually by the rotating chairs.
The live corl.org year-site controls; this file is a 2026-07-08 snapshot.
