# Worked Example: Reframing an Introduction for Journal of Risk and Uncertainty

This is a fictional example. It does not describe a real paper.

## Before

> This paper studies an important topic using a large dataset and several robustness checks. We contribute to the literature by adding new evidence and showing heterogeneous effects.

## Diagnosis

- The first sentence does not name the JRU audience or the journal's risk, uncertainty, decision theory, behavioral choice, insurance, and experimental evidence on risky decisions scope.
- The contribution could fit several siblings: Experimental Economics, Journal of Economic Behavior and Organization, Games and Economic Behavior, and Management Science.
- The mechanism words that matter here are absent: risk preference, uncertainty attitude, prospect-theory test, insurance behavior.

## After

> This paper asks how **risk preference** changes the decision margin that JRU readers care about. The design ties the estimate to **uncertainty attitude**, separates the main mechanism from **prospect-theory test**, and reports uncertainty in a way that makes the policy or theory implication portable beyond the study setting.
