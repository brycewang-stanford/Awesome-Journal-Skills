# External Tools - SIGMOD

Access date: 2026-07-08

Open these before acting on any deadline-sensitive advice. SIGMOD re-declares
its round calendar, page budget, and platform details every cycle, and the
research track's rules live partly on the conference site and partly on the
PACMMOD journal pages — both must be checked. Note the access caveat: from
some networks, `sigmod.org` and `dl.acm.org` reject automated fetches, so a
browser (or search-engine rendering as a fallback) may be required.

## Official workflow surfaces

- Current-cycle research CFP: https://2027.sigmod.org/calls_papers_sigmod_research.shtml
- Current-cycle important dates (full round calendar): https://2027.sigmod.org/calls_papers_important_dates.shtml
- PACMMOD author guidelines: https://dl.acm.org/journal/pacmmod/author-guidelines
- PACMMOD journal home and issue archive: https://dl.acm.org/journal/pacmmod
- Availability & Reproducibility Initiative: https://reproducibility.sigmod.org/
- SIGMOD organization and awards hub: https://sigmod.org/
- Conference edition sites: https://2026.sigmod.org/ (Bengaluru, held May 31 - June 5, 2026) and https://2027.sigmod.org/ (Huntington Beach, June 13-19, 2027)
- Neighbor-venue calendars for routing: https://www.vldb.org/2026/submission-guidelines.html (PVLDB rolling model) and https://dl.acm.org/journal/tods/sigmod-policy (TODS invitation lane)

## Author-side verification loop

1. Confirm the round you target on the important-dates page, including the
   abstract+COI cutoff one week before the paper deadline.
2. Confirm the Microsoft CMT site link published in the current CFP and that
   your account, conflicts, and topic areas are set there.
3. Re-read the current anonymity wording — funding and colleague
   acknowledgements are banned at submission time, which surprises authors
   arriving from journals.
4. Re-check the revision mechanics (extra page, letter length, highlighting
   rules) only from the current CFP; these are precisely the details that
   drift between cycles.
5. For artifacts, check the ARI edition page for this year's registration
   site, dates, and badge criteria before promising evaluators anything.
6. For industrial-track plans, confirm the track's CFP exists for the
   current cycle — as of 2026-07-08 the 2027 industrial call was not yet
   posted (待核实).

## Do not infer

- Do not project round dates from one cycle onto the next; only the
  quarterly pattern is stable.
- Do not assume PVLDB's resubmission rules mirror SIGMOD's embargo, or vice
  versa — read both before advising a rejected-paper reroute.
- Do not treat PACMMOD format as the submission format; it applies only to
  final publication.
- Do not assume ARI logistics (HotCRP site, hardware support, timelines)
  repeat from a prior edition.
- Where the conference site and the PACMMOD journal pages appear to
  disagree, treat the newer statement as controlling, record the conflict,
  and ask the chairs if the discrepancy is deadline-relevant.
