# JHR Submission Checklist

- Scope: empirical microeconomics with policy relevance; not HR-management/personnel research.
- Portal: submit through `http://jhr.msubmit.net`; do not email the editorial office directly.
- Fee: confirm current nonrefundable initial-submission fee on the live Authors page; no fee for invited revisions.
- Length: 40 pages including references, tables, and figures at 12pt / 1.5 spacing / 1-inch margins, or 50 pages if double-spaced.
- Front matter: per-author disclosure statement, archive-plan footnote, Data Availability Statement, and RCT pre-analysis-plan statement if relevant.
- Replication: public repository or waiver request, read-me, code, data, and CC0-compatible deposit where allowed.
- Review option: decide whether to attach prior journal decision letters and reports.
- Files: main manuscript, Online Appendix, cover letter, and any supplemental materials.
