# Worked Example: Reframing an Introduction for International Economic Review

This is a fictional example. It does not describe a real paper.

## Before

> This paper studies an important topic using a large dataset and several robustness checks. We contribute to the literature by adding new evidence and showing heterogeneous effects.

## Diagnosis

- The first sentence does not name the IER audience or the journal's general-interest economics with a distinctive theory, econometrics, macro, micro, and international reach scope.
- The contribution could fit several siblings: QJE, JPE, REStud, Econometrica, The Economic Journal, and European Economic Review.
- The mechanism words that matter here are absent: general-equilibrium discipline, structural clarity, international readership, theory-empirics balance.

## After

> This paper asks how **general-equilibrium discipline** changes the decision margin that IER readers care about. The design ties the estimate to **structural clarity**, separates the main mechanism from **international readership**, and reports uncertainty in a way that makes the policy or theory implication portable beyond the study setting.
