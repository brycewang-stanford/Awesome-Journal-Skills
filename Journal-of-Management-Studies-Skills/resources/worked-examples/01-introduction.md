# Worked Example: Reframing an Introduction for Journal of Management Studies

This is a fictional example. It does not describe a real paper.

## Before

> This paper studies an important topic using a large dataset and several robustness checks. We contribute to the literature by adding new evidence and showing heterogeneous effects.

## Diagnosis

- The first sentence does not name the JMS audience or the journal's management and organization studies, strategy, entrepreneurship, innovation, OB, and critical perspectives scope.
- The contribution could fit several siblings: Journal of Management, Organization Studies, AMJ, AMR, and Strategic Management Journal.
- The mechanism words that matter here are absent: theoretical pluralism, organization studies, critical boundary, international management.

## After

> This paper asks how **theoretical pluralism** changes the decision margin that JMS readers care about. The design ties the estimate to **organization studies**, separates the main mechanism from **critical boundary**, and reports uncertainty in a way that makes the policy or theory implication portable beyond the study setting.
