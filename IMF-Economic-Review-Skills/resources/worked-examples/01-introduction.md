# Worked Example: Reframing an Introduction for IMF Economic Review

This is a fictional example. It does not describe a real paper.

## Before

> This paper studies an important topic using a large dataset and several robustness checks. We contribute to the literature by adding new evidence and showing heterogeneous effects.

## Diagnosis

- The first sentence does not name the IMFER audience or the journal's international macroeconomics, finance, development, policy, exchange rates, sovereign risk, and IMF-relevant evidence scope.
- The contribution could fit several siblings: Journal of International Economics, JIMF, JMCB, and Economic Policy.
- The mechanism words that matter here are absent: international macro, sovereign risk, capital-flow policy, IMF policy audience.

## After

> This paper asks how **international macro** changes the decision margin that IMFER readers care about. The design ties the estimate to **sovereign risk**, separates the main mechanism from **capital-flow policy**, and reports uncertainty in a way that makes the policy or theory implication portable beyond the study setting.
